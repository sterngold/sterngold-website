**MARBRE OF CALVES HEAD**

Yield: 10 servings | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 22.11.2005

**TERRINE:**
- Blanch 500 g calves head and 200 g veal shoulder meat separately.
- Bring water to a boil with bouquet garni, sachet bag, and 20 g lemon juice. Add blanched meats, cover with towel, and simmer gently for 1½ hours until tender.
- Remove all fat and cut meat into 1 cm salpicon.
- Marinate with 30 ml walnut oil and 50 ml sherry vinegar; heat through.
- Season with salt and pepper.
- Blanch savoy cabbage leaves 5 minutes, refresh, and dry on paper towel (remove center ribs).
- Line terrine mold with cabbage leaves.
- Mix vegetable brunoise with chopped parsley and chervil; fold into calves head.
- Fill terrine, press with weight, and chill.

**VINAIGRETTE:**
- Sauté 30 g bacon brunoise without fat.
- Add 100 g vegetable brunoise and 20 g chopped shallots; sweat gently.
- Remove from heat and whisk with 40 g walnut oil, 20 g pumpkin seed oil, and 80 g sherry vinegar.
- Season to taste.
- Serve lukewarm with sliced terrine and light salad bouquet.
