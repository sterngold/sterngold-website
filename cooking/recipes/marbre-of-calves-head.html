Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% (Net quantity)

500 g Calves head
200 g Veal shoulder meat
300 g *Bouquet garni for white Veal stock
1 pc Sachet bag
20 g Lemon juice *
80 g Vegetable brunoise mix
50 ml Sherry vinegar
30 ml Walnut oil
10 g Parsley, Italian, fresh
5 g Chervil, fresh
1 x Seasoning (black pepper)
200 g Savoy Cabbage








Carrot, Celery, Leek, Pickled, gherkins.

- BLANCH CALVES HEAD AND VEAL SHOULDER
- BRING WATER TO A BOIL AND ADD BOUQUET GARNI,SACHET BAG AND LEMON JUICE.
- ADD CALVES HEAD AND VEAL SHOULDER AND COVER WITH TOWEL.
- SIMMER ON LOW HEAT FOR 11 /2 HOURS UNTIL SOFT AND TENDER
- REMOVE ALL THE FAT AND CUT HEAD AND SHOULDER INTO 1 CM SALPICON - MARINATE WITH OIL AND VINEGAR AND HEAT UP.
- SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER MILL
- BOIL SAVOY CABAAGE LEAVES UNTIL COOKED FOR APPROX. 5 Min. AND REFRESH. - DRY WITH PAPER TOWEL
- LINE OUT TERRINE MOLD WITH CABBAGE LEAVES (MID RIPS REMOVED)
- ADD BLANCHED VEGETABLE BRUNOISE AND CHOPPED HERBS TO CALVES HEAD
- ADD CALVES HEAD INTO TERRINE MOLD AND PRESS WITH SOME WEIGHT. - CHILL

Vinaigrette:
100 gr. Vegetable brunoise
30 gr. Bacon brunoise
20 gr. Shallots chopped
40 gr. Walnut oil
20 gr. Pumkin seed oil
80 gr. Sherry vinegar
Salt and pepper mill

- SAUTE THE BACON BRUNOISE IN SAUTEUSE (NO FAT)
- ADD VEGETABLE BRUNOISE AND SHALLOTS AND SWEAT.
- remove from heat and add with remaining ingredients into bowl and season to taste with salt and pepper mill.
- Serve with little salad bouquet.
- Serve lukewarm with sliced terrine of calves head.
