PURPLE POTATO GNOCCHI
Yield: 5 servings | Chef: Patrick Diethelm

Ingredients:
500 g purple potatoes, peeled
140 g white wheat flour
40 g egg
10 g pasteurized egg yolk
4 g salt
Nutmeg (pinch)
30 g ruccola

Method:
Cook peeled purple potatoes in salted water until tender; drain well and dry briefly. Pass through ricer or sieve while hot. Cool slightly. Fold in egg and pasteurized egg yolk. Combine sifted flour with salt and nutmeg, then fold into potato mixture. Chill dough. Form into small quenelles using two spoons and poach in boiling salted water until they rise and cook through, approximately 3–4 minutes. Finish with fresh ruccola.
