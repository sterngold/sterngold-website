Saucier Sauce hot

White wine sauce (fish)

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 1

Ingredients

700 g ml Fish veloute sauce
300 g ml Fish stock
250 g ml Heavy cream 36%
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
1 pcs x Lemon juice

Production

- reduce fish stock to glaze
- add glaze and cream to fish veloute, boil for 5 minutes
- strain through cheesecloth
- season with salt, cayenne and lemon juice
WHITE WINE SAUCE (FISH)
Station: Saucier | DCT

METHOD
Reduce fish stock to glaze. Add glaze and cream to fish velouté and boil for 5 minutes. Strain through cheesecloth. Season with salt, cayenne, and lemon juice.

Notes

APPLICATION - use as a basic ingridient in white fish sauce
