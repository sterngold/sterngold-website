Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% [Net quantity)

20 g butter
200 ml milk
120 g White wheat flour
40 g egg yolk
160 g Gruyere cheese spicy
1 x Seasoning (salt, nutmeg)
1 x Seasoning (pepper)
1 g Thyme twig, fresh

75 g White wheat flour
2.5 pc Eggs
250 g Mie de pain

Preparation:
- Bring milk, butter and seasoning to a boil.
- Prepare like a choux paste
- Bring milk and butter to a boil, add all the flour at once and stir with plastic spatula.
- Remove from the heat and stir in the egg yolks and grated cheese
- Season to taste with salt, pepper mill, nutmeg and fine chopped thyme
- Spread croquettes mixture about 2 cm thick on sheet pan prepared with parchment paper and cool down for at least one hour in refrigerator.
- Peel of the ba ng paper and place onto floured table
- Cut croquette mixture into rectangles or any fancy shapes.
- Dredge them in flour dip, in eggs and coat with mie de pain
- Deep fry at 170° C
- Drain on kitchen paper and serve hot with vegtable coulis as hot appetizer.
or as amuse bouche
CHEESE CROQUETTES

Bring milk, butter, salt, nutmeg, and pepper to a boil. Add flour all at once, stirring constantly with a plastic spatula until the mixture forms a ball. Remove from heat and stir in egg yolks and grated Gruyère cheese. Season to taste with salt, pepper mill, nutmeg, and finely chopped fresh thyme.

Spread croquette mixture approximately 2 cm thick on parchment-lined sheet pan and refrigerate for at least one hour. Peel off parchment and transfer to floured work surface. Cut into rectangles or fancy shapes. Standard breading procedure: dredge in flour, dip in beaten egg, coat with panko.

Deep fry at 170°C until golden. Drain on kitchen paper and serve hot with vegetable coulis as an appetizer or amuse bouche.
