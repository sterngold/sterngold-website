Pumpkin date pickles

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Category: ECA - Sauces Garde-manger
Source: Patrick Diethelm
Date: 13.11.2005
Cost of goods: CHF 0.33 /Personen Remark:
Ingredients for 20 Personen: ät; 100% (Net quantity)

- Split dates in half and remove the seed cut into 1 cm dice and marinate with sherry wine for 1 hour - Cut pumkin into 5mm jardiniere
- Start toasting Szechuan pepper on low temperature add crystal sugar and ginger
- Deglaze with the apple vinegar
- Add star anise and salt and bring to a boil
- Add pumkin and simmer 10 minutes on low fire until soft, then add marinated dates and let rest for 10 minutes.
- Before serving add chopped mint leaves to give fresh flavour to pickles and served with terrine
