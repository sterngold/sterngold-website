PUMPKIN DATE PICKLES
Yield: 20 portions | Chef: Patrick Diethelm

Ingredients:
200 g ripe pumpkin meat
200 g dates
200 ml dry sherry wine
10 g Szechuan pepper
10 g ginger
200 g crystal sugar
200 ml apple vinegar
1 g star anise
Salt (pinch)
1 g fresh mint leaf

Method:
Halve dates, remove seeds, cut into 1 cm dice, and marinate in sherry wine for 1 hour. Cut pumpkin into 5 mm jardinière. Toast Szechuan pepper on low heat, add crystal sugar and ginger. Deglaze with apple vinegar. Add star anise and salt, bring to boil. Add pumpkin and simmer on low heat 10 minutes until soft. Add marinated dates and rest 10 minutes. Before service, fold in chopped mint to brighten flavor. Serve with terrine.
