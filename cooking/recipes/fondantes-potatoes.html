**Fondantes Potatoes** | Station: Saucier | Serves 6

**Mise en place:**
700 g potatoes (Type A or B), 75 g butter, 600 ml veal broth, 12 g butter, 12 g meat glaze.

**Method:**
Wash and peel jumbo-sized potatoes. Turn into egg shapes with a flat bottom using a turning knife. Butter an ovenproof dish.

Arrange potatoes side by side in the buttered dish. Pour hot veal broth around them and add 75 g butter to reach approximately two-thirds the height of the potatoes. Cover with aluminum foil or butter paper.

Bake in the oven at 180°C. Brush potatoes occasionally with the resulting cooking stock. When properly timed, potatoes will be shiny brown when cooked and most liquid will be absorbed.

Just before service, dissolve the meat glaze with a small amount of water and bring to a boil. Brush the potatoes with this melted glaze de viande to impart a beautiful sheen.
