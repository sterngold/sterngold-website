Fondantes Potatoes

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Ingredients for 6 servings at 100% [Net quantity)

700 g


12 g 600 ml 12 g
75 g
Potatoes A or B Typ


Butter
Veal broth / bouillon
Butter
Meat glaze
Wash and peel jumbo size potatoes. Turn into egg shapes [with flat bottom). Butter a ovenproof dish



Melt glace de viande with some water and bring to a boil

Brush the potatoes with the glaze before serving

- PLACE POTATOES SIDE BY SIDE IN OVENPROOF DISH.
- ADD HOT BOUILLON AND THE BUTTER, TO REACH ABOUT TWO-THIRDS OF THE HEIGHT OF THE POTATOES COVER WITH ALUMINIUM FOIL OR BUTTER PAPER
- PLACE DISH IN OVEN AND BAKE AT 180°C.
- BRUSH POTATOES OCCASIONALLY WITH RESULTING STOCK .
- IF THE TIMING IS CORRECT, THE POTATOES WILL BE SHINY BROWN WHEN COOKED, AND MOST LIQUID WILL BE SOAKED UP BY THE POTATOES.
- BRUSH POTATOES WITH THE MELTED GLACE DE VIANDE AT THE MOMENT OF SERVICE TO GIVE THEM A NICE SHEEN.
