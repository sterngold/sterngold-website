Garde Manger Sauce cold

Sweet and Sauer

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen

Ingredients

400 g Pumpkin
200 g Water
200 g Vinegar, white wine
300 g Sugar
2 pcs p nelken
2 pcs Stern anis
Vanille
Caneel
Ginger

Production

Warm the fond add pumpkin; boil once and live to cool down
Warm the fond and add pumpkin. Bring to a boil once, then allow to cool completely.
