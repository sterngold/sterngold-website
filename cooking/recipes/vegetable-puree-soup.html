Entremétier Soup

Vegetable puree soup

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

53 g Butter
172 g Onions (Trim and cut into medium dice)
180 g Leeks (Wash, trim and cut into medium dice)
120 g Knob Celery (celeriac) (Trim and cut into medium dice)
440 g Carrot (peel and slice)
180 g Potatoes
2.0 kg liter Veal broth
1 pcs x Salt
350 g ml Heavy cream 36%
16 g Butter
3 g Chervil, fresh
100 g Toasted bread

Production

- sautee leeks, carrots and knob celery in butter
- add broth to a boil and skim
- simmer for 20 min. add potaotes and simmer fro 20 min longer
- stir in cream and add cold butter
- puree with food mill
- bring to boil again
- season to taste
- sprinkle with chervil

- cut the toast bread into 1 cm croutns and bake in oven until golden brown
VEGETABLE PURÉE SOUP

Sauté leeks, carrots, and knob celery in butter. Add broth and bring to boil, skimming. Simmer 20 minutes. Add potatoes and simmer 20 minutes longer.

Stir in cream and add cold butter. Purée with food mill and bring to boil again. Season to taste and sprinkle with chervil.

Garnish: Cut toast bread into 1cm croutons and bake in oven until golden brown. Serve alongside soup.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
