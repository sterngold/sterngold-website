Green bean salad

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

1.1399999856948853 kg String beans, fresh ( or green beans)
20 g Salt
# Dressing
90 g Onions
2 pcs piece Garlic, cloves
10 g Savory, fresh
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
100 g ml Vinegar, white wine
150 g ml Oil, sunflower

Production

#Dressing
- peel and chop onions and garlic gloves
- chop the savory leaves
- combine all dressing ingredients and mix well

- snip off ends of greens, break in half if large and wash
- boil beans in ample slat water and drain (can be steamed too)
- combine beans, while still warm with dressing
**Green Bean Salad**

Method:

*Dressing:*
Peel and chop onions and garlic cloves. Chop savory leaves. Combine all dressing ingredients and mix well.

*Assembly:*
Snip ends from green beans, break in half if large, and wash. Boil beans in ample salted water and drain (or steam).

Combine warm beans with dressing and toss to coat.

Notes

- Vegetable salads should be mixed with dressing while still warm, and they should never be served directly from the refrigerator - Italian dressing is also suitable
