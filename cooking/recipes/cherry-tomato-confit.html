CHERRY TOMATO CONFIT

Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 07.11.2005
Category: ECA - Mediterranean cuisine

Ingredients (10 servings):
- 250 g Italian cherry tomatoes
- 1 sprig fresh thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 40 ml olive oil

Method:
1. Halve the cherry tomatoes and place into an ovenproof dish.
2. Add thyme sprig and bay leaf to the tomatoes, then drizzle with olive oil.
3. Place in a 80°C oven for 1 hour with the oven door slightly ajar to allow steam to escape.
4. Season with rock salt before service.
