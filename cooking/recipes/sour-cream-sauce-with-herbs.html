Sour Cream Sauce with herbs
Sauerrahmsauce mit Kräutern Sauce LI la crème acidulée [sm/tone)

Recipe # : 90
Category ECA - Sauces Garde-manger
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 10.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 5.92 / EUR 3.75

ur6pean G ourtytet Cune
Ingredients for 0.200 liters at 100% (Net quantity)
150 ml Sour cream 35%
30 g Honey Melt Honey over Bain marie
10 g Lemon juice *
5 g Dijon mustard
3 g Dill Chopped
1 g Chervil fresh Chopped
5 g Horseradish, fresh Freshly grated
5 g Chives, fresh Cut into fine slivers
1 x Seasoning (salt, cayenne pepper)
- Combine all the ingredients and season to taste - Serve chilled with fish terrine
