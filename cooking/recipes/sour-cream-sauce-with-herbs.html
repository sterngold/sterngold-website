SOUR CREAM SAUCE WITH HERBS | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Yield: 200 ml

Ingredients:
150 ml sour cream (35%)
30 g honey
10 g lemon juice
5 g Dijon mustard
3 g dill, chopped
1 g chervil, chopped fresh
5 g horseradish, freshly grated
5 g chives, cut into fine slivers
Salt and cayenne pepper to taste

Method:
Melt honey over a bain-marie. Combine all ingredients and season to taste. Serve chilled with fish terrine.
