**MARBRE OF GUINEA FOWL AND PRAWNS**

Yield: 10 servings | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 22.11.2005

**PREPARATION:**
- Remove skin and fat from 400 g guinea fowl supremes; remove tenderloin first and cut meat into long strips.
- Peel and clean 150 prawns.
- Season meat and prawns with pepper and coriander mill. Add 30 ml dry sherry wine and marinate.
- Blanch thinly sliced 200 g Pfälzer carrots (or regular carrots) and refresh.
- Chop 1 g chervil and 6 basil leaves.

**ASSEMBLY:**
- Line terrine mold with plastic film and seasoned carrot slices.
- Season marinated meat with salt and fold in herbs.
- Fill terrine mold, sprinkling 1 g aspic powder between layers.
- Cover with overhanging carrots and plastic film. Rest 10 minutes in refrigerator.
- Poach in steamer at 75–80°C until internal temperature reaches 60°C.
- Add weight on top and cool down completely.
