Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
22 g Gelatine leaves 500 ml Heavy cream (36%)
Method Pacotise :
- PACOTISE THE CONTENT OF THE BEAKER AND ADD INTO A BOWL - WARM THE BROCCOLI OVER A WATER BATH UNTIL LIQUID
- SOAK THE GELATINE IN COLD WATER
- DISSOLVE THE GELATINE WITH A LITTLE BROCCOLI CONCENTRATE - STIR INTO THE REMAINING MIXTURE
- WHIP THE HEAVY CREAM
- COOL DOWN THE BROCCOLI MIXTURE UNTIL 25°C ( BAVAROIS TEMP.) THEN FOLD IN THE WHIPPED CREAM
- FILL INTO PREPARED CLEAN BOWL AND LET REST FOR 1-2 HOURS - SCOOP OUT DUMPLINGS WITH A WARM SPOON AND ARRANGE
- FOR GARNISHES BOIL SOME BROCCOLI FLEURETS AND REFRESH AND MARINATE WITH OIL AND VINEGAR AND SEASONING
Boil fresh broccoli in salted water until al dente. Refresh and squeeze out all water. Add broccoli to a Pacojet beaker with light chicken stock and salt. Press down and freeze at minus 20°C for 24 hours.

Pacojet the frozen mixture into a bowl. Warm gently over a water bath until liquid. Soak gelatine leaves in cold water, then dissolve in a small amount of the warm broccoli purée. Stir back into the remaining mixture. Whip heavy cream to soft peaks. Cool the broccoli mixture to 25°C, then fold in the whipped cream. Fill into prepared bowl and rest 1–2 hours. Scoop dumplings with a warm spoon and arrange on plate.

For garnish: boil broccoli florets, refresh, and marinate with oil, vinegar, and seasoning.
