- BOIL THE BROCCOLI IN SALTED WATER NOT TO SOFT
- REFRESH AND SQUEEZE OUT ALL THE WATER

Brokkolimousse
Recipe # :
Category : ECA - Paco jet
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 01.12.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.38 / EUR 0.24

European Gourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
22 g Gelatine leaves 500 ml Heavy cream (36%)
Method Pacotise :
- PACOTISE THE CONTENT OF THE BEAKER AND ADD INTO A BOWL - WARM THE BROCCOLI OVER A WATER BATH UNTIL LIQUID
- SOAK THE GELATINE IN COLD WATER
- DISSOLVE THE GELATINE WITH A LITTLE BROCCOLI CONCENTRATE - STIR INTO THE REMAINING MIXTURE
- WHIP THE HEAVY CREAM
- COOL DOWN THE BROCCOLI MIXTURE UNTIL 25°C ( BAVAROIS TEMP.) THEN FOLD IN THE WHIPPED CREAM
- FILL INTO PREPARED CLEAN BOWL AND LET REST FOR 1-2 HOURS - SCOOP OUT DUMPLINGS WITH A WARM SPOON AND ARRANGE
- FOR GARNISHES BOIL SOME BROCCOLI FLEURETS AND REFRESH AND MARINATE WITH OIL AND VINEGAR AND SEASONING
