BARLEY SOUP FROM GRISONS
Station: Entremétier | Yield: 2,000 liters

INGREDIENTS
21 g Butter
38 g Onions
34 g Carrot
43 g Leeks
19 g Knob celery (celeriac)
40 g Air-dried ham (Rohschinken) or Bündnerfleisch
90 g Pearl barley
16 g White wheat flour
2 l White veal stock
350 ml Heavy cream (36%)
3 g Fresh chives
Salt to taste

METHOD
Peel and chop onions fine. Clean carrot, leeks, and knob celery; cut all into brunoise. Cut air-dried ham into brunoise. Chop fresh chives into small slivers. Heat veal stock.

Sweat leeks, onions, carrots, and knob celery in butter. Add ham brunoise and continue cooking. Add pearl barley and cook further. Dust with flour. Pour in hot veal stock and simmer approximately 20 minutes until barley is tender.

Add additional stock if needed and stir in cream. Season to taste with salt.

Garnish with fresh chives before service. Total cooking time 40 minutes to 1 hour.
