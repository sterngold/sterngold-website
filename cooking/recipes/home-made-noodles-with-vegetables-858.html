Home-Made Noodles with Vegetables
Nudeln mit Gemüsestreifen Nouilles à /a julienne de légumes


Rezept Nr. : 633
Kategorie : Pasta Dishes
Koch : P.Diethelm
Datum : 03.08.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 0.23 / EUR 0.15

EUROPEAN CULINARY CENTER
Zutaten für 10 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)
600 g Home-made noodle dough 50 g Salt
200 g Vegetables julienne
25 g Butter
1 x Seasoning (salt, nutmeg)
- Make noodle dough according to Home-Made Noodle Dough. Roll out dough and cut into wide noodle, about 25 mm wide.
- Wash, trim, and clean vegetables, such as carrots, celery and zucchini.
- Cut vegetables into fine julienne.
- Briefly blanch vegetables and drain.
- BOIL NOODLES AL DENTE IN AMPLE SALTED WATER AND DRAIN.
- HEAT BUTTER AND SAUTÉ VEGETABLES.
- TOSS HOT NOODLES WITH VEGETABLES. SEASON TO TASTE WITH SALT AND NUTMEG.
Note: If boiled noodles are not used right away, rinse with cold water, drain again, and blend with small amount of oil to prevent noodles from sticking together.
