SALMON CUTLETS WITH MUSTARD CREAM SAUCE

Preparation: Cut salmon trout fillets (skinless) into 20 cutlets. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Brush with melted butter, place in a 1/1 gastro norm insert, and steam. Keep warm.

Sauce: Combine white wine, fish stock, and chicken stock. Bring to a boil and reduce by half. Remove from heat and whisk in cold butter in small nuggets—do not allow sauce to return to a boil. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Fold in lightly whipped heavy cream and mild prepared mustard.

Garnish: Peel cucumbers, remove seeds, and slice. Sauté in butter. Peel tomatoes, remove seeds, cut into concasse. Add to cucumbers with fresh chopped dill. Season to taste.

Plating: Pour sauce over salmon cutlets and garnish with cucumber-tomato mixture.

Yield: 5 servings
