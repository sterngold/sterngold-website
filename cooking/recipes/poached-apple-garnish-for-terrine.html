Poached Apple garnish for terrine

Production

Poached Apple garnish for terrine
Pochierte Äpfelgarnitur for Terrine
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Garde-manger special
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 14.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.18 / EUR 0.12


Ingredients for 20 servings at 100% [Net quantity)
800 g Apples
320 ml White wine (Sylvaner or Riesling)
1 I Water
32 ml Lemon juice
130 g Crystal sugar
1 pc Saffron, pinch
- PEEL APPLES AND KEEP IN LEMON WATER CUT APPPLES, SEE COURSE INSTRUCTION
- COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS, EXCEPT APPLES AND BRING TO A BOIL - ADD APPLES AND POACH UNTIL WELL DONE
- COOL DOWN IN STOCK IN ICE WATER
- Take out, place on wire rack and garnish with medallion of hot smoked poultry medallion [see course instruction) and vegetables.
POACHED APPLE GARNISH FOR TERRINE
Chef Patrick Diethelm | DCT | 14.11.2005

Prepare: Peel apples and keep in lemon water. Cut apples as per course instruction.

Poaching liquid: Combine white wine (Sylvaner or Riesling), water, lemon juice, crystal sugar, and a pinch of saffron. Bring to boil.

Cooking: Add apples and poach until well done. Cool down in stock in ice water.

Finish: Remove apples and place on wire rack. Garnish with medallion of hot smoked poultry medallion (see course instruction) and vegetables.
