CHATEAU POTATOES
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Recipe #621 | Cost: CHF 0.22

Ingredients (3 servings):
500 g Charlotte potatoes
30 ml peanut oil
15 g butter
15 g salt
Salt and pepper to season

Method:
Wash and peel potatoes. Turn into château size (turned potatoes, approximately 5 cm).

Blanch in salted water until nearly cooked. Drain and spread on sheet pan to cool quickly and stop cooking.

Heat oven to 210°C. Add oil to roasting pan and heat 10 minutes until smoking.

Add potatoes and roast in oven at 200°C, turning occasionally until golden brown and fully cooked. Handle carefully to avoid breaking.

Pour off excess oil. Add butter and continue roasting briefly, turning potatoes gently to coat evenly with butter.

Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
