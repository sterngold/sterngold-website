Chateau Potatoes
Schlosskartoffeln Pommes chateau

Recipe # : 621
Category : ECA - Entremetier/ side dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 25.10.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.22 / EUR 0.14
Ingredients for 3 servings at 100% (Net quantity)

500 g Potatoes- Type A Charllote


15 g Salt
30 ml Peanut oil
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper)
15 g Butter
Wash and peel potatoes.
TURN INTO CHATEAU SIZE Blanch in salted water, drain, and spread out to cool quickly and to prevent further cooking.

- HEAT UP OVEN TO 210°C AND ADD ROTISSOIRE FOR 10 MIN. - ADD OIL AND POTATOES
- CONTINUE ROASTING IN THE OVEN AT 200°C, TURNING THE POTATOES UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN AND COOKED.
- BE CAREFUL NOT TO BREAK POTATOES.
- POUR OFF EXCESS OIL, ADD BUTTER, AND CONTINUE ROASTING BRIEFLY, TURNING LIGHTLY IN THE BUTTER TO COAT EVENLY.
