Garde Manger Salad

Tonin salade

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: Franken

Production

Бял лук – нарязан на ситно
1 или 2 сварени яйца
малко зелен лук
каперси
лимонов сок – доста
сол
майонеза
кисело мляко – yogurt
Риба тон
в хладилник до 6 дни може да се държи
INGREDIENTS:
Finely diced white onion, 1 or 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped green onion, capers, generous lemon juice, salt, mayonnaise, yogurt or sour cream, and canned tuna.

COMBINE all ingredients in a bowl, adjusting lemon juice and salt to taste. Mix gently to maintain structure. Hold in cooler up to 6 days.
From Franken · Chef V. I. Sterngold
