STUFFED QUAIL WITH KIRSCH SAUCE

Yield: 8 servings


8 quails, dressed
50 ml clarified butter
50 ml Zuger Kirsch
200 ml quail juice
50 g butter

Stuffing:
20 g chopped shallots
10 g butter  60 g apple
120 g chopped mushrooms
90 g fresh mie de pain
40 g blanched bacon brunoise
20 ml kirsch
30 g egg yolk
30 ml cream
chopped basil and parsley
salt and pepper mill

Stuffing: Sweat the chopped mushroom until dry, add shallots,bacon and apple 3x3mm, deglaze with kirsch
Add into a bowl and stir in remaining ingredients
Season to taste with salt, pepper and herbs

Quail mise en place: Unbone the quails hollow very carefully without perforating the skin. Season the inside with salt and pepper. Stuff the quails and sew the opening.

Sauce: Use the carcasse and prepare a kirsch sauce with thyme.

Preparation: Season the quails and roast at 220° C for about 10-15 min, basting frequently. Let rest for about 10 min.

Vegetable: Glaze all the prepared blanched vegetables.

Remove the skewer from the quail and arrange with starch dish and glazed vegetables. - brush quails with little glaze
Pour some sauce around each portion, arrange and garnish with some fresh herbs. and glazed cherrys.
STUFFED QUAIL WITH KIRSCH SAUCE
Yield: 8 servings

Stuffing:
1. Sweat chopped mushrooms until dry. Add shallots, bacon brunoise, and apple (3×3mm dice). Deglaze with kirsch.
2. Transfer to bowl and fold in remaining ingredients: mie de pain, egg yolk, cream, blanched bacon, chopped basil and parsley. Season with salt and pepper mill.

Quail Mise en Place:
1. Carefully unbone quails without perforating skin.
2. Season cavity with salt and pepper.
3. Stuff each quail and sew opening closed.

Sauce:
Prepare kirsch sauce from carcasses with thyme.

Cooking:
1. Season quails and roast at 220°C for 10–15 minutes, basting frequently.
2. Rest for 10 minutes.
3. Glaze all blanched vegetables.
4. Remove skewer from quail.
5. Brush quails lightly with glaze. Arrange with starch dish and glazed vegetables.
6. Pour sauce around each portion. Garnish with fresh herbs and glazed cherries.
