- Clean the octopus, and put them in a pot with the vinegar and the water. - Add sachet bag and season with salt
- Bring it to a boil, add cork, cover it, and simmer the octopus until it's tender.
- Drain the octopus, trim the suckers (which are unpleasantly rubbery) from the tentacles, and dice the octopus, putting the pieces in gastronorm or pastry bowl.
- Season with chopped basil and parsley, salt and pepper mill
- Add extra vergine olive oil
- Serve with tomato confit, black olives and crostinis
