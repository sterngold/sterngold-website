SMOKED FISH PARFAIT TERRINE
Zug-Lucerne, Switzerland
Yield: 10 servings

INGREDIENTS
150 g Smoked fish fillets
20 g Fine chopped shallots
50 g Tomato concassé
50 g Button mushrooms, sliced
10 g Butter
50 g Noilly Prat
50 g White wine
150 g Fish stock
150 g Cream
5 g Chopped dill
8 g Sea salt
15 g Freshly grated horseradish, chopped
Pepper, lemon juice to taste
4 sheets Gelatine
30 g Cream
150 g Whipped cream (soft peaks)
5–6 Freshly smoked fish fillets

METHOD
Sweat shallots, mushrooms, and tomato concassé in butter. Deglaze with Noilly Prat and white wine, add fish stock and reduce to syrup. Add 150 g cream and reduce to desired consistency. Cool completely. Skin and bone smoked fillets, blend with cooled sauce in food processor, and season. Pass through drum sieve. Skin and fillet fresh smoked fish. Soak gelatine in cold water, squeeze dry, and dissolve in 30 g cream. Whisk into fish mousse. Line terrine mold with plastic film. Fold dill, horseradish, and whipped cream into mousseline. Layer with remaining fillets in mold. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. Unmold and slice carefully.
