SMOKED FISH PARFAIT TERRINE
Yield: 10 servings
150 g smoked fish fillets
20 g fine chopped shallots
50 g tomato concasse
50 g button mushrooms sliced
10 g butter
50 g noilly prat
50 g white wine
150 g fish stock
150 g cream
5 g chopped dill
8 g sea salt
15 g freshly grated, chopped horseradish
8 g sea salt
pepper mill, lemon juice
4 sheet gelatine
30 g cream
150 g whipped cream (soft)
5-6 freshly smoked clean fillets
Preparation:
Sweat the shallots, button mushroom and tomato concassé in butter
Add the noilly prat, white wine and fish stock nd reduce to syrup
Pour in the cream and reduce to сГesired consistency
Cool down

Remove the skin and fat from the smoked fish fillets and blend in food processor with the 3 cold fish sauce and season
- Pass through drum sieve.


Remove the skin from the freshly smoked lokal fish and fillet.
Soak the gelatine in cold water, squeeze out and dissolve in 30 g cream.
Stir into fish mousse with a whisk.

Line out terrine mold first with plastic foil
*- Fold in the dill, horseradish, whipped cream into mousseline and fill with remaining fillets into prepared terrine mold
Cover and rest for one hour in refrigerator
Unmold the parfait, slice carefully.
