SPINACH LASAGNA WITH MEAT SAUCE | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Yield: 20 servings

Ingredients:
1 kg green lasagna sheets (purchased or fresh)
100 g salt
40 g butter
1 kg Bolognese sauce
1 L cream sauce
100 g Parmesan cheese, grated
50 g butter, melted

Method:
Add salt to water and bring to a boil. Cook lasagna sheets until al dente. Butter an ovenproof gratin dish. Heat both sauces. Spread a layer of cream sauce on the bottom of the dish. Cover with half the lasagna sheets, spread with Bolognese sauce, and top with remaining lasagna sheets. Cover with remaining cream sauce. Sprinkle with cheese and melted butter. Bake in oven until hot and brown on top.
