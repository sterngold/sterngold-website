Saucier Cold Appetizer

Foie gras dóie truffle

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Ingredients

500 g Stuffed goose liver
10 g ml Madeira wine
30 g ml Port wine, red
2 g Crystal sugar
40 g Fleur de sel
1 g White pepper, fine mill

Production

- Add the broken up goose liver for 1 hour into ice water
- Clean the liver and remove all the senew and blood parts
- spread the liver out and place on parchment papper
- mariante with all the ingrdients and place into vaccum bag
- vaccum pack and marinate for 1 hour
- poach in combi steamer at 65 C for 10 minutes in Bain Marie
- Center should not be higher than 42 C
- Place vacuum bag into ice water and refresh until cold

- Slice the clean truffel into lam de de truffle
- Take liver out of bag and place thin sliced truffles on it
(place on plasitc folio and spread out)
- Roll tightly up and form sausage
- wrap the roll into plasric foil and alufoil and chill for 1 day

- For servingunwrap from alu and lsice 5 mm thick
- Sprinkle with fleur de sle and fresh crushed pink pepper corn and serve with brioche
Soak broken goose liver in ice water for 1 hour. Clean liver thoroughly, removing all sinew and blood. Spread liver on parchment paper. Marinate with all ingredients, place in vacuum bag, and seal. Marinate for 1 hour. Poach in combi steamer at 65°C for 10 minutes in bain-marie, ensuring center does not exceed 42°C. Chill in ice water until cold.

Slice truffle into thin sheets. Remove liver from bag and layer thin truffle slices over it (on plastic film). Roll tightly into a sausage shape. Wrap in plastic foil and aluminum foil, chill for 1 day.

For service: Unwrap from aluminum foil and slice 5 mm thick. Sprinkle with fleur de sel and freshly crushed pink peppercorn. Serve with brioche.
