SOUR CREAM ICE CREAM | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | For 6 servings

Ingredients:
500 ml sour cream (35%)
150 g vanilla sugar
50 g lemon juice
3 g lemon zest

Method:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl and transfer to a beaker. Freeze overnight. Pacojet twice and return to freezer for 30 minutes. Serve with fresh fruits.
