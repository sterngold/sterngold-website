ORANGE GARNISH FOR PÂTÉ
Category: ECA - Garde Manger | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 28.11.2005

Ingredients (5 servings):
- 5 oranges
- 2.5 leaves gelatin
- 3 g fresh peppermint
- 1 orange (for juice and zest)
- 125 ml apple juice
- Cayenne pepper to taste

Method:
1. Wash oranges and remove zest.
2. Blanch orange zest and cook with grenadine syrup; set aside for garnish.
3. Segment oranges, removing flesh carefully; reserve juice.
4. Dissolve soaked, squeezed gelatin in warm apple juice. Combine with strained orange juice and remaining apple juice.
5. Fill silpat molds or small timbales with dry orange segments.
6. Add one-third of chopped cooked zest and chopped mint.
7. Fill with apple juice-gelatin mixture.
8. Chill 1 hour until set; unmold carefully and cool.
9. Garnish with mascarpone or crème fraîche, corn flakes chips, and mint before service.
