Orange garnish for pate
Orangengarnitur für Pastete Garniture a /'orange


Recipe #
Category : ECA - Garde-manger special
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 28.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.28 / EUR 0.18

EuropeanGourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 5 servings at 100% (Net quantity)

5
pc
Orange
2.5
pc
Gelatin, leaves
3
g
Peppermint, fresh
1
pc
Orange (juice and grated peel)
125
ml
Apple juice
1
x
Ceyenne pepper



- WASH THE ORANGE AND REMOVE ZEST
- COOK THE BLANCHED ORANGE ZEST WITH SOME GRENADINE SYRUP FOR GARNISH
- FILLET THE ORANGE AND REMOVE THE SEGMENTS, RESERVE THE JUICE
- DISSOLVE THE SOAKED AND SQUEEZED GELATIN WITH SOME WARM APPLE JUICE. ADD THE STRAINED ORANGE JUICE AND REMAINING APPLE JUICE
- FILL UP THE SILPAT MOLDS
OR SMALL TIMBALES WITH THE DRY ORANGE SEGMENTS
- ADD 1/3 OF THE CHOPPED COOKED ZESTE AND CHOPPED MINT
- FILL UP WITH APPLE JUICE MIXTURE
- COOL DOWN FOR 1 HOUR UNTIL FIRM
- UNMOLD CAREFULLY
- COOL DOWN
- UNMOLD AND GARNISH WITH SOME MASCARPONE OR QUIMIQ AND SMALL CORN FLAKES CHIPS AND SOME MINT DECOR.
