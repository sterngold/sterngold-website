Production

Italian mashed Potatoes with olive oil
Kartoffelpüree mit Olivenoel Pommes mousseline et huile d 'olive

Recipe # : 609
Category : ECA - Entremetier/ side dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 15.12.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.27 / EUR 0.17

European Gourrrtet cuisine

Ingredients for 9 servings at 100% [Net quantity)

960 g Potatoes Bintje
9 g Salt
140 ml Olive oil
50 ml Heavy cream (36%)
1 x Nutmeg (pinch)


Wash, peel and cut into large chunks.

- STEAM POTATO CHUNKS
- WHILE POTATOES ARE STILL HOT, PASS THEM THROUGH A FOOD MILL. - BRING CREAM TO A BOIL ADD HOT CREAM TO POTATOES.
- ADD OLIVE OIL AND STIR TO MAKE A SMOOTH PURÉE.
SEASON TO TASTE WITH NUTMEG AND SALT AND WHITE GROUND PEPPER.
Note: The amount of Cream and Olive oil varies, depending on the type of potatoes used and the moisture content.
