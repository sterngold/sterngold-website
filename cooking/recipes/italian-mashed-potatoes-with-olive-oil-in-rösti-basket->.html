ITALIAN MASHED POTATOES WITH OLIVE OIL
Recipe #609 | Chef Patrick Diethelm | 15.12.2005
Yield: 9 servings (100g each) | Cost: CHF 0.27

INGREDIENTS
960 g Bintje potatoes
9 g Salt
140 ml Olive oil
50 ml Heavy cream (36%)
1 pinch Nutmeg
White ground pepper to taste

METHOD
Wash, peel, and cut potatoes into large chunks. Steam until tender.

While potatoes are still hot, pass through a food mill. Bring cream to a boil and add to the hot potatoes immediately.

Stir in olive oil to create a smooth purée. Season to taste with nutmeg, salt, and white pepper.

NOTE: Adjust cream and olive oil ratios according to potato moisture content and variety.
