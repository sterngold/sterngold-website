Garde Manger Cold Appetizer

Cake can carapaudinebiet en buffelmozzarella

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Ingredients

60 pcs piece Egg
6.0 kg piece Egg
160 g Flour, white wheat
1.0 kg Salt
Aubergine
Red beat

Production

Cut and grill aubergine
Place baking papper in the terrine form
Place the Aubergine
Beat eggs very well
Add flour, salt, cayanne pepper
Add layer mixture, layer red beit (clean and long strips), layer mixture, layer mozzarella, layer grilled aubergine
Salt every biet
Bake 180 C - till is ready - around 30 - 35 min
20 min with lid on - 10 min without lid
Cut and grill aubergine. Line terrine mold with baking paper. Beat eggs thoroughly with flour, salt, and cayenne pepper to create a binding mixture. Layer as follows in the mold: aubergine, egg mixture, red beet strips (cleaned and cut lengthwise, seasoned with salt), egg mixture, buffalo mozzarella, grilled aubergine. Bake at 180°C for 30–35 minutes total: 20 minutes covered with lid, then 10 minutes uncovered, until set.
