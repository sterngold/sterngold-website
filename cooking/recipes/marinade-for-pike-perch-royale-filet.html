MARINADE FOR PIKE PERCH ROYALE FILET

Ingredients for 8 servings:
100 ml olive oil
100 ml sherry vinegar
200 ml beer
60 ml fish stock
30 g shallots
2 sprigs fresh thyme
30 g ground ginger
60 g lemongrass (or additional ginger)
40 ml lemon juice
10 g salt
2 g pink peppercorns

Method:
Combine all ingredients. Use to marinate pike perch fillets.
