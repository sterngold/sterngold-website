Glazed chicken ballotine

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Glazed chicken ballotine
Geflügelballotine

Ingredients for 6 servings at 100% (Net quantity)

- Debone poularde for ballotine (see demo and detailed instruction)
- Mix cream, egg, trimmed chicken leg meat and chill in freezer for 5 minutes add salt and pacotice twice in pacojet. (or freeze over night and pacotice)
- Remove and chill on ice
- Add list of condiments or any other seasonal ingredients
- SEE COURSE DEMONSTRATION
- Pipe farce on debonesd poularde and roll to even size ballotine. - Tie carefully with kitchen twine and chill.
- For the final stage of cooking use the cooking method glazing or roasting. (see traditional cooking methods)
- The cooking time will be approx. 20 Minutes and 5 Minutes resting time !!
Important; use directly reduced Fond de veau brun !!!
