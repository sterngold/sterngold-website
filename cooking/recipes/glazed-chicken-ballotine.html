**GLAZED CHICKEN BALLOTINE**

Yield: 6 servings | Station: Saucier

**Method:**
1. Debone poularde for ballotine (see demonstration for detailed technique).
2. Prepare farce: combine cream, egg, and trimmed chicken leg meat. Chill in freezer 5 minutes. Process twice in pacojet. (Alternatively, freeze overnight and process.) Remove and chill over ice.
3. Fold in seasonal condiments or garnish ingredients as desired.
4. Pipe farce evenly onto deboned poularde skin. Roll to consistent ballotine shape.
5. Tie carefully with kitchen twine and chill thoroughly.
6. Cook using glazing or roasting method (see traditional cooking methods) for approximately 20 minutes, followed by 5 minutes rest.

**Critical:** Use only freshly reduced brown veal stock (fond de veau brun) for finishing.
