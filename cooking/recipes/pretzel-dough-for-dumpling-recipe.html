PRETZEL DOUGH FOR DUMPLING RECIPE
Yield: 800g

Ingredients:
250g water
20g yeast
5g malt
10g salt
25g peanut oil
500g white wheat flour

Dough Preparation:
Mix water, yeast, malt, flour, and salt together until a dough forms. Three-quarters through kneading, incorporate the oil, then the salt. Bulk ferment 15-20 minutes.

Caustic Soda Solution:
Boil 200g warm water with 10g sodium hydroxide discs until completely dissolved. Cool to approximately 40°C before use.

Final Preparation:
Divide dough into 2 pieces and shape into long loaves. After brief rest, form into standard bread loaves. Proof lightly, then place in cool storage. Before baking, dip loaves in caustic soda solution, transfer to parchment paper, and sprinkle generously with coarse salt. Bake at 175°C with open steam escape.
