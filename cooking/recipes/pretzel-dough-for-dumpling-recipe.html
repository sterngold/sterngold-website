Dough Preparation
Mix the dough ingredients to a dough without the oil and salt.
After three quarters of the kneading time add the oil, followed by the salt. Bulk fermentation time 15 to 20 minutes.
Caustic soda water solution
in trade offer:
Caustic soda solution below 5% strength is available ready for use and without poison certificate (max. 4,9%).
b) ones own preparation of the soda solution.
200 g warm water
10 g Sodium hydroxide, pure, sold in discs
Boil the water and sodium Hydroxide discs together until the discs are completely dissolved.

Temperature of use approx. 40°C. Final preparation
Divide dough into 2 pieces and mould out long. After a short recovery shape into normal bread loaves. Allow to prove a little and then place in a cool place. Before baking, dip in the caustic soda solution, place on parchment paper and sprinkle with coarse salt. Bake at 175 ° C with an open steam escape.
