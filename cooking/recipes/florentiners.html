Florentiners

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

1.
300 g sugar
600 g butter
900 g flour
3 eggs
(doug - blind bake 20 min)

2.
550 g botter
5,5 dl cream
550 g honey
1170 g sugar
(do all in a pan)

3.
1070 almonds (cut)
800 g cheries (from thin - cut small)
300 g rosijnen
150 g lemon rasp

(use 1/3 of the recipe )
(bake on the doug for 25 min on 170 g )
FLORENTINERS | VandeMarkt Pâtisserie

Dough:
Cream 300 g sugar with 600 g butter. Mix in 900 g flour and 3 eggs. Press into pan and blind bake at 170°C for 20 minutes.

Topping:
Combine 550 g butter, 550 ml cream, 550 g honey, and 1170 g sugar in a pan. Heat together.

Finishing:
Prepare 1070 g almonds (cut), 800 g cherries (cut small), 300 g raisins, and 150 g lemon zest. Use one-third of this mixture. Spread over dough and bake at 170°C for 25 minutes.
From VandeMarkt · Chef V. I. Sterngold
