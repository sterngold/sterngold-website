Make dough as in Home Made Noodle Dough, but using the ingredients in this recipe. Let dough rest in refrigerator.
PREPARATION FOR VEGETABLES

Cut cabbage into small dice, boil or steam until done, and drain. Cut bacon into small dice, blanch, and drain.

METHOD FOR NOODLES
Roll out dough to about 2 mm thick.
Cut dough into noodles about 12 mm wide.
Boil noodles al dente in ample salt water, drain, rinse in cold water, and drain again.
METHOD FOR VEGETABLES
Butter an ovenproof dish [gratin dish) with some of the butter.
Sauté bacon and all vegetables in remaining butter, toss with noodles, and season to taste with salt and pepper and nutmeg.
Place noodle mixture in gratin dish, add cream sprinkle with cheese, and bake until hot and brown on top. Blend flour, paprika, salt and pepper.
METHOD FOR GARNISH
Dredge onion rings in the flour mixture and shake off excess. Deep-fry onion rings at 180°C until golden brown.
At the moment of service, place onion rings on top of baked noodles.
Heat butter in a deep cast iron pan, add sage, and pour foaming over the noodles and onions.
NOODLE DOUGH
Make dough with white wheat flour, buckwheat flour, egg, olive oil, milk, and salt as per standard homemade noodle dough recipe. Rest dough in refrigerator. Roll to 2mm thickness and cut into noodles 12mm wide. Boil al dente in ample salted water, drain, rinse in cold water, and drain again.

VEGETABLE PREPARATION
Peel and dice potatoes, blanch, and drain. Blanch spinach, drain, rinse in cold water, and drain. Trim and halve green beans, blanch, and drain. Dice cabbage, boil or steam until done, and drain. Dice bacon, blanch, and drain.

ASSEMBLY
Butter an ovenproof gratin dish. Sauté bacon and all vegetables in butter, combine with noodles, and season to taste with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Transfer to gratin dish, add cream, sprinkle with grated Sbrinz, and bake until golden and hot.

GARNISH
Peel onions and cut into thin rings. Blend flour with paprika, salt, and pepper. Dredge onion rings in flour mixture, shake off excess, and deep-fry at 180°C until golden. At service, place fried onions on top. Heat butter in cast iron pan with chopped sage, let foam, and pour over noodles and onions at the moment of service.
