TOMATO EGGPLANT TERRINE EN GELLE

Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients:
1 eggplant (20 cm long)
10 ripe, tasty tomatoes
40 g sugar
20 g finely chopped garlic
10 g finely chopped rosemary
Chicken broth
Raspberry vinegar
Balsamic vinegar
3 sheets gelatine (for 110 g bouillon)
Salt and pepper
Olive oil
Green and black olives, basil, seasonal salad for garnish

Method:
Slice eggplant lengthwise into 3 mm slices on meat slicer. Deep fry until light golden (soft, not crispy). Peel tomatoes, quarter them, and remove seeds with paring knife. In non-stick pan, combine sugar with crushed garlic and chopped rosemary, allow to melt. Add tomatoes. Deglaze with raspberry vinegar, then balsamic vinegar. Add beef consommé, cover, and braise quickly. Strain juice and reserve; place tomatoes on a tray. Reduce juice for vinaigrette base. Layer eggplant and tomato mixture in terrine form (30 cm × 5 cm, 5 dl volume). Bloomed gelatine dissolved in reduced juice binds the terrine. Set for at least 4 hours. Serve with olives, basil, and seasonal salad.
