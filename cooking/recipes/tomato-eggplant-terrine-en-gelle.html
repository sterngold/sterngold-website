Tomato Eggplant Terrine en Gelle

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Yield: 8 servings
1 eggplant, 20 cm long
10 tasty, ripe tomatoes 40 g sugar
20 g fine chopped garlic
10 g fine chopped rosemary
chicken broth in gastro norm
raspberry vinegar bottle balsamic vinegar bottle
3 sheet gelatine for 110 gram of bouillon salt, pepper mill
Terrine form 30cm x 5cm, 5 dl volume Olive oil for vinaigгette
Garnish:
green and black olives basil
seasonal salad
Preparation:
Slice eggplant 3 mm thick lenthwise on meat slicer and deep fry until light golden (soft, not crispy)
Peel tomatoes, cut into quarter and remove seeds with paring knife
Prepare remaining rise en place!!
