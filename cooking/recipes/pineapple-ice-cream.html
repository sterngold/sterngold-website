Pineapple ice cream

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Pineapple ice cream


Апапавд/асе
Recipe # :
Category : ECA - Paco jet
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date :1711.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.55/ EUR 0.35

European Gourmet Cuisine

Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% [Net quantity)
400
g
Pineapple, fresh peeled
200
g
Heavy cream
80
g
Egg yolk past.
140
g
Crystal sugar

- Cut peeled pineapple into chunks and fill into pacojet beaker
- Add egg yolk and crystal sugar into bowl and whip until foamy - Bring cream to a boil and stir into foamy egg mixture - Add back to sauteuse and stir until starts to thicken
* * * NO SCRAMBLED EGGS !!!! * * *
- Strain through china cap and pour into beaker and freeze - Pacotice
