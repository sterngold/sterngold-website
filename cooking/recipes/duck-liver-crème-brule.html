Garde Manger Cold Appetizer

Duck liver crème Brule

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen

Ingredients

600 g Cream
400 g Milk
100 g Cognac
Thyme, fresh chopped
Rosemary
Seasoning
400 g liver

Production

boil, simmer and reduction. 400 g liver.
Mix with the hot mass. Bring boil to boil. Fill / chill.
From Tschuggen · Chef Ulf Wein
