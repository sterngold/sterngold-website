Brussels Sprouts

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Sautéed Brussels Sprouts
SautierterRosenkohl Choux de Bruxelles sautés


Recipe ii : 567
Category : ECA - Entremetier/ side dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date :07.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.82 / EUR 0.52


Ingredients for 4 servings at 100% (Net quantity) 400 g Brussel sprouts
3 g Salt
24 g Butter
15 g Shallots
30 g Air dried deer
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper)
25 g Pine seeds
Wash, trim. Boil in salted water (or steam) until almost done. Drain and refresh.


Peel and chop.

- REMOVE FIRST THE NICE LEAFS. BLANCH AND REFRESH
- KEEP ON THE SIDE
- SAUTE SHALLOTS AND AIR DRIED DEER BRUNOISE IN BUTTER.
- ADD BRUSSELS SPROUTS . SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER AND SAUTÉ UNTIL HOT WITH ADDING A LITTLE VEGETABLE STOCK .
- ROAST THE PINE SEEDS AND SPRINKLE OVER THE BRUSSEL SPROUTS
