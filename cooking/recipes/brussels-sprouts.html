SAUTÉED BRUSSELS SPROUTS

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:
400 g Brussels sprouts
3 g salt
24 g butter
15 g shallots
30 g air-dried venison
25 g pine seeds
Salt and pepper to taste

Method:
Wash and trim Brussels sprouts. Boil in salted water (or steam) until almost done. Drain and refresh. Remove the nice outer leaves, blanch, refresh, and reserve.

Peel and chop shallots. Cut air-dried venison into brunoise.

Sauté shallots and venison brunoise in butter. Add Brussels sprouts, season with salt and pepper, and sauté until hot, adding a little vegetable stock as needed.

Toast pine seeds lightly and sprinkle over the Brussels sprouts before service.
