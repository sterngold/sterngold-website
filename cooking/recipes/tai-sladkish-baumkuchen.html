Tai Sladkish (Baumkuchen)

Chef: Jan Wasch Source: Vijf

Production

300 gr. Bloem
20 egs – 30 gr. Whites / 30 gr. Yolks
650 gr. Gesmolten botter
4,5 lepels kaneelpowder
3 leepels anijspowder
1 leepel kruidnagel powder
2 leepel nootmuskat
2 leepel kardamon
1 zaad vanilie stokje
600 gr. Sugar
1. 20 x 30 gr egg whites + 150 gr sugar
2. 20 x 30 gr yolks +150 gr sugar
3. 650 gr. Botter + 300 gr. Suger + 1 vanillie zaad + 300 gr bloem + salt
4. divide in two pieces – one with the kruiden
**Tai Sladkish (Baumkuchen)** | Vijf, Pâtisserie

**Ingredients:**
- 300 g flour
- 20 egg whites (30 g each) + 150 g sugar
- 20 egg yolks (30 g each) + 150 g sugar
- 650 g melted butter
- 300 g sugar
- 4.5 tsp cinnamon powder
- 3 tsp anise powder
- 1 tsp clove powder
- 2 tsp nutmeg
- 2 tsp cardamom
- 1 vanilla pod
- Salt

**Method:**
1. Whip egg whites with 150 g sugar to soft peaks.
2. Whip egg yolks with 150 g sugar until pale.
3. Cream melted butter with 300 g sugar, split vanilla pod, 300 g flour, and salt.
4. Divide butter mixture into two portions—add spice blend (cinnamon, anise, clove, nutmeg, cardamom) to one portion only.
5. Fold whipped whites and yolks into respective butter mixtures.
6. Proceed with baumkuchen assembly.
From Vijf · Chef Jan Wasch
