Preparation

- Rub the inside of Fondue caquelon pot with the garlic clove

- Add wine to caquelon and heat up

- Sprinkle in the cheese and melt over heat, stirring continuesly

until smooth and creamy

- Make а slury with Kirsch and Corn starch; add to boiling cheese mixture

to thicken to creamy fondue

- Season with pepper mill and freshly ground nutmeg

- Serve immediately and place Caquelon on Rechaud (Food warmer)

- Serve fresh white bread cubes with hot fondue

- Serve with good chilled dry white wine or black tea
