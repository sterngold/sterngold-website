SWISS CHEESE FONDUE BASIS

Yield: 2-4 servings
Chef Instructor: Patrick Diethelm

Ingredients:
0.5 garlic clove, peeled
150 g dry white wine
300 g Gruyere cheese or fondue mixture, grated
15 g cornstarch
White pepper (from mill)
Nutmeg (pinch)
20 g Kirsch, Calvados, or Brandy
250 g white bread, cut into 2.5 cm cubes

Method:
1. Rub the inside of fondue caquelon with peeled garlic clove.
2. Add wine to caquelon and heat.
3. Sprinkle in cheese and melt over heat, stirring continuously until smooth and creamy.
4. Make a slurry with Kirsch and cornstarch; add to boiling cheese mixture to thicken to creamy consistency.
5. Season with freshly ground white pepper and nutmeg.
6. Serve immediately, placing caquelon on rechaud (food warmer).
7. Serve with fresh white bread cubes alongside hot fondue.
8. Accompany with chilled dry white wine or black tea.
