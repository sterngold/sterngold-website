Garde Manger Sauce cold

Chutney s  mango / pears

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen

Ingredients

1.0 kg Pumpkin
400 g Sugar
50 g Lemon juice
50 g Vinegar, white wine

Production

Make caramel – induction / pan with sugar / on 3 or 5 / golden
Add 1 part lemon juice (4 pieces) 1 part white vinegar
Add the salpicon and cook on 5 / 6
When ready add finely cut red chilly and with blender
Add the macedoine – cook till tender
