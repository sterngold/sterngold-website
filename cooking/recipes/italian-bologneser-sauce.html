**Italian Bolognese Sauce**

Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 09.08.2005
Yield: 2 liters | Cost: CHF 15.68 / EUR 9.92

**Ingredients:**
- 30 ml olive oil
- 1 kg ground beef
- 60 g butter
- 36 g shallots, peeled and chopped
- 30 g garlic
- 50 g tomato paste
- 1 l canned tomato concassée
- 200 ml red cooking wine
- 800 g brown veal stock (fond de veau brun lié)
- 10 g fresh Italian parsley, chopped
- 5 g fresh oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Method:**
Sauté ground meat in olive oil. Sweat shallots and garlic. Add tomato paste and concassée, continuing to cook. Deglaze with red wine. Add brown veal stock and bring to a boil. Cover and simmer for 1 hour. Season to taste and finish with chopped herbs.
