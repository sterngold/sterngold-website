Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Italian Bologneser Sauce
Ita/ienische B%gnaise Sauce Sauce ita/ienne
Rezept Nr. :62
Kategorie : Sauces
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 09.08.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 15.68/ EUR 9.92

Zutaten fOr 2.000 Liter zu 100% [NettomengeJ

30 ml Olive oil

1 kg Beef ground meat
60 g Butter
36 g Shallots Peel and chop.
30 g Garlic
50 g Tomato paste
1 l * Tomato Concasse Can
200 ml Red cooking wine
800 g Fond de veau brun lie
10 g Parsley. Italian. fresh Chop.
5 g Oregano. fresh
1 x Seasoning [salt and pepper)

- SAUTE MEAT IN OLIVE OIL
- SWEAT SHALLOTS AND GARLIC
- ADD TOMATO PASTE. CONCASSE AND CONTINUE COOKING.
DEGlAZE WITH RED WINE
- ADD BRUN VEAL STOCK AND BRING TO A BOIL.
- COVER AND SIMMER FOR 1 HOUR
- SEASON TO TASTE.
- ADD CHOPPED HERBS
