Garde Manger Bread

Parmesan bread

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen

Production

1. Make a mixture from the parmesan, eggs, herbs, seasoning -
2. Place on one side of the bread and broil under the salamander
3. Repeat on the other side of the toast.

Parmesan
eggs
toast broad
herbs
seasoning

Notes

For the mixture can be used different things - paprika and so on.
