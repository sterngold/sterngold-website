Cumberland Sauce
Cumber/andSauce Sauce Cumber/and
Recipe # : 41
Category : Sauces
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 28.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 4.94 / EUR 3.12
Ingredients for 0.600 liters at 100% (Net quantity)
1 pc Orange
1 pc Lemon
40 ml Red cooking wine
100 ml Red Port wine
4 g Mustard powder
400 g Red currant jelly
16 g Horseradish, fresh Peel horseradish and ginger and grate fine. 6 g Ginger
1 x Seasoning (salt)
- Wash oranges and lemons in hot water, dry with a towel. - Peel outer skin [zest) and cut into very fine julienne. - Blanch zest and boil in red wine until soft.
- Squeeze juice from oranges and lemons.
- MELT RED CURRANT JELLY IN BOWL OVER STEAM
- ADD ORANGE JUICE AND LEMON JUICE TO RED CURRANT JELLY.
- ADD RED WINE/ZEST MIXTURE.
- MIX MUSTARD POWDER WITH PORT WINE; ADD TO FRUIT JUICE/JELLY MIXTURE. - ADD HORSERADISH AND GINGER.
- MIX WELL SEASON TO TASTE, AND CHILL
APPLICATIONS
Serve with cold poultry or game dishes. Serve with meat pates and terrines.
