MELBA (RASPBERRY) SAUCE
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Recipe #719 | Yield: 1 liter

Ingredients:
855 g fresh raspberries
250 g powdered sugar
Lemon juice to taste

Method:
Combine raspberries with powdered sugar. Purée and strain through fine sieve. Season with lemon juice to taste.
