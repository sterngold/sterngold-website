STUFFED SUPREME OF GUINEA- FOWL WITH PUFF PASTRY

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

VITZNAU-LUCERNE SWITZERLAND
RAW POULTRY MOUSSELINE FARCE
120 g lean poultry meat
10 g past. egg white 100 g cream
3 g salt
Preparation:
- Mince the lean poultry meat very fine or blend, also very fine, in a food processor with egg white, salt, cream.
- cool down on ice
- Pass through a drum sieve
- Place the bowl on ice and cool down
- chill on ice and use for your purposes.
POULTRY MOUSSELINE FARCE
Location: Vitznau-Lucerne, Switzerland

Ingredients:
120g lean poultry meat
10g pasteurized egg white
100g cream
3g salt

Method:
1. Mince lean poultry meat very fine or blend in food processor with egg white, salt, and cream until homogeneous.
2. Cool down on ice.
3. Pass through drum sieve.
4. Place bowl on ice and cool down thoroughly.
5. Chill on ice until ready to use.
