Pommes Lorette
Fritirte Kartoffelkrapfen mit Käse Pommes Lorette


Recipe # : 584
Category : Potato Dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 18,10,2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.22 / EUR 0.14

European Gourme t Cuisine
Ingredients for 6 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
420 g Potatoes Bintje, peeled
4 g Salt (optional)
760 g *Choux Paste
50 g Sbrinz Grated

1 x Seasoning (salt, nutmeg)

20 ml Peanut oil
- Cut peeled potatoes into even sized chunks.
- Steam potatoes and drain well.
- When cooked, dry potatoes briefly in the oven at 150°C. on parchment paper with open steam escape - Pass potatoes through a food mill.
- Blend hot puréed potatoes with puff paste and cheese and season to taste with salt and nutmeg. - Cut parchment paper into 50 mm wide strips and oil strips lightly.
- Dress into lorette shape or
- Dress portion size rings onto paper strips, using a pastry bag with a star tube.
- AT SERVICE TIME, HEAT OIL IN DEEP FRYER TO 180°C.
- DIP PAPER STRIPS IN THE HOT OIL; THE POTATO ROSE I I ES WILL SLIDE OFF.
- FRY POTATOES UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN AND DRAIN ON ABSORBENT PAPER. - SERVE ON PAPER DOILIES, AS A GARNISH OR A SIDE DISH.
Note: The paper strips should be long enough for one or two portions. Fry portions to order.
