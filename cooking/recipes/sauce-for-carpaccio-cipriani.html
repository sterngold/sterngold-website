SAUCE FOR CARPACCIO CIPRIANI

Recipe by P. Diethelm | 10 servings

Ingredients:
30 g egg yolk paste
10 g prepared mustard
30 ml olive oil
50 ml sunflower oil
30 ml milk
5 ml lemon juice
1 ml Worcestershire sauce
sea salt and pepper to taste

Method:
1. Prepare mayonnaise with egg yolk paste and mustard.
2. Slowly emulsify in olive oil, then sunflower oil.
3. Add milk, lemon juice, and Worcestershire sauce.
4. Season with sea salt and freshly ground pepper.

Traditional service: Drizzle only with extra virgin olive oil, sea salt, pepper, and Parmesan slices.
