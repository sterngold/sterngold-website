Saucier Sauce hot

Italian Bolognese Sauce

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

30 g ml Olive oil
1.0 kg Beef ground meat
60 g Butter
36 g Shallots
30 g Garlic
50 g Tomato paste
1.0 kg liter Tomato Concasse Can
200 g ml Wine, red, cooking
800 g ml Fond de veau brun lie
10 g Parsley, fresh, chopped
5 g Oregano, fresh
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper

Production

- saute meat in olive oil
- sweat shallots and garlic
- add tomato paste, concasse and continue xooking
- degalze with wine
- add brun veal stock and bring to boil
- cover and simmer for 1 hour
- season to taste
- add chopped herbs
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
