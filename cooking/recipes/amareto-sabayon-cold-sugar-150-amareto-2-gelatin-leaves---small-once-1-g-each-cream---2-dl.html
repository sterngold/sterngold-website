Pâtisserie Patisseri Mise en place

Amareto sabayon (cold) sugar 150 amareto 2 gelatin leaves - small once 1 g each cream - 2 dl

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

egg yolk 6 - 180 g
sugar 150
amareto
2 gelatin leaves - small once 1 g each
cream - 2 dl
AMARETTO SABAYON (COLD) | VandeMarkt Pâtisserie

Whisk 6 egg yolks (180 g) with 150 g sugar until pale.

Add Amaretto liqueur.

Bloom 2 gelatin leaves (1 g each) in cold water, then melt and fold into sabayon.

Whip 200 ml cream to soft peaks and fold into sabayon until combined.
From VandeMarkt · Chef V. I. Sterngold
