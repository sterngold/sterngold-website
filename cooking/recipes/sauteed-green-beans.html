SAUTÉED GREEN BEANS

Recipe by P. Diethelm | 8 servings

Ingredients:
1 kg green beans
100 g butter
72 g onions
10 g garlic
200 g vegetable stock
16 g salt
Black pepper to taste

Method:
1. Blanch green beans and refresh in ice water. Drain well.
2. Peel and chop onions and garlic.
3. Sweat onions and garlic in butter without color.
4. Add blanched green beans. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Add vegetable stock and heat through.
6. Remove beans, arrange, and serve immediately.
