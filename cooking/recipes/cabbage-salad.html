Garde Manger Salad

Cabbage salad

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

1.274999976158142 kg Cabbage
200 g Smoked bacon
135 g Onions
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
1 pcs x Nutmeg (pinch)
10 g Caraway seeds, whole
200 g ml Beef broth powder
# Dressing
20 g Mustard, mild
150 g ml Vinegar, herbs
150 g ml Oil, sunflower

Production

- cabbage - trim and cut into thin slices
- bacon - cut into small dice
- onions - peel and chop

- PUT CABBAGE IN A LARGE BOWL
- SAUTE BACON, ADD ONIONS AND SAUTE LIGHTLY. ADD TO CABBAGE
- SEASON CABBAGE WITH SALT, NUTMEG, CARAWAY SEEDS AND PEPPER
- BRING BOUILLON TO A BOIL AND POUR OVER CABBAGE. LET VABBAGE MARINATE UNTIL COOL
- MIX ALL DRESSING INGREDIENTS AND ADD TO CABBAGE. TOSS TO MIX WELL
CABBAGE SALAD

Prepare ingredients: trim cabbage and cut into thin slices; cut bacon into small dice; peel and chop onions.

Place cabbage in a large bowl. Sauté bacon until crisp, add onions and sauté lightly. Add to cabbage.

Season cabbage with salt, nutmeg, caraway seeds, and pepper. Bring bouillon to a boil and pour over cabbage. Let marinate until cool.

Mix all dressing ingredients together and add to cabbage. Toss thoroughly to combine.

Notes

Red vabbage salad may be made by substituting cabbage, and by using red wine vinegar. See also recipes for salad of red cabbage, apples and grapes
