Boiled Potatoes

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Boiled Potatoes
Salzkartoffeln Pommes nature


Rezept Nr. : 619
Kategorie : Potato Dishes
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 11.08.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 0.09 / EUR 0.06

EUROPEAN CULINARY CENTER

Zutaten für 3 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge) 405 g Potatoes
6 g Salt
Wash and peel potatoes. Cut into even-shaped, even-sized pieces.

- START COOKING POTATOES IN HOT SALTED WATER.
- BRING TO A BOIL AND SKIM. COVER, AND SIMMER UNTIL COOKED. - DRAIN CAREFULLY.
- brush with melted butter and serve
- Or use high compression steamer.
