Onion Sauce

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Onion Sauce
Zwiebe/sauce
Sauce aux oignons (/yonnaisej .
Rezept Nr. : 108
Kategorie : Sauces
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 26.05.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 16.13/ EUR 1 0.20


Zutaten fUr 1.000 Liter zu 100% [Nettomenge)
405 g Onions Peel, cut in half and slice emince
80 g Butter
100 ml Dry white wine
50 ml White wine vinegar
800 g Fond de veau brun lie
10 g Parsley. Italian, fresh
1 x Seasoning (salt)
Chop.
- SAUTE ONIONS IN BUDER UNTIL ABOUT THREE-QUARTERS COOKED.
- ADD WINE AND VINEGAR AND BOIL DOWN UNTIL ALMOST REDUCED.
- ADD BROWN VEAL STOCK AND SIMMER FOR 5 MINUTES.
- SEASON TO TASTE AND ADD PARSLEY.
APPLICATION
Serve with grilled sausages [bratwurst) or boiled beef gretine [miroton de boeuf)
