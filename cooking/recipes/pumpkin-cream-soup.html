Pumpkin cream soup

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 3

Ingredients

48 g Butter
150 g Onions (cut into medium size)
250 g Carrot
120 g Leeks (white leaves only. Wash, trim and cut into medium dice)
36 g Knob Celery (celeriac) (Cut into medium size)
1.5 kg Pimpkin (peel and remove seeds. Cut into small size)
30 g Flour, white wheat
2.4000000953674316 kg liter Veal broth
600 g ml Heavy cream 36%
150 g ml Milk
1 pcs x Salt
6 g Chervil , fresh , chopped

Production

- pumpkin - with melon baller, scoop out 20 small balls to use as garnish, and boil in vegetable stock or steam until cooked

- sweet leeks, onions, carrots and knob celery in butter
- add sliced pumpkin and continue cooking
- dust with flour and stir well
- add stock and bring to boil, stirring continously
- boil until vegetables are soft, skimming occasionally
- stir in cream and milk. Bring to a boil again. Season to taste
- puree with bar blender and strain through fine china cap

- add pumpkin garnish to soup, sprinkle with chervil and serve
PUMPKIN CREAM SOUP

Prepare garnish: Using a melon baller, scoop 20 small pumpkin balls. Cook separately in vegetable stock or steam until tender.

Soup base: Sweat sweet leeks, onions, carrots, and knob celery in butter. Add sliced pumpkin and continue cooking. Dust with flour and stir well. Add stock and bring to boil, stirring continuously.

Simmer until vegetables are soft, skimming occasionally. Stir in cream and milk, bring to boil again. Season to taste.

Finish: Purée with immersion blender and strain through fine china cap. Add reserved pumpkin balls, sprinkle with chervil, and serve.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
