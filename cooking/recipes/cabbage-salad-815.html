Cabbage Salad

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Cabbage Salad
Weisskohlsa/at
Salade de choux blancs
Rezept Nr.: 525
Kategorie: Salads
Koch : Patrick Diethelm Datum: 16.02.2004
Warenkosten: CHF 0.59 / EUR 0.37
Zutaten fOr 10 Personen zu 100'/, (Nettomenge)
1.275 kg Cabbage Trim and cut into thin slices.
200 g Smoked bacon Cut into small dice.
135 g Onions Peel and chop.
1 x Seasoning (salt, nutmeg)
10 g Caraway seeds, whole
1 x Seasoning (black pepper
200 ml *BeefBroth
Dressing:
20 g Prepared mustard, mild
150 ml Herb vinegar
150 ml Sunflower oil
- PUT CABBAGE IN A LARGE BOWL.
- SAUTE BACON, ADD ONIONS, AND SAUTE LIGHTLY. ADD TO CABBAGE.
- SEASON CABBAGE WITH SALT, NUTMEG, CARAWAY SEEDS, AND PEPPER.
- BRING BOUILLON TO A BOIL AND POUR OVER CABBAGE. LET CABBAGE MARINATE UNTIL COOL.
- MIX ALL DRESSING INGREDIENTS AND ADD TO CABBAGE. TOSS TO MIX WELL.
Note: Red Cabbage salad may be made by substituting red cabbage, and by using red wine vinegar. See also recipes for Salad of Red Cabbage, Apples, and Grapes.
