PEPE SAN Paste

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: Tabac Servings: 4

Ingredients

200 g Onions
2 pcs Garlic
6 pcs Chillie peppers
200 g Oil
8 pcs Lime leaves
4 pcs Ginger
200 g Santen
Bouillon
Curry powder
10 pcs Kemerin noten
Cocos milk
Lemon juice

Production

per portie
200 - 250 gr vis
(10 min. stomen)
spanse peper - 1/4
Limoen blad - 3
Bananen blad
Pepe San Paste (per portion)

Method:
- Steam fish (200–250 g) for 10 minutes until cooked through
- Combine steamed fish with ¼ Spanish pepper, 3 lime leaves, and banana leaf
- Process to paste consistency

Serve as directed.
From Tabac · Chef V. I. Sterngold
