Pâtisserie Dessert

Coffee parfait

Chef: Dennis Source: Tschuggen

Ingredients

160 g piece Egg yolk
4 pcs piece Egg
250 g Sugar
250 g ml Coffee (kochen)
200 g Chocolate, dark (couverture)
1.0 kg liter Heavy cream 36% (geschalgene)
1 pcs x Wine, white, dry (weinbrand)
2 pcs el Coffee, instant

Production

-mix egg yolk and eggs and 1/2 sugar on bain marrie
- coffee and rest of the sugar reduce to 100 ml
- mix egg mixture, coffee reduction, couverture and alchohol
- add cream
Whisk egg yolks and whole eggs with half the sugar over bain-marie until ribbon stage. Reduce coffee and remaining sugar to 100 ml. Fold coffee reduction into egg mixture along with couverture and alcohol. Fold in whipped cream.
From Tschuggen · Chef Dennis · 30.03.2006
