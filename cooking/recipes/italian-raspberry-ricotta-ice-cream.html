ITALIAN RASPBERRY RICOTTA ICE CREAM
Recipe #609 | Chef Patrick Diethelm | 17.11.2005
Yield: 10 servings | Cost: CHF 0.64

INGREDIENTS
400 g Frozen raspberries
150 g Ricotta cheese
5 g Lime zest
40 g Vanilla sugar
20 g Glucose syrup
10 g Egg whites
40 g Lime juice
20 g Vanilla sugar (for sauce)
30 ml Water (for sauce)
6 Basil leaves, fresh

METHOD
Combine raspberries, ricotta, vanilla sugar, glucose syrup, lime zest, half the lime juice, and egg whites in a bar blender. Mix until smooth. Transfer to a container and freeze overnight.

Prepare basil-lime sauce: Bring remaining lime juice, water, and 20g vanilla sugar to a boil. Chill completely, then add basil chiffonade.

Pacotise frozen ice cream twice using a Pacojet. Serve with basil-lime sauce.
