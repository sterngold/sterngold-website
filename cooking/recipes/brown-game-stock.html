Brown game stock

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

1.0 kg Os de giber (Chop as small as possible. Rinse bones first with hot, than with cold water)
200 g Game trimmings
200 g Mirepoix for Brown stock
16 g Tomato paste
400 g ml Wine, red, cooking
2.5 kg liter Water
10 g Salt
1 pcs piece Sachet bag (Consisting of bay leaves, cloves, peppercorns and thyme, juniper berries)

Production

- roast bones and trimmings slowly in rotisoir at 200 C
- add mirepoix and continue roasting
- pour off excess fat
- add tomato paste and carefully continue roasting
- add a small amount of water two or three times and boil down each time, until almost evaporated
- if bones are roasted in oven transfer bones and pan drippings to a suitable marmite
- add remaining water and wine
- bring to boil add slat and skim
- simmer for 3 to 4 hours
- add sachet bag for final hour
- carefully strain through double layer of cheesecloth
Roast bones and trimmings slowly in rotisserie at 200°C. Add mirepoix and continue roasting. Pour off excess fat. Add tomato paste and carefully continue roasting. Add small amounts of water two or three times, boiling down each time until almost evaporated. Transfer bones and pan drippings to a suitable marmite. Add remaining water and wine. Bring to boil, add salt, and skim. Simmer for 3 to 4 hours. Add sachet bag for the final hour. Carefully strain through double layer of cheesecloth.

Notes

Depending on final use, red wine can be replaced by white wine.
