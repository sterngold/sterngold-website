BÉARNAISE SAUCE
Recipe #33 | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 16.08.2005
Yield: 500 ml

INGREDIENTS
500 ml Hollandaise sauce
15 ml Tarragon vinegar
30 ml Dry white wine
5 g Fresh tarragon
3 g Fresh chervil
Salt to taste

METHOD
Wash and chop tarragon leaves. Wash and chop chervil leaves.

Combine tarragon, tarragon vinegar, and dry white wine in saucepan. Reduce over heat until almost completely evaporated.

Blend the reduction with hollandaise sauce. Fold in chopped chervil. Season to taste with salt.

APPLICATIONS
Serve with sautéed, roasted, or grilled beef dishes, or with grilled fish. Use to fill artichoke bottoms or tomatoes.
