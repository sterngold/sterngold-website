Garde Manger Soup

Gazpacho

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 3

Ingredients

600 g Cucumber (Peel, split, remove seeds, cut into chunks)
400 g Bell peppers, assorted, red (Peel, split, remove seeds, cut into chunks)
1.25 kg Tomatoes (Peel, split, remove seeds, cut into chunks)
120 g Onions (Peel and cut into slices)
11 g Garlic (Peel and cut into slices)
120 g Bread crumbs, fresh, white (Soak in broth)
500 g ml Beef broth
60 g ml Vinegar, red wine
90 g Olive oil
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
1 pcs x Tabasco
150 g Bread, white (pullman loaf)
150 g Cucumber
150 g Bell peppers, assorted
150 g Tomatoes

Production

- fill bar blender with cucumbers, tomaotes, bell peppers, onions, garlic and soaked mie de pain, with cold broth or consomme
- add vinegar and oil in small amounts and puree mixture to a homogenues puree
- season well with salt and pepper and tabasco
- chill soup very well, and serve in chilled soup cups
Garnish:
- serve croutons and diced vegetables on the side in small dishes
- cut bread into small dice and toast croutons in oven
- peel cucumbers and tomatoes, split in half, remove seeds and cut into brunoise
- split bell peppers, remove seeds, wash and cut into brunoise
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
