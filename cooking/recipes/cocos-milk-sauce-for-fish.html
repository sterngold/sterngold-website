Coconut Milk Sauce for Fish

Combine 1 l coconut milk with 1 l fish stock and 1 l cream. Add lemon leaf and lemongrass. Reduce by half. Strain. Finish with a splash of Noilly Prat and green curry paste to taste. Season with lime juice and salt.
