Production

Vegetarian Cannelloni
Vegetarische Cannelloni Canneloni aux légumes


Rezept Nr. : 639
Kategorie : Pasta Dishes
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 09.08.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 0.83 / EUR 0.52

EUROPEAN CULINARY CENTER
Zutaten für 10 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)
500 g *Ravioli Dough
300 g Vegetarian product
100 g Vegetable brunoise mix 50 ml Olive oil
45 g Shallots
20 g Garlic
100 ml *Tomato Concasse
100 g Vegetable stock
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper)
10 g Basil, fresh Chopped
10 g Herbs, fresh (chervil,chives and Chopped
parsley)
100 g Ricotta cheese or curd cheese.
50 g Butter 100 g Egg yolk
Sauce:
300 ml *Mornay Sauce 200 ml *Tomato Concassé 75 g Sbrinz Grated
50 g Butter Melted
For Filling
- Peel and chop shallots and garlic.
- Wash kitchen herbs and chop leaves.
- SAUTÉ VEGETARIAN PRODCUT IN OLIVE OIL UNTIL LIGHT BROWN,
ADD SHALLOTS,GARLIC AND VEGETABLE BRUNOISE
- ADD TOMATO CONCASSE AND VEGETABLE STOCK BRING TO A BOIL SIMMER OVER LOW HEAT FOR 5 MINUTES
- CHILL MIXTURE
- ADD HERBS AND SEASON TO TASTE WITH SALT AND PEPPER.
- ADD EGG YOLKS, BUTTER AND RICOTTA AND CHILL FILLING AGAIN THOROUGHLY.
- ROLL OUT NODDLE DOUGH AND MAKE LASAGNE SHEET (SEE COURSE INSTRUCTION) - BLANCH QUICKLY AND REFRESH
- DRY WITH PAPER TOWEL AND FILL, ROLL UP AND PLACE ON
- BUTTER AN OVENPROOF DISH,GASTRO NORM (GRATIN DISH) WITH SOME OF THE BUTTER. - PLACE ROLLS INTO DISH
- TOSS WITH MORNAY SAUCE AND TOMATOES CONCASSÉ.
- SPRINKLE WITH CHEESE AND MELTED BUTTER.
BAKE IN HOT OVEN 200° C UNTIL HEATED THROUGH AND BROWN ON TOP.
