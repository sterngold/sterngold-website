100 g Vegetable brunoise mix 50 ml Olive oil

20 g Garlic

parsley)

300 ml *Mornay Sauce 200 ml *Tomato Concassé 75 g Sbrinz Grated

For Filling
- Peel and chop shallots and garlic.
- Wash kitchen herbs and chop leaves.
- SAUTÉ VEGETARIAN PRODCUT IN OLIVE OIL UNTIL LIGHT BROWN,
ADD SHALLOTS,GARLIC AND VEGETABLE BRUNOISE
- ADD TOMATO CONCASSE AND VEGETABLE STOCK BRING TO A BOIL SIMMER OVER LOW HEAT FOR 5 MINUTES
- CHILL MIXTURE
- ADD HERBS AND SEASON TO TASTE WITH SALT AND PEPPER.
- ADD EGG YOLKS, BUTTER AND RICOTTA AND CHILL FILLING AGAIN THOROUGHLY.
- ROLL OUT NODDLE DOUGH AND MAKE LASAGNE SHEET (SEE COURSE INSTRUCTION) - BLANCH QUICKLY AND REFRESH
- DRY WITH PAPER TOWEL AND FILL, ROLL UP AND PLACE ON
- BUTTER AN OVENPROOF DISH,GASTRO NORM (GRATIN DISH) WITH SOME OF THE BUTTER. - PLACE ROLLS INTO DISH
- TOSS WITH MORNAY SAUCE AND TOMATOES CONCASSÉ.
- SPRINKLE WITH CHEESE AND MELTED BUTTER.
BAKE IN HOT OVEN 200° C UNTIL HEATED THROUGH AND BROWN ON TOP.
VEGETARIAN CANNELLONI (Canneloni aux légumes) | Chef Patrick Diethelm | DCT, Pasta Station | August 2005 | Yield: 10 servings

Ingredients:
500 g ravioli dough
300 g vegetarian product
100 g vegetable brunoise mix
50 ml olive oil
45 g shallots, peeled and chopped
20 g garlic, chopped
100 ml tomato concassé
100 g vegetable stock
100 g ricotta or curd cheese
50 g butter
100 g egg yolk
10 g fresh basil, chopped
10 g fresh herbs (chervil, chives, parsley), chopped
Salt and pepper to taste

Sauce:
300 ml Mornay sauce
200 ml tomato concassé
75 g Sbrinz, grated
50 g butter, melted

Method:
1. Prepare filling: Sauté vegetarian product in olive oil until light brown. Add shallots, garlic, and vegetable brunoise.
2. Add tomato concassé and vegetable stock, bring to boil, simmer over low heat 5 minutes. Chill mixture.
3. Add herbs and season with salt and pepper. Fold in egg yolks, butter, and ricotta. Chill thoroughly.
4. Roll out dough into lasagne sheets, blanch quickly, refresh, and dry with paper towel.
5. Fill, roll up, and place on buttered ovenproof dish.
6. Toss with Mornay sauce and tomato concassé. Sprinkle with cheese and melted butter.
7. Bake at 200°C until heated through and browned on top.
