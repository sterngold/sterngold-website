Brown veal stock

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 3

Ingredients

1.7999999523162842 kg Veal bones (Chop as small as possible)
600 g Calves feet (Chop)
300 g Mirepoix for Brown stock
30 g Tomato paste (or tommato trimmings)
300 g ml Wine, white, dry
4.5 kg liter Water
1 pcs piece Sachet bag (Consisting of peppercorns, cloves, thyme, bay leaves and rosemary)
15 g Salt

Production

- roast veal bones and calves feet slowly. Use rotissoire , convection oven or regular oven at 200 C
- add mirepoix and continue roasting
- pour off excess fat
- add tomato paste and continue roasting at 180 C
- add a small amount of water two or three times and reduce each time until almost evaporated
- transfer bones and pan drippings to a suitable marmite. Add wine and water
- bring to boil, add slat and skim
- simmer for 3 to 4 hours
- after 2 hours add sachet bag
- strain first through china cap or hair sieve and than strain again through cheese cloth
- reduce down to 1/3
Roast veal bones and calf's feet slowly in rotisserie, convection oven, or regular oven at 200°C. Add mirepoix and continue roasting. Pour off excess fat. Add tomato paste and continue roasting at 180°C. Add small amounts of water two or three times, reducing each time until almost evaporated. Transfer bones and pan drippings to a suitable marmite. Add wine and water, bring to boil, add salt, and skim. Simmer for 3 to 4 hours. Add sachet bag after 2 hours. Strain first through china cap or hair sieve, then strain again through cheesecloth. Reduce down to one-third.
