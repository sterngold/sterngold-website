**Ham Mousse**

Ingredients (10 servings):
- 180 g ham
- 180 ml heavy cream (36%)
- 90 ml chicken stock
- 10 g gelatin
- Salt and pepper to taste

Method:
Cut ham into fine salpicon.

Bring cream to a boil with chicken stock. Remove from heat and add gelatin, stirring to dissolve.

Add ham and blend in bar blender until smooth.

Season with salt and pepper mill. Strain through fine sieve.

Cool completely, then fill into prepared terrine mold or bowl. Refrigerate until set firm.

See course instruction for service ideas.
