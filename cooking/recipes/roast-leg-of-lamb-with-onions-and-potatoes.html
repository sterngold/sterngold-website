Roast Leg of Lamb with Onions and Potatoes

GARNISH
Peel potatoes and cut into 5 mm slices. Blanch. Peel onions and slice (émincer). Sauté potatoes in peanut oil, then add onions. Season with salt, nutmeg, and white pepper. Add butter and sauté potatoes and onions together until done.

LAMB
Season leg of lamb with seasoning salt. Roast with peanut oil in hot oven at 220°C, turning to brown on all sides. Lower heat to 180°C and roast, basting occasionally with drippings, until desired doneness (54°C internal temperature, checked with meat thermometer). Remove from pan and keep warm on wire rack.

SAUCE
Drain fat from pan. Add vegetables and sauté for a few minutes. Add wine and reduce. Add brown veal stock, bring to boil, and boil for 10 minutes. Strain sauce through china cap and skim off remaining fat.

PRESENTATION
Slice meat and serve on top of potato and onion garnish. Serve sauce on the side.
