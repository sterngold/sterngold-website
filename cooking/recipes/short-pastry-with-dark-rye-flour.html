SHORT PASTRY WITH DARK RYE FLOUR
Chef Patrick Diethelm | Recipe #710 | Cost: CHF 3.70 / EUR 2.34
Yield: 400 g

INGREDIENTS
150 g White wheat flour
100 g Dark rye flour
100 g Butter (cold)
90 ml Water
5 g Salt

METHOD
Cut cold butter into small nuggets and add to sifted flour. Rub together until mixture resembles fine granules. Add salted water and work quickly into a smooth dough — do not overwork or knead excessively, as this develops gluten and toughens the dough. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before use.
