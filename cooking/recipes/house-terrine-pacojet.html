House Terrine Pacojet (Continuation)
Hausteггine PACOJET Teггine maison
Recipe # : 229
Category : ECA - Paco jet
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date :17.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.74 / EUR 0.47
- PACOTISE:
PACOTISE THE FROZEN FARCE TWICE, REMOVE AND PLACE INTO BOWL ON ICE
MIX THE FARCE WITH THE CHOPPED PORK NECK,CUT MORELS, DICED HAM WITH COGNAC AND THE PISTACCIOS
- FILL THE TERRINE INTO PREPARED TERRINE MOLD (NO AIR BUBBLES) - COVER WITH REMAINING FATBACK
POACH TERRINE IN STEAMER AT 75°C UNTIL CENTER TEMPERATURES 60°C - REMOVE AND COOL DOWN
- DURING COOLING, PRESS THE TERRINE WITH ABOUT 1 KG WEIGHT ON TOP
THIS RECIPE YIELDS ABOUT 30 SLICES OF 50G EACH
DAY 1: Cut fatback into thin slices and line terrine mold, reserving some slices for the top layer.

Sweat chopped shallots and garlic in butter. Dice pork neck (first portion), veal shoulder, smoked bacon, and chicken livers. Pass through meat grinder with 3mm blade. Add seasoning, cream, and press into Pacojet beaker, pressing down well. Freeze 24 hours at -20°C.

Soak morels, wash, and dice into 5mm pieces. Dice ham and marinate with cognac. Line terrine mold with thin fatback slices.

DAY 2: Cut trimmed pork neck (second portion) into dice and chop once with two-blade cutter.

Pacotise the frozen farce twice. Remove to a bowl on ice. Mix farce with chopped pork neck, diced morels, diced ham with cognac, and pistachios. Fill prepared terrine mold without air bubbles. Cover with remaining fatback slices.

Poach terrine in steamer at 75°C until center reaches 60°C. Remove and cool while pressing with approximately 1kg weight on top.

Yields approximately 30 slices of 50g each.
