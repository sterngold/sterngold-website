STUFFED VEAL ROLL ON ON BASIL SAUCE WITH GLAZED VEGETABLES

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

STUFFED VEAL ROLL ON ON BASIL SAUCE WITH GLAZED VEGETABLES

Yield: 5 servings
5 veal escalope, 100g each
salt, pepper
clarified butter
50 ml Port wine
150 ml reduced fond de veau brun

Raw mousseline farce:
150 g veal meat trimmings
20 g egg white
100 g cream
salt, pepper
5g fine chopped herbs
chervil, basil, thyme
60 g brunoise (blanched)
carrot, leek green

VE#GETABLES
400 g seasonal vegetables

Sauce:
2 dl Fond de veau blanc
little white roux
1 dl cream
30 g butter
60 g bacon brunoise crispy
10 g chopped chervil
lemon juice

Preparation:
- Cut veal into thin escalope
- Flatten between plastic foil
- Prepare the farce in food processor and place on ice
- Add the herbs, blanched brunoise and season to taste
- Spread the farce on the escalope and roll up tightly
- Wrap the roll into pork caul or tie with kitchen twine (see instruction)
- Prepare all the vegetables and glaze each with vegetable stock
- Reduce the fond de veau blanc, add the cream and reduce to desired consistency add the chervil a la minute.
- Sauté the veal roll on medium heat in clarified butter in the oven, basting frequently 220 200 C
- Place on wire rack and let rest at 150°C. for another 10-15 minutes
- Arrange the vegetable with the starch dish
- Carve the veal roll in a angle to show and present the cutaway
- Arrange the veal rolls and brush a la minute with the reduced Port wine and brown veal stock glaze
