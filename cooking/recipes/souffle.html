SPINACH SOUFFLÉ
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Station: Pâtisserie | Date: 25.10.2005 | Recipe #299

Ingredients (5 servings):
50g butter
50g white wheat flour
200ml milk
Salt and pepper to taste
90g egg yolk paste
0.5 clove garlic, chopped
125g frozen chopped spinach
150g egg whites
5g cornstarch
10g butter (for molds)
10g white wheat flour (for molds)

Method:
- Melt butter in sauteuse and add flour.
- Add hot milk and whisk well. When mixture begins to thicken, switch to wooden paddle.
- Cool slightly.
- Blend in egg yolks one by one. Add garlic and spinach.
- Season with salt and pepper.
- Whip egg whites and fold in cornstarch.
- Fold about 25% of beaten egg whites into the paste.
- Gradually fold in remaining egg whites.
- Fill prepared soufflé molds three-quarters full.
- Place in water bath and finish at 200°C.

Baking times: Large molds 20–25 minutes; individual molds 10–12 minutes.
- Serve on paper doily immediately. Pairs well with Madeira sauce.

Note: This method works for other vegetable purée soufflés using the same proportions.
