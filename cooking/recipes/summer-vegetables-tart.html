Production

Summer Vegetables Tart
Sommerliche 0emüsewähe Tarte au légumes

Zutaten für 5 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)
400 g *Short Pastry [sugarless] Line I tart molds with dough, poke some holes in dough.
25 g 300 g

100 ml 200 ml 120 g 100 g
10 g 40 g
1 x
1 x
Filling:
Butter
Vegetable mixture Custard:
Milk
Heavy cream (36%) Egg whole
Parmesan grated Basil, fresh
White wheat flour Nutmeg (pinch)
Seasoning (salt and pepper)
Leek, Carrot, Zucchini, Celery cut into julienne
Chopped

- SWEAT VEGETABLE JULIENNE IN BUTTER UNTIL CRUNCHY (NO COLOR) - LET VEGETABLES COOL AND PLACE EVENLY IN MOLDS.
- COMBINE CUSTARD INGREDIENTS WITHOUT BASIL AND STIR
- STRAIN THROUGH FINE CHINA CAP.
- ADD CHOPPED BASIL
- FILL MOLDS WITH CUSTARD.
- BAKE IN THE OVEN AT 200°C FOR APPROX.30 MINUTES.
