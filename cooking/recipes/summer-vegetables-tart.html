SUMMER VEGETABLES TART

Yield: 5 servings

Ingredients:
400 g short pastry (sugarless)
25 g butter
300 g vegetable mixture (leek, carrot, zucchini, celery cut into julienne)

Custard:
100 ml milk
200 ml heavy cream (36%)
120 g egg whole
100 g Parmesan, grated
10 g basil, fresh chopped
40 g white wheat flour
Nutmeg (pinch)
Salt and pepper to taste

Method:
1. Line tart molds with short pastry and poke holes in dough.
2. Sweat vegetable julienne in butter until tender but still crisp, with no color. Allow to cool completely.
3. Distribute cooled vegetables evenly in lined molds.
4. Combine all custard ingredients except basil. Stir until smooth.
5. Strain through fine sieve.
6. Add chopped basil to custard.
7. Fill molds with custard mixture.
8. Bake at 200°C for approximately 30 minutes until set.
