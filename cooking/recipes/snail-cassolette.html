Snail Cassolette

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

1T7NAU-LUCERNE SWITZERLAND
BAKED FRENCH SNAIL CASSOLETTE
10 g fine chopped shallot
1 clove of garlic, fine chopped
40 g vegetable brunoise (leek, carrot, knob celery)
20 g fennel brunoise
20 g butter
25 snails
20 g black truffle brunoise
20 g pernod
250 g white veal stock
100g cream
pinch of saffron
chopped fennel green salt and pepper mill white truffle oil
200 g puff pastry
10 g egg yolk
Preparation:
- Sweat the shallots, garlic and vegetable brunoise in butter
- Add the snails and the truffle, pour in the pernod
- Reduce in separate pan the and white veal stock by one third
- Add the cream and reduce to the desired consistency or add little roux.
- Stir in the snail mushroom mixture and season to taste
- Add the chopped fennel green and fill up the four prepared casseroles or cocottes - Roll out the puff pastry about 3 mm thick
- Stamp out rounds about 2 cm larger in diameter than the serving dish
- Brush the edge of the puff pastry with the eggyolk and use to cover the small cocottes, pressing the egg washed dough firmly to the sides of the dish
- Garnish with some puff pastry trimmings on the top
- Allow to rest for 20 min. in the refrigerator
- Bake at 220° C for about 10 -15 min. until the puff pastry is golden brown.
