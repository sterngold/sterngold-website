Warm Potato Espuma with Herbs
Warmer Kartoffel Espuma mit Kräutern
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Trendy Espuma ' s
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 12.12.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.21 / EUR 0.13

European Gourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 6 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
280 g Bintje potatoes
80 g Milk past.
40 ml Sour cream 35%
40 g Vegetable stock
30 g Butter
8 g Herbs, fresh (chervil,chives and parsley]
- STEAM THE POTATOES UNTIL WELL DONE COOKED - STRAIN POTATOES TWICE THORUGH A FOOD MILL
. BLEND THE VEGETABLE STOCK WITH MILK, CREAM AND BUTTER WITH ALL THE CHOPPED HERBS UNTIL GREEN AND STRAIN THROUGH A CHINA CAP.
- MIX ALL THE INGREDIENTS INTO POTATO AND STIR.
- FILL INTO ISI PROFI WHIP AND KEEP WARM AT MAX 70°C. IN BAIN MARIE.
