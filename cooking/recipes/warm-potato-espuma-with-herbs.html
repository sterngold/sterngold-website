WARM POTATO ESPUMA WITH HERBS
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | DCT Entremétier Station | 12.12.2005
Yield: 6 servings | Cost: CHF 0.21

INGREDIENTS
280 g Bintje potatoes
80 g milk paste
40 ml sour cream (35%)
40 g vegetable stock
30 g butter
8 g fresh herbs (chervil, chives, parsley)

METHOD
1. Steam potatoes until fully cooked.
2. Pass potatoes twice through a food mill.
3. Blend vegetable stock, milk, cream, butter, and all chopped herbs until green. Strain through china cap.
4. Mix herb mixture into strained potatoes and stir to combine.
5. Fill into ISI Profi whip and hold warm at maximum 70°C in bain-marie.
