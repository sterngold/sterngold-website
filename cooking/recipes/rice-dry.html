Entremétier Side dish

Rice dry

Riz creole

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen Servings: 10

Ingredients

500 g Rice, siam-patna
3.5 kg liter Water
40 g Salt
50 g Butter

Production

- water with salt boil
- add rice and boil vigarously for 10 to 12 min
- remove water, cool down in cold water, remove and dry well
- add to buttered
- palce butter cubes on top
- place buttered paper
- warm in oven, or steamer, with fork mix
From Tschuggen · Chef Ulf Wein
