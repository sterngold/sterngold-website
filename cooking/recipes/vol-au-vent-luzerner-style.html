Vol au vent house Luzern Style
Luzerner Chugellpastete Vol-au-vent lucornoise
Recipe # : 421
Category : ECA - Saucier meat
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date :20.1 0.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 1.07 / EUR 0.68

Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
400 g *German Puff Pastry
0.6 PC Egg yolks
150 g Veal shoulder, trimmed, boneless
150 g Pork butt trimmed
60 m1 Apple wine (cider)
450 ml *White Veal Stock
0.5 PC Onion Studded with 1 clove
150 g Veal sausage stuffing
18 g Butter
21 g White wheat flour
100 g Mushroom, fresh
77 g Apples for cooking
40 g White raisins
Б g Butter
200 m1 Heavy cream (36%)
1х Seasoning (salt)

- Make а puff pastry house or large patty shell.
- Combine apple wine and veal stock,
- Add veal, pork, and onion studded with cloves.
Bring to а boil and boil until veal and park are tender. (1 hour)
Remove meat from stack, cut into salpicon, and set aside.
- Strain stock and reserve.
- pipe veal sausage stuffing into poaching liquid.
- Peel and core apples and cut into jardiniere.
-Trim and wash mushrooms and cut jardiniere size.

- POACH SAUSAGE MEATBALLS iN RESERVED STOCK.
RESERVE STOCK AGAIN FOR SAUCE.
MAKE SAUCE BY MELTING BUTTER AND ADDING FLOUR TO MAKE А WHITE ROUX.
LET COOL SLIGHTLY. HEAT SOME OF RESERVED STOCK.
ADD HOT STOCK TO COOL ROUX, STIR TO DISSOLVE ROUX, BRING TO А BOIL, STIRRING CONSTANTLY, AND SIMMER FOR 30 MINUTES. REMOVE FROM HEAT.
- SAUTÉ MUSHROOMS, APPLES, AND SULTANAS IN BUTTER.
-WHEN SAUCE 1S COOKED, ADD VEAL AND PORK, MEATBALLS, MUSHROOMS, APPLES, AND SULTANAS. BRING TO А BOIL AND STIR 1N HEAVY CREAM. SEASON TO TASTE.
- FILL PASTRY HOUSE AT THE MOMENT OF SERVICE.
