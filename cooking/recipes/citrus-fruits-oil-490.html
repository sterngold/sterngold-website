3 x Estragon Zweig

Citrus Fruits Oil

Ingredients:
5 dl warm-pressed olive oil
5 orange zests
6 lime zests
3 star anise
4 cloves
3 bay leaves
3 coriander seeds
3 tarragon sprigs
3 g fresh ginger

Method:
Warm the olive oil gently. Add all spices and aromatics: crushed star anise, cloves, bay leaves, and coriander seeds. Add tarragon sprigs and thinly sliced fresh ginger. Infuse over low heat for 20–30 minutes without boiling. Add the citrus zests in the final minutes of infusion. Cool completely, then strain through fine sieve. Bottle and store in a cool, dark place.
From Tschuggen · Chef Ulf Wein
