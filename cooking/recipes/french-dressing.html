French Dressing

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

French Dressing PO
Franzosische Salatsauce
Rezept Nr.
Kategorie : ECA - Sauces Garde-manger
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 16.02.2004
Warenkosten: CHF 3.18/ EUR 2.01

Zutaten fUr 3.000 Liter zu 100'1. (Nettomenge)
#1
15 g Salt
1 g Pepper ground
375 g Egg past.
30 g Dijon mustard
1 g Beef broth powder
8 g Garlic

#2
1.75 I Sunflower oil
500 ml Herb vinegar
175 ml Water

# 1 ADD ALL THE INGREDIENTS INTO MIXING BOWl AND STIR UNTIL CREAMY
# 2 ADD ALL THE INGREDIENTS INTO BAR BLENDER AND MIX WITH 'I#.1 UNTIL CREAMY WHITE.
SEASON TO TASTE
STRAIN THROUGH CHINA CAP AND FILL INTO BOITLES.
Label with Date and Name (expire date + 10 Days)
