POTATO PARSLEY CREAM
Yield: 6 servings | Chef: Patrick Diethelm

Ingredients:
350 g Bintje potatoes, steamed
50 g frozen green peas
120 ml heavy cream (36%)
50 g fresh parsley
50 g Italian parsley
6 g salt
Pepper to taste

Method:
Steam potatoes. Blanch frozen peas in salted water and refresh immediately. Heat cream gently and add both parsleys. Combine all ingredients with the heated cream. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to Pacojet beaker and freeze at -20°C for 24 hours. Pacotise and heat à la minute in sauteuse before service.
