Garde Manger Salad

Potato salad with cucumbers

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

1.7999999523162842 kg Potatoes Waxy
667 g Cucumbers
20 g Salt
# Dressing
180 g Onions
400 g ml Beef broth
200 g ml Oil, sunflower
100 g ml Vinegar, herbs
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
# Garnish
20 g Chives, fresh (Cut)

Production

- wash and boil potato in slat water. (can be steamed)
- peel potatoes, while still warm and cut into 4 mm slices
- peel and chop onions

- HEAT BOUILLON AND POOR OVER POTATOES
- ADD CHOPPED ONIONS AND SLICED CUCUMBERS
- STIR TOGETHER OIL, VINEGAR, SALT AND PEPPER
- ADD DRESSING TO POTATOES AND BLEND CAREFULLY
- ALLOW DRESSING TO PENATRATE THE POTATOES
- SERVE SPRINKLED WITH CHIVES

Notes

-do not serve potatoes salad directly form refrigerator - the amount of dressing needed varies depending on the type of potatoes used - potato salad should be made fresh daily - potato salad can also be made with mayonnaise, yogurt or light/low-fat sour cream

From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
