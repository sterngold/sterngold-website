Pecanos tart

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

Deeg x 2
370 bloem
salt
180 butter
5 spoons fet
cold watter - 8 spoon

Fulling
6 eggs
4 spoons botter - melt
4 spoons flour
1 tee spoon vanille
salt
250 sugar
8 dl maisstroop - honing - marple syroop
370 gehaakt nooten
120 whoole nooten

200 C for 10 min - 40 - 160 C
PECANS TART | VandeMarkt Pâtisserie

Dough (x2):
Mix 370 g flour with salt. Cut in 180 g butter and 5 spoons fat. Bind with 8 spoons cold water.

Filling:
Melt 4 spoons butter. Whisk together 6 eggs, melted butter, 4 spoons flour, 1 teaspoon vanilla, salt, and 250 g sugar.

Add 800 ml (8 dl) of a mixture of corn syrup, honey, and maple syrup. Fold in 370 g chopped pecans and 120 g whole pecans.

Bake at 200°C for 10 minutes, then 40 minutes at 160°C.
