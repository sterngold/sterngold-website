Ricotta spinach Tortellini with Pesto
Torte/lini mit Pesto Tortellini al pesto

Recipe # : 644
Category : Pasta Dishes
Chef : P.Diethelm
Date : 03.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.36 / EUR 0.23

Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
500 g *Ravioli Dough
180 g Spinach, fresh or frozen 200 g Ricotta cheese
50 g Parmesan cheese grated 50 g Egg yolk
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper)
1 x Nutmeg [pinch)
For Filling
- Blanch spinach, drain in cold water, and drain again. Squeeze out most moisture and purée in food processor.
- Purée or strain ricotta cheese to obtain a smooth paste.
- Blend all filling ingredients.
- ROLL OUT RAVIOLI DOUGH ABOUT 1 MM THICK
- CUT SQUARES OR CIRCLES WITH CUTTER, ABOUT 30 TO 40 mm IN DIAMETTER.
- PUT SMALL AMOUNT OF FILLING IN CENTERS, USING A PASTRY BAG.
- MOISTEN THE CIRCLE EDGES WITH WATER AND FOLD OVER DOUGH TO MAKE HALF-MOONS. BE SURE EDGES ARE WELL SEALED.
- PULL TOGETHER THE POINTED COMERS OF THE HALF-MOONS AND PRESS THE TWO COMERS TOGETHER, OVERLAPPING THEM SLIGHTLY; PRESS TO SEAL THE RESULT SHOULD BE A CLOSED CIRCLE, WITH FILLING ON ONE SIDE.
- PUT TORTELLINI ON A FLOURED BOARD UNTIL USE.
- SIMMER TORTELLINI FOR 2-3 MINUTES IN SALTED WATER, DRAIN AND SAVE SMALL AMOUNT OF BOILING WATER.
- TUSS PASTA IN OLIVE OIL OR LITTLE PESTO
- SERVE PESTO WITH TORTELLINI A PART.
