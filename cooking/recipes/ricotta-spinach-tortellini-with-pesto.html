**Ricotta Spinach Tortellini with Pesto** | Recipe 644 | Chef P. Diethelm

**Ingredients (10 servings):**
- 500 g ravioli dough
- 180 g fresh or frozen spinach
- 200 g ricotta cheese
- 50 g parmesan cheese, grated
- 50 g egg yolk
- Salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste

**Method:**
1. Blanch spinach, cool in cold water, and drain thoroughly. Squeeze out excess moisture and purée in food processor.
2. Purée or strain ricotta to obtain a smooth paste.
3. Blend spinach, ricotta, parmesan, egg yolk, and seasonings together.
4. Roll out ravioli dough to 1 mm thickness.
5. Cut squares or circles 30–40 mm in diameter using a cutter.
6. Place small amount of filling in center using a pastry bag.
7. Moisten circle edges with water and fold over dough to form half-moons, ensuring edges are well sealed.
8. Pull together the pointed corners of the half-moons and press them together, slightly overlapping to seal. Result should be a closed circle with filling on one side.
9. Place tortellini on a floured board until service.
10. Simmer tortellini in salted water for 2–3 minutes, drain, and reserve a small amount of cooking water.
11. Toss pasta in olive oil or a small amount of pesto.
12. Serve pesto on the side.
