Beurre Blanc (for fish)
Bt!urre-blanc-5auce
Beurre blanc {1ere methode}

Recipe 1# :35

Category : Sauces

Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 12.12.2005

Cost of goods : CHF 11.55 LEUR 7.31

Ingredients for 0.150 liters at 100% [Net quantity)

34g Shallots
38 ml Dry white wine
23 ml White wine vinegar
150 ml *FishFumet
100 g Butter Cold
1 x Seasoning [salt and pepper]

- PEEL SHALLOTS AND CHOP FINE
- COMBINE SHALLOTS WITH DRY WHITE WINE AND REDUCE
- ADD WHITE WINE VINEGAR AND REDUCE AGAIN
-ADD FISH FUMET AND REDUCE TO ABOUT 1/.2
- SEASON WITH SALT AND FRESHLY GROUND WHITE PEPPER --
- REMOVE FROM HEAT AND ADD THE COLD BUTTER IN SMALL AMOUNTS, WHIPPING VIGOROUSLY

- WHIP SAUCE ONCE MORE WITH A HAND HELD ELECTRIC MIXER [IF AVAILABLE).

- BE SURE SAUCE DOES NOT RETURN TO A BOil

APPLICATIONS
Serve with grilled ocean fish and crustaceans. Serve with warm fish terrines.
Serve with poached fish.
