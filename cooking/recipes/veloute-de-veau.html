Veloute de veau

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 0

Ingredients

25 g Butter
30 g Flour, white wheat
600 g ml Veal stock, white
1 pcs x Salt

Production

- make a light yellow roux with butter and flour. Cool the roux
- bring veal stock to a boil, add roux and mix well
- bring to a boil, stirring continously
- simmer for about 30 minutes
- season to taste
- strain through fien china cap

Notes

- use in creamed veal veloute sauce - instant roux powder can be used instead

From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
