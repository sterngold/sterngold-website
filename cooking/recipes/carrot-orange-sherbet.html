- DISSOLVE THE GELATIN WITH 50 ML ORANGE JUICE AND ADD TO WARM CARROT. 4 - ADD ALL THE REMAINING INGREDIENTS INTO BOWL WITH CARROTS
- FREEZE AT -24°C FOR AT LEAST 12 HOURS.
- PACOTISE TWICE AND SERVE WITH SWEET AND SOUR CARROT CHIPS AND ORANGE SALAD
- serviert mit Mohnmeriguesstangen und Orangenrahm aus Pacojet
