Entremétier Soup

Minestrone soup

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 3

Ingredients

60 g Bacon, smoked (Cut into brunoise)
60 g ml Olive oil
108 g Onions (chop)
228 g Leeks (white leaves only. Paysanne)
153 g Savoy cabbage (paysanne)
84 g Knob Celery (celeriac) (paysanne)
144 g Carrot (paysanne)
48 g Tomato paste
3.0 kg liter Beef broth
180 g Potatoes (Cut into fine sliver (paysanne))
60 g Spaghetti (break into small pieces by wrapping in a towel and moving back and forth over the rim of a table)
60 g Beans, borlotti (Soak beans in cold water, drain and boil in water until soft)
114 g Tomatoes (Peel half, remove seeds, cut into small concasse)
75 g ml Olive oil
11 g Garlic
6 g Parsley, fresh
6 g Basil, fresh
1 pcs x Salt
96 g Parmesan, grated

Production

- sautee onions and bacon in olive oil
- add leeks and cabbage, then remaining knob celery and carrots and continue cooking
- add tomato paste and bouillon
- bring to boil, skim and simmer until vegetables are almost tender
- add potaotes and spaghetti; boil for 10 min longer
- add cooked beans, tomaotes, pesto. Bring to boil
- season to taste
- serve with grated cheese on the side

Notes

Make pesto
