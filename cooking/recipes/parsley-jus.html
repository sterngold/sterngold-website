European Gourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
15 ml Olive oil
50 g Parsley, fresh
50 ml *Chicken Stock
50 g Vegetable stock
10 g Cornstarch
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper) 25 ml Olive oil
SWEAT THE CHOPPED PARSLEY WITH OLIVE OIL
- ADD CHICKEN AND VEGETABLE STOCK
- ADD CORNSTARCH
- BRING TO A BOIL ADD PARSLEY AND BLEND WITH BAR MIXER - SEASON TO TASTE WITH SALT AND PEPPER MILL.
STRAIN THROUGH CHINA CAP AND COOL DOWN ON ICE WATER RING TO A BOIL (A LA MINUTE)
- STIR IN OLIVE OIL AND SERVE.
- ADD EXTRA STOCK WHEN TO THICK JUS
PARSLEY JUS (DCT | Garde Manger)
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 10.11.2005
Yield: 10 servings

Sweat chopped parsley in 15 ml olive oil. Add chicken stock and vegetable stock, bring to a boil. Mix cornstarch with a little stock to create slurry and add to pot. Return to boil while stirring. Blend thoroughly with immersion blender. Strain through china cap.

Chill over ice water bath. To serve à la minute: reheat to boil, adjust consistency with additional stock if too thick, stir in 25 ml olive oil, and season to taste with salt and pepper mill.
