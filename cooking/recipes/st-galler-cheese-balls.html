ST. GALLER CHEESE BALLS

Yield: 8–10 servings
Chef: Patrick Diethelm, DCT European Culinary Center

INGREDIENTS
150g Milk
40g Butter
6g Salt
110g White wheat flour
10g Eggs, pasteurized
400g Tilsiter cheese, red (cut into 2–3mm dice or grated)
5g White wheat flour (for shaping)
Pepper mill
Nutmeg

METHOD
1. Bring milk, butter, and salt to a boil.
2. Add all flour at once and stir until the mixture is firm and dry (choux paste).
3. Remove from heat and fold in eggs and diced cheese.
4. Season to taste with pepper mill and nutmeg. Chill.
5. Portion into 20–30g balls and shape with the aid of a light dusting of flour.
6. Chill and allow to rest.
7. Deep fry à la minute at 170°C until golden brown.
8. Remove and arrange on prepared tomato concasse with basil chiffonade, or serve as fingerfood.
