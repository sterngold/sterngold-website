Red Fruits Mix / Marinate

Chef: Jan Wasch Source: Vijf

Ingredients

1 pcs bag Red fruits
1 pcs x Vanille, stick
1 pcs x Caneel
500 g Sugar
2 pcs x Oranges (ZEST TOO)
1 pcs x Lemons (ZEST TOO)
1 pcs x Crème cassis
1 pcs x Rum

Production

1 Плодове замразени
2 Канела пръчка
3 Ванила – млако резънче – оклол 5 см – вадят се семената плюс обвивката
4 Портокал плюс обвивката
5 Лимно плюс обвивката
6 Ром
7 Creme de Kasisi – няколко капки
8 Покрива се със захар
Red Fruits Mix / Marinate
Source: Vijf | Pâtisserie

Ingredients:
- Frozen red fruits
- Cinnamon stick
- Vanilla pod (approx. 5 cm), split and seeds scraped
- Orange with zest
- Lemon with zest
- Rum
- Crème de cassis (a few drops)
- Sugar

Method:
Combine frozen fruits with cinnamon stick, vanilla pod with seeds and pod, orange and lemon (both with zest), rum, and crème de cassis. Cover with sugar and marinate until fruits thaw and flavors infuse.
From Vijf · Chef Jan Wasch · 25.03.2006
