Fish Veloute Sauce

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Fish Veloute Sauce
Fisch-Ve/oute Ve/oute de poisson

Rezept Nr. :53

Kategorie : Sauces

Koch : P.oiethelm

Datum : 11.08.2005

Warenkosten: CHF 2.33 LEUR 1.47

Zutaten fOr 1.000 Liter zu 100% (NettomengeJ
50 g Butter
60 9 White wheat flour
1.2 / *Fish Stock
1 x Seasoning (salt, ceyenn~ pepper)
- ADO FLOUR INTO MELTED BUTTER TO MAKE A ROUX.
-COOL ROUX.
- BRING FISH STOCK TO A BOIL AND ADO ROUX, WHILE STIRRING.
- BRING TO A BOIL.
SIMMER FOR ABOUT 30 MINUTES.
SEASON TO TASTE.
STRAIN THROUGH FINE CHINA CAP.
APPLICATIONS
Use as an ingredient in white wine sauce.
