**Fish Velouté Sauce** | Chef P. Diethelm | August 11, 2005 | Cost: CHF 2.33 | Yield: 1,000 ml

**Mise en place:**
50 g butter, 60 g white wheat flour, 1.2 l fish stock, salt and cayenne pepper.

**Method:**
Melt butter and whisk in flour to create a roux. Cool the roux slightly.

Bring fish stock to a boil. Add the cooled roux while stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Return to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.

Simmer for approximately 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season to taste with salt and cayenne pepper.

Strain through a fine china cap.

**Application:**
Use as an ingredient in white wine sauce or other fish-based preparations.
