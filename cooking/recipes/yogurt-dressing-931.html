Production

Yogurt Dressing
Joghurtsalatsauce
Sauce a salade au yogourt
Rezept Nr. : 494
Kategorie : Salads
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 16.02.2004
Warenkosten: CHF 5.26/ EUR 3.33


Zutaten fOr 1.000 Liter zu 100% (Nettomenge)
750 g Yogurt, plain

60 g Herbs, fresh (dill, fennel leaves and tarragon)
10 g Salt
1 g White pepper ground
100 ml Orange juice
50 ml Lemon juice
1 x Seasoning (salt)

- WASH AND CHOP HERBS SUCH AS PARSLEY, CHIVES, AND CHERVIL.
- COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS INTO A SMOOTH DRESSING.
