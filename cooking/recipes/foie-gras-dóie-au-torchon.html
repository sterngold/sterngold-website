Garde Manger Cold Appetizer 🇫🇷 France

Foie gras dóie au torchon

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 3 Method: Poaching

Ingredients

300 g Stuffed duck liver
5 g Fleur de sel
1 g Sugar
5 g ml Madeira wine
20 g ml Port wine, white

Production

- Clean the stuffed duck liver and remove all bloody parts senews etc.
- place the liver pieces and add all the ingridients into vacuum bag
- vacuum pack
- chill and marinate for one our
- poach into combi oven with steam at 64 C for 10 min in bain marie (30 C)
- chill for short time in ice water
- open vacuum bag and
separate all the fat from the poached liver and shape into one roll
- roll into plastick foil and chill for 1 - 3 days

- serve with toasted brioche and apple confit
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
