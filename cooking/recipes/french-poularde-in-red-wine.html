FRENCH POULARDE IN RED WINE (Coq au vin rouge)
Yield: 10 servings

Ingredients:
2.4 kg Bresse poularde
Salt and pepper
50 g clarified butter
200 g mirepoix (diced vegetables)
20 g tomato paste (5/1 concentrate)
500 ml red wine
200 ml meat glaze
10 g fresh thyme

Method:
1. Cut chicken into sauté-sized pieces and season with salt and pepper.
2. Marinate chicken and mirepoix in red wine for 30 minutes.
3. Heat clarified butter in sautoir until hot.
4. Seal chicken pieces until golden brown on all sides; remove and set aside.
5. Add mirepoix to pan and sauté; stir in tomato paste and continue cooking.
6. Deglaze pan with reserved red wine marinade, scraping up fond.
7. Return chicken to pan, add thyme, bring to boil and simmer until chicken is tender.
8. Remove chicken and keep warm.
9. Reduce sauce to desired consistency; add meat glaze and blend well.
10. Strain through china cap and adjust seasoning.
11. Return chicken to sauce, bring to boil.
12. Serve in gratin dish.
