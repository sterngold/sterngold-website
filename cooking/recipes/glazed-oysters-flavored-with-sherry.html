Glazed Oysters Flavored with Sherry

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Glazed Oysters Flavored with Sherry
Time 35 ‘
Temperature 160 C
Warme Austere mit Sherry Huitres chaudes au xerés
Recipe # : 369
Category :Shellfish Dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 26.10.2005
Cost of goods CHF 5.87 / EUR 3.72
Ingredients for 4 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
12 pc Oyster, large and flat Shuck carefully. Save juice and bottom shells. Strain oyster juice. Clean and dry oyster shells and keep.
10 g Butter
18 g Shallots
30 ml Sherry wine, dry 60 ml *Fish Fumet
20 ml Heavy cream (36%) 50 ml *Hollandaise Sauce
30 ml Heavy cream (36%)

Chop.




Whip and set aside for finishing sauce.
- CAREFULLY OPEN OYSTERS
- REMOVE OYSTERS FROM WATER, RESERVE JUICE, AND KEEP OYSTERS
- SWEAT SHALLOTS IN BUTTER, ADD RESERVED OYSTER JUICE, SHERRY, FISH FUMET AND HEAVY CREAM.
- REDUCE STOCK AND STRAIN THROUGH FINE CHINA CAP. HEAT STOCK AGAIN. - FOLD IN WHIPPED CREAM AND HOLLANDAISE SAUCE.
- PLACE ONE OYSTER ON EACH WARM HALFSHELL FILLED WITH BOILED POTATO OR ARTICHOKE RAGOUT,
COVER WITH SAUCE, AND GLAZE IN HOT OVEN AT 250°C
