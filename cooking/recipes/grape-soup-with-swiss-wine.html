Entremétier Soup

Grape soup with Swiss wine

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 1

Ingredients

50 g Butter
50 g Shallots
75 g Leeks (White leaves only. Wash and trim. Cut into matignon)
250 g ml Wine, white, dry (Sylvaner or Riesling)
750 g ml Veal stock, white
200 g ml Heavy cream 36%
1 pcs x Salt
120 g Grapes, white, seedless (peel and cut in half. Reserve for garnish)
2 g Marjoram
50 g ml Sour cream 35%

Production

- sweet vegetable in butter
- add 50% wine and fond de veau blanc
- simmer for 30 min
- add cream and simmer for 5 min
- add remaining wine and season to taste
- blend with bar blender
- strain through fine china cap
- garnish with grapes
- serve with freshly smoked fish on toasted triangle crouton
- drizzle some sour cream into the soup
- sprinkle with marjoram leaves
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
