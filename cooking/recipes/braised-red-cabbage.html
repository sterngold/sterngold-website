Production

Braised Red Cabbage
Geschmortes Rotkraut Chaux rouges brais


Recipe # : 543
Category : ECA - Entremetier/ side dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 07.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.78 / EUR 0.49

European Gourmet Cuisine'
Ingredients for 4 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
600 g Red cabbage
150 g Apple
120 ml Red cooking wine
30 g Butter 80 g Onions
20 ml Raspberry vinegar
300 ml Water
1 pc Sachet bag
1 x Seasoning (salt)
60 g Red currant jelly 40 g Butter
- Trim cabbage, cut into quarters, and remove core.
Cut cabbage into thin slices or on a slicer or with a mandolin.
- Peel apples,core,and cut into thin slices.
- Combine cabbage and apples and Marinate in red wine
- Peel and slice onions into emince
- Make sachet bag with bay leaf, cloves, juniper berries and peppercorns.



- SWEAT ONIONS IN BUTTER.
- ADD MARINATED CABBAGE AND APPLES.
- ADD VINEGAR,WATER, SACHET BAG.
- COVER AND BRAISE UNTIL CABBAGE IS TENDER.
- REMOVE SACHET BAG.
- ADD RED CURRANT JELLY, BUTTER AND SEASON TO TASTE WITH SALT AND PEPPER.
