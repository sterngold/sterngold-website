**Creamed Sweetbreads in Patty Shells** | Chef Patrick Diethelm, 25.10.2005

**Sweetbreads:**
Soak sweetbreads overnight in cold water. Blanch in boiling water, chill, and drain. Poach in veal stock and white wine at a boil for 15–20 minutes. Cool in stock, drain, and reserve stock. Remove skin and break into small pieces.

**Patty shells:**
Shape 600 g puff pastry into 10 shells approximately 6 cm diameter. Brush with beaten egg and refrigerate at least 1 hour. Bake and keep warm.

**Mushroom and sweetbreads mixture:**
Trim and quarter 150 g fresh mushrooms. Chop 9 g shallots. Sauté mushrooms and shallots in 10 g butter with lemon juice. Add sweetbreads, cover, and keep warm. Drain off juices and reduce to glaze consistency.

**Sauce:**
Make a roux with 30 g butter and 30 g flour. Whisk into 1 L reserved stock, bring to boil stirring constantly, and simmer 10 minutes. Mix 100 ml heavy cream with 1 egg yolk, stir into boiling sauce without allowing to boil further. Combine sauce with sweetbreads mixture and season to taste with salt and lemon juice.

**Assembly:**
Cut lids from warm patty shells with a sharp knife. Fill shells with creamed sweetbreads, replace lids, and serve immediately. Serve any remaining filling on the side.
