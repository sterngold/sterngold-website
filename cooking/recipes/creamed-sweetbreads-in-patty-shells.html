Creamed Sweetbreads in Patty Shells
Kalbsmilkenpastetchen Bouchees au as de veau


Recipe # : 293
Category : Hot Appetizers
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 25.10.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 2.50 / EUR 1.58

Eur-4--an

Ingredients for 5 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
600 g Puff pastry
0.5 pc Eggs
300 g Sweetbreads
750 ml *White Veal Stock 50 ml Dry white wine
30 g Butter 1
30 g White wheat flour 2 100 ml Heavy cream (36%) 1 pc Egg yolks
150 g Mushroom, fresh
9 g Shallots
10 g Butter
1 x Seasoning (lemon juice)
1 x Seasoning (salt)
- Soak sweetbreads in cold water overnight.
- Blanch sweetbreads in boiling water, chill; and drain.
- Use puff pastry to make 10 small patty shells about 6 cm
- Brush patty shells with egg and refrigerate for at least 'I hour. - Bake patty shells and keep warm.
- Trim and wash mushrooms and cut into quarters.
- Chop shallots.
- Make a roux with butter 1 and flour 2.
- BRING VEAL STOCK AND WHITE WINE TO A BOIL; ADD SWEETBREADS.
- RETURN TO A BOIL, SKIM AND POACH SWEETBREADS FOR 15-20 MINS. LET COOL IN STOCK. DRAIN SWEETBREADS; RESERVE STOCK. - REMOVE SKIN AND BREAK SWEETBREADS INTO SMALL PIECES.
SAUTE MUSHROOMS WITH SHALLOTS AND LEMON JUICE IN BUTTER. ADD SWEETBREADS, COVER, HEAT MIXTURE, AND KEEP WARM. - COMBINE ROUX WITH 1 L RESERVED STOCK. BRING TO A BOIL, STIRRING CONTINUOUSLY. SIMMER FOR 10 MINUTES AND KEEP HOT.
- DRAIN OFF JUICES FROM MUSHROOM/SWEETBREADS MIXTURE. REDUCE TO A GLAZE CONSISTENCY AND ADD TO A STOCK.
- MIX HEAVY CREAM AND EGG YOLKS, AND STIR INTO BOILING WATER BUT DO NOT ALLOW TO BOIL ANY LONGER.
- COMBINE SAUCE WITH SWEETBREADS AND SEASON TO TASTE.
USING A SHARP KNIFE, CUT LIDS FROM PA I I Y SHELLS; RESERVE LIDS. - FILL WARM PATTY SHELLS WITH SWEETTBREADS AND PLACE LIDS ON TOP. - SERVE IMMEDIATELY AND DECORATE WITH CHERVIL
SERVE ANY REMAINING FILLING ON THE SIDE.
