Farce:
300 g Chicken breast, skinless

- Wash and trim vegetables.

CHICKEN VEGETABLE TERRINE

Ingredients (1 terrine):
60 g carrot
50 g string beans
50 g broccoli
60 g yellow bell pepper
50 g red bell pepper
15 g salt
Plastic foil and blanched vegetables or Swiss chard for lining

Mousseline Farce:
300 g chicken breast, skinless
8 g sea salt
30 g egg whites
300 g heavy cream

Method:
Line terrine mold with plastic foil, then with blanched vegetables or Swiss chard, creating a vegetable shell.

Wash and trim all vegetables. Cut carrots, beans, and peppers into small batonnet. Cut broccoli into fine florets.

Boil or steam vegetables in salted water until lightly cooked but still firm. Chill in ice water, season with salt, and dry on absorbent paper.

For mousse: Dice chilled chicken breast. Blend in food processor with salt, cream, and egg whites until smooth. Pass through fine sieve. Place over ice and season to taste.

Line terrine mold with thin layer of mousse. Alternate layers of vegetables and mousse, ensuring no air pockets. Top layer must be mousse. Cover with plastic foil.

Poach in convection oven at 75–80°C until center temperature reaches 60–62°C. Place weight on top and cool completely before unmolding.
