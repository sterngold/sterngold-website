Chicken vegatable terrine

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Ingredients for 1.00 pieces at 100% (Net quantity)
60 g Carrot
50 g String beans, fresh
50 g Broccoli, fres
60 g Yellow bell pepper
50 g Red bell pepper
15 g Salt

Farce:
300 g Chicken breast, skinless
8 g Sea salt
30 g Egg whites
300 g * Hèavy cream

- Line terrine mold carefully with plastic foil and then with blanched vegetables or Swiss chard.
- Wash and trim vegetables.
Cut into small long batonet. Cut broccoli into fine florets.
Boil vegetables in salt water, or steam, until cooked lightly but still firm.
Chill in ice water. Salt. Drain and dry on absorbent kitchen paper.


For Mousseline Farce

- Cut chicken breasts into small dice and chill with cream and egg white
Blend chicken meat in food processor with salt and strain through drum sieve.
Place in a bowl over ice. Season to taste.

- LINE PREPARED TERRINE MOLD WITH FARCE.
- FILL TERRINE WITH LAYERS OF VEGETABLES AND FARCE. BE SURE THERE ARE NO AIR BUBBLES.THE TOP LAYER SHOULD BE FARCE. COVER WITH PLASTIC FOIL
- POACH IN CONVECTION OVEN AT 75° - 80 °C, UNTIL TERRINE HAS A CENTER TEMPERATURE OF 60-62°C. PLACE WEIGHT ON TOP AND LET COOL.
