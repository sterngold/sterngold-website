Pastry Cream (without eggs)

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Pastry Cream (without eggs)
Fii//creme
Creme patissiere
Rezept Nr. :706
Kategorie : Desserts
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 28.07.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 1.61 LEUR 1.02


Zutaten fUr 0. 600 Liter zu 100% (Nettomenge)
500 ml Milk
0.5 st Vanilla bean
100 g Crystal sugar
50 g Vanilla cream powder

- SPLIT VANILLA BEAN. SCRAPE OUT MARROW. AND ADD MARROW AND BEAN TO
400 ml MILK. ADD SUGAR. BRING MILK TO A BOIL. AND REMOVE VANILLA BEAN.
- MIX VANILLA CREAM POWDER WITH REMAINING 100 ml MILK AND ADD TO HOT MILK.
- BRING TO A BOIL. WHILE STIRRING.
- CHILL CREAM IN ICE WATER. OR POUR INTO A SHALLOW PAN TO COOL.
