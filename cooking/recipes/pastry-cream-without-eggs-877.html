PASTRY CREAM (WITHOUT EGGS)
Source: DCT | Station: Saucier | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 28.07.2005
Yield: 0.600 liters

Ingredients:
- 500 ml whole milk
- 0.5 vanilla bean
- 100 g crystal sugar
- 50 g vanilla cream powder

Method:
Split the vanilla bean lengthwise and scrape out the marrow. Add the marrow and bean pod to 400 ml of milk along with the sugar. Bring to a boil, then remove and discard the vanilla bean.

Whisk the vanilla cream powder with the remaining 100 ml cold milk until smooth. Add this mixture to the hot milk, stirring constantly. Bring to a boil while stirring continuously.

Chill the cream in an ice water bath or transfer to a shallow pan to cool quickly.
