Chicken broth

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

1.2999999523162842 kg Chicken necks and backs (Roast bones (carcasse))
2.5 kg liter Water
10.0 kg Salt
200 g Bouquet garni for Veal broth
Sachet bag (Consisting of bay leaves, cloves, peppercorns and thyme)

Production

- add cold water to bones and bring to slwo boil
- add slat and skim frequently
- simmer for 1 to 2 hours
- add sachet bag and bouquet garbi after 1 hour
- strain carefully through cheesecloth
CHICKEN BROTH

Method:
1. Add cold water to bones and bring to a slow boil.
2. Add salt and skim frequently throughout cooking.
3. After 1 hour of simmering, add sachet bag and bouquet garni.
4. Continue simmering for a total of 1 to 2 hours.
5. Strain carefully through cheesecloth.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
