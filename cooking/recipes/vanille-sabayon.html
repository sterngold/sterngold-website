Pâtisserie Patisseri Mise en place

Vanille sabayon

Chef: Urs Meichtry Source: DCT

Ingredients

80 g Wine, white, dry
50 g Cristal sugar
6 g Lemon juice
50 g Egg yolk
20 g Wine, sweet dry
Vanille

Production

Combine wine, sugar, lemon and egg on baine marrie and beat till foamy
From DCT · Chef Urs Meichtry
