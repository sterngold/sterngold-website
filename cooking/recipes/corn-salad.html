Garde Manger Salad

Corn salad

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

800 g Corn kernels, canned drained
85 g Bell peppers, assorted, red
128 g Apple
# Dressing
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
1 pcs x Cayenne papper, ground
50 g ml Vinegar, white wine
50 g ml Mayonnaise
100 g ml Oil, sunflower

Production

- RINSE CORN WITH COLD WATER AND DRAIN WELL
- CUT PEPPERS IN HALF, REMOVE SEEDS, AND CUT INTO SMALL DICE
- PEEL AND CORE APPLES AND CUT INTO SMALL DICE
- COMBINE DRESSING INGREDIENTS AND MIX WITH SALAD. LET SALAD MARINATE
Rinse corn with cold water and drain well. Cut peppers in half, remove seeds, and dice small. Peel and core apples, then dice small. Combine dressing ingredients and mix with salad. Allow to marinate.

Notes

ITALIAN dressing is also suitable for this salad
