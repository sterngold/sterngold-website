- Sift together the flour and salt into mixing bowl.
- ADD WATER/YEAST/OIL TO FLOUR AND MIX WITH DOUGH HOOK, AT SLOW SPEED, ABOUT 10 MINUTES.
- CUT AND FORM INTO PORTION AND PLACE ON FLOWERED SHEE I PANS - BRUSH WITH LITTLE OIL
- COVER DOUG WITH TOWEL AND PROOF IN A WARM PLACE 25°C
- ROLL OUT DOUGH VERY THIN AND PLACE ON OILED BAKING TRAY
2.
- SPREAD WITH CREME FRAICHE
- SPRINKLE WITH THINLY SLICED ONIONS EMINCE AND BACON STRIPES - SEASON WITH PEPPER MILL, SALT AND NUTMEG
- SPRINKLE WITH GRATED GRUYERE CHEESE
- BAKE AT 270 -300°C - Serve immediately IMPORTANT:
- Bake at very hot maximum oven temperatures
- Gruyere can also be replaced with Emmental or Sbrinz cheese
