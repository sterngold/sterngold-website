TARTE FLAMBÉE AUX OIGNONS ET LARD (FLAMMKUCHEN)

Yield: 10 servings
Chef: Patrick Diethelm
Recipe #: 682

Dough Ingredients:
20 g fresh yeast
500 g white wheat flour
275 ml cold water
8 g salt
25 ml oil

Topping Ingredients:
500 g crème fraîche
300 g onions
300 g bacon slices
200 g Gruyère cheese, grated
Salt and pepper (from mill)
Nutmeg (pinch)

Dough Method:
1. Sift flour and salt into mixing bowl.
2. Add water, yeast, and oil to flour. Mix with dough hook at slow speed for 10 minutes.
3. Cut and form into portions. Place on floured sheet pans and brush lightly with oil.
4. Cover dough with towel and proof in warm place at 25°C until doubled.
5. Roll out dough very thin and place on oiled baking tray.

Assembly & Baking:
1. Spread dough with crème fraîche.
2. Sprinkle with thinly sliced emincé onions and bacon strips.
3. Season with pepper mill, salt, and nutmeg.
4. Top with grated Gruyère cheese.
5. Bake at 270–300°C (very hot oven) until golden and crispy.
6. Serve immediately.

Note: Gruyère can be replaced with Emmental or Sbrinz cheese. Bake at maximum oven temperature for optimal results.
