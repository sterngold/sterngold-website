Sabsago Glarner Zigerbrutli
Serves 15

INGREDIENTS
100 g Sabsago Ziger
100 g raw Ziger
50 g heavy cream, whipped
Prepared bread slices
Seasonal fruits and walnuts for garnish
Mint leaves or chervil for decoration

METHOD
Grate Ziger on fine Bircher grater. Strain raw Ziger through drum sieve. Whip together until foamy and light in consistency. Fold in whipped cream. Pipe mixture onto prepared bread slices. Garnish with seasonal fruits and walnuts. Decorate with mint leaves or chervil.

Serve as Swiss amuse-bouche or fingerfood for cocktail parties.
