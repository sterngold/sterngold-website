**Bulgur Pilav**

Prepare: Wash bulgur, pass through a hair sieve, and dry thoroughly.

Method: Sweat finely chopped shallots and garlic in olive oil. Add bell pepper jardinière and stir. Add bulgur and stir well. Pour in vegetable stock, bring to a boil with bay leaf, and season with salt and pepper. Cover and cook in oven at 170°C for 20 minutes. Remove from oven, add tomato concassé and chopped Italian parsley, and let rest 5 minutes. Stir carefully and serve.

Note: In Turkey, bulgur is traditionally served as a side dish or intermediate course with turşu (pickled vegetables).

*Yield: 10 servings | Cost CHF 0.59*
