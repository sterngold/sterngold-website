Fish terrine with prawns

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 1

Production

Fish terrine with prawns
Fischterrine mit Riesencrevetten
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Terrine/Paté/Galantine / Mart
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date :14.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 1.43 / EUR 0.90

Black truffles or horn of plenty

- CUT RAW FISH IN DICES
- CUT CHIVES AND CHOP DILL
- MIX ICE COLD CREAM, EGG WHITE, SALT AND FISH
- ADD THE MIXTURE INTO FOOD PROCESSOR AND BLEND UNTIL SMOOTH MOUSSELINE FARCE
- REMOVE AND PLACE IN BOWL ON ICE
- ADD THE POACHED PRAWNS (see course infos)
CHIVES AND CHERVIL AND SEASON WITH SALT,
LEMON JUICE AND NOUILLY PRAT, ADD CHOPPED PISTACCHIOS, BLACK TRUFFLE BRUNOISE.
- LINE OUT TERRINE MOLD WITH THIN WELL DONE COOKED CARROT SLICES FILL IN THE MOUSSELINE FARCE AND COVER
- COVER UP WITH PLASTIC FOIL.
- POACH AT 75°C IN STEAMER FOR APPROX.20-25 MIN. UNTIL CENTER TEMP. OF 55°C. - COOL THE TERRINE IN THE REFRIGERATOR FOR 1 HOUR.

Notes

APPLICATIONS - use as an ingridient in white wine sauce
