FRENCH SAFFRON OYSTER SOUP WITH PERNOD

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

TZNAU- iCERN SWITZERLAND
FRENCH SAFFRON OYSTER SOUP WITH PERNOD
Yield: 6 servings
12 fresh oysters creuse or belon 100 g dry white wine
600 g fish stock
200 g cream
10 g white roux
saffron
salt, pepper
65 g young spinach leaves, blanched 30 g pernod
30 g butter
20 g fennel brunoise
20 g carrot brunoise
20 g leek brunoise
50 g egg yolks 100 g cream
Garnish: Fennel green Melba toast
Preparation:
- Wash and open the oysters
- Reserve the juice, remove and reserve
- Remove the stalks, blanch and dry the spinach on paper towel - Wrap the oysters in the leaves with little egg white.
- Add the pernod, wine, fish stock and the reserved oyster water into rondeau
- Simmer and reduce down to half, thicken with little roux then strain through china cap - add cream and saffron and bring to a boil
A LA MINUTE METHOD
- sweat the vegetables brunoise in sauteuse and add to soup
- Mix the egg yolk with the whipped cream and add half in the soup - Stir well over a low heat but do not boil again, season to taste
- Warm fish stock to 65°C add the oysters for a few seconds. (a la minute)
- Place one prepared oysters in each espresso cup and ladle some soup over each portion - Sprinkle some fennel green to the soup
- spoon the remaining liaison on the soup and gratinate until golden brown. - serve melba toast.
