Garde Manger Hot Appetizer

Asparaugs with goat cheese

Азпержи със козе сирене

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: Father

Production

bekonat se parji dokato stane na korichka, izwajda se
asperjite se waryat 7 minuti
serwirat se 4-5 aspreji ako e predyastie, 10 ako e osnowno blyudo.
otgore se nastargwa sireneto. otgore se slaga bekona
razbiwat se zehtina, limonowiya sok i s towa se poraswa

500 gr zeleni asperji
v
4 parcheta becon
zehtin
limon - sok i parcheta
sol i piper.
Cook bacon until crispy; remove and set aside.

Boil asparagus for 7 minutes. Plate 4-5 spears for appetizer portions, 10 for main course.

Grate goat cheese over asparagus. Top with bacon pieces.

Whisk olive oil with lemon juice and season. Dress the plate with this mixture.

Ingredients:
- 500 g green asparagus
- 4 bacon slices
- Olive oil
- Lemon (juice and zest)
- Salt and pepper

Notes

аспержи със сос камамбер аспержи печени с моцарела аспержи със козе сирене
