RHUBARBE VANILLE SAFFRON PICKLES
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 22.11.2005
Yield: 5 servings
Cost of goods: CHF 0.38 / EUR 0.24

Ingredients:
75 g crystal sugar
150 ml water
0.5 vanilla bean
30 ml sherry vinegar
1 pinch saffron
300 g fresh rhubarb
Pepper and nutmeg to taste

Method:
Split vanilla bean in half. Peel rhubarb and cut into macédoine or bâtonnet. Add sugar, water, vanilla bean, sherry vinegar, and saffron to a pot and reduce by half. Remove vanilla bean and scrape out seeds with a paring knife. Add rhubarb and simmer for 5 minutes—do not overcook. Season with nutmeg and pepper mill. Cool down and serve.
