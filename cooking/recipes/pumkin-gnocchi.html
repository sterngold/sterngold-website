PUMKIN GNOCCHI

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

VITZNAU-LUCERNE SWITZERLAND
PUMKIN GNOCCHI
Yield: 10 servings
400 g pumkin marrow /no skin
150 g white wheat flour
60 g grated parmesan
50 g egg
20 g curd cheese
40 g brunoise of sauteed pumkin brunoise
40 g roasted chopped pumkin seeds
5 g chopped chervil
nutmeg, salt, pepper, pinch of baking powder
30 g butter
50 g pumkin julienne
Preparation:
Cut the pumkin skin off.
Wrap in alu foil ans place it on ovenproof dish and bake at 160° C approx. 35 minutes until well done
Strain/pass pumkin through a drum sieve and cool down. Add egg, cold pumkin brunoise, chervil to dough and chill. Add sifted flour and parmesan.
Season to taste with salt, pepper, nutmeg and baking powder. Allow to rest for 20 min. Scoop out small quenelles into boiling salt water. Poach for 3 - 4 min and drain
Toss in butter, season and arange into the-soup on top of apple rosti
• Small dumplings can be served as soup garnish
PUMPKIN GNOCCHI
Yield: 10 servings | Station: Pasta

Ingredients:
400 g pumpkin marrow (no skin)
150 g white wheat flour
60 g grated Parmesan
50 g egg
20 g curd cheese
40 g pumpkin brunoise, sautéed
40 g roasted chopped pumpkin seeds
5 g chopped chervil
30 g butter
50 g pumpkin julienne
Nutmeg, salt, pepper, pinch of baking powder

Method:
Skin pumpkin and wrap in aluminum foil. Place on ovenproof dish and bake at 160°C for approximately 35 minutes until fully cooked. Pass pumpkin through drum sieve and cool. Add egg, cold pumpkin brunoise, and chervil. Chill dough. Fold in sifted flour and Parmesan. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and baking powder. Rest 20 minutes. Scoop small quenelles into boiling salted water and poach 3–4 minutes. Drain and toss in butter. Arrange on soup with pumpkin julienne and apple rosti.
