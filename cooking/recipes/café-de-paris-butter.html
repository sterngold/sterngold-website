Saucier Sauce cold

Café de Paris butter

Chef: P. Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 1

Ingredients

600 g Butter
50 g ml Wine, white, dry
25 g Shallots
50 g Anchovies, chopped
20 g Cappers in can, extra fine, chopped
20 g Basil, fresh chopped
10 g Thyme, fresh chopped
10 g Rosemary, fresh, chopped
40 g Parsley, fresh, chopped
5 g Paprika
5 g White pepper, ground
3 g Curry powder
25 g Garlic, chopped
100 g piece Egg
10 g ml Meat glaze
50 g Ketchup, tomato
50 g ml Lemon juice
30 g ml Cognac
3 g Salt
30 g ml Worchestershire suace
10 g ml Tabasco

Production

- combine all the chopped ingridinets with the butter ahd whip until foamy
- pipe rosettes on prepared tray with parchmetn papper and chill
- serve with grilled meat or fis
Combine all chopped ingredients with butter and whip until foamy. Pipe rosettes onto a parchment-lined tray and chill. Serve with grilled meat or fish.
