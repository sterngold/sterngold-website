CHICKEN LIVER MOUSSE (PACOJET)

Ingredients:
300 g chicken livers
50 g clarified butter
80 g shallots
60 g white bread (Pullman loaf)
20 ml red Port wine
20 ml Marsala wine
6 g gelatine leaves
200 ml heavy cream (36%)
60 g egg whites
12 g salt
1 pinch marjoram
Pepper to season

Method:
Clean chicken livers carefully. Soak gelatine leaves in cold water.

Cut bread into small croutons. Chop shallots.

Sauté chicken livers in butter quickly over high heat. Remove and set aside.

Add shallots to pan and cook briefly. Deglaze with Port and Marsala wines. Add marjoram. Combine with liver in bowl.

Squeeze out gelatine and dissolve with 30 g cream, stirring well into liver mixture.

Add remaining cream, egg whites, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly.

Fill PACOJET beaker, ensuring level surface. Freeze at −20°C for 24 hours.

PACOTISE twice, portion by portion or process entire beaker. Serve immediately.
