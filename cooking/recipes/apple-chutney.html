Apple Chutney — Recipe (Patrick Diethelm, ECA, 2005)

In sauteuse, combine 75 g crystal sugar, 100 ml apple juice, 50 ml fresh orange juice, 5 g chopped orange zest, 5 g roughly chopped sultanas, 10 g peeled grated ginger, 1 g curry powder, Cayenne pepper to taste, 1 coriander mill, scraped seeds from 0.5 vanilla bean (split bean retained), and 50 ml sherry vinegar. Reduce to half volume.

Add 300 g apples cut into 5 mm dice. Simmer 5 minutes. If too watery, thicken with cornstarch slurry. Remove vanilla bean. Cool completely. Just before service, add 2 g fresh chopped peppermint or lemon thyme.
