Apple chutney

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Apple Chutney
Apfel Chutney
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Sauces Garde-manger
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 14.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.22 / EUR 0.14

European Gourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 5 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
75 g Crystal sugar
100 ml Apple juice
50 g Orange juice fresh
5 g Orange zest Chopped
5 g Sultanas Roughly chopped
1 x Ceyenne pepper  1 x Coriander mill
10 g Ginger Peeled, grated
1 g Curry powder
0.33 pc Vanilla bean Scraped out Split in half
50 ml Sherry vinegar
300 g Apple Green Peel, cut into 5 mm dice
2 g Peppermint, fresh
- ADD SUGAR, JUICE, VANILLA BEAN, SPICES AND SHERRY VINEGAR IN SAUTEUSE - REDUCE TO 1/2
- ADD APPLES AND SIMMER FOR 5 MIN.
- If to watery, thicken with little corn starch and water
- REMOVE VANILLA BEAN
- cool down, and add just before serving chopped mint or lemon thyme.
