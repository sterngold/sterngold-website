Gravad Lax

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Gravad Lax (Scandinavian)
Roh marinierter Lachs
Saumon mariné suédoise [Graved Lax)

Recipe # : 252
Category : ECA - Garde-manger fish
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 10.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 22.56 / EUR 14.27

European Gourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 1.600 kilogram. at 100% [Net quantity)
1.5 kg Salmon fillets 1, with skin
50 g Dill, fresh
40 g Sea salt, coarse
60 g Crystal sugar
2 g White peppercorns
640 g *Mustard Sauce for Gravad Lax
Trim salmon and remove any remaining bones. (
Dry fish fillets with absorbent paper.
Remove dill leaves from stems and chop coarsely.
Crush peppercorns and blend with salt and sugar.
Sprinkle some seasoning mixture in a shallow stainless steel pan.
Place one salmon fillet, skin side down, in pan.
Sprinkle some seasoning mixture and dill.
Put second fillet on top, skin side up.
Sprinkle with remaining seasoning mixture and dill.
Cover with transparent wrap.
Place light weight on top.
Marinate for 1- 2 days. (depense on size fillet
) - Turn fish fillet, to switch top fillet to bottom. - When ready to serve, dry fillets.
- Wash if necessary
PRESENTATION
- Carve salmon and serve on lettuce leaves.
- Garnish with lemon wedge and dill leaves and blossoms. - Serve mustard sauce on the side.
