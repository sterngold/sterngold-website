Saucier Soup

Hungarian Goulash soup

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

48 g Butter, clarified
480 g Beef chuck trimmed (Cut beef into 1 cm cubes)
432 g Onions (Peel and chop)
2 pcs piece Garlic, cloves, peeled (puree)
2 g Caraway seeds, whole (chop very finely)
48 g Paprika (hungarian)
24 g Flour, white wheat
2.4000000953674316 kg liter Beef broth
240 g Portatoes (Peel and cut into permentier size)
204 g Bell peppers, assorted, red (Split in half, remove seeds and wash. Cut into jardiniere size)
228 g Tomatoes (Peel, split remove seeds, cut into tomato concasse)
piece Lemon (grate peel)
1 pcs x Salt

Production

- saute meat and onions in clarified butter
- add garlic, carawey seeds and paprika and continue cooking
- dust with flour, add stock and cook until meat is almost tender
- add red peppers and potatoes and continue cooking until tender
- add diced tomatoes and grated lemon peel
- season to taste

Notes

Additional stock may be needed during cooking

From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
