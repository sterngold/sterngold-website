Entremétier Side dish

Potatoes chutney

Chef: Dennis Source: Tschuggen

Ingredients

10.0 kg Potatoes type A (cut in macedoine or brunoise)
5.0 kg Onions (cut in cubes (brunoise))
1 pcs Garlic (whole piece, heimling raus, fine cut)
1 pcs piece Mango (brunoise)
3 pcs piece Ananas (brunoise)

Production

- cut and blanch potatoes
- cook with the rest of ingredients

Notes

- can add different spices or asia rice vinegar, soya

From Tschuggen · Chef Dennis · 30.03.2006
