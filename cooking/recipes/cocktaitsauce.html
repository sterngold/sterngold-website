Sauce cocktail

Rezept Nr. :39

Kategorie : Sauces

Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 19.04.2005

Warenkosten: CHF 6.52 LEUR 4.12


EUROPEAN CULINARY CENTER
Zutaten fOr 0.500 Liter zu 100% [Nettomenge]
400 ml*Mayonnaise
200 ml Tomato Ketchup
100 ml Sour cream35, %
17 g Horseradish
17 ml fresh Cognac
1 x Seasoning (Tabasco Sauce]
1 x Seasoning (lemon juice]

Peel and grate.
- COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS.
- SERVE WITH TOBASCO SAUCE AND LEMON JUICE.
- ADO SPICES ACCORDING TO TASTE TO GIVE SAUCE A TANGY FLAVOR.
- arrange the hot egg on the salad.
- spoon the sauce to cover the poached egg.
- garnish with fresh sprig of chervil
