COCKTAIL SAUCE

Yield: 500ml | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Recipe #39

INGREDIENTS
400ml mayonnaise
200ml tomato ketchup
100ml sour cream (35%)
17g horseradish, peeled and grated
17ml fresh cognac
Tabasco sauce, to taste
Fresh lemon juice, to taste

METHOD
1. Peel and grate the horseradish.
2. Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl.
3. Season to taste with Tabasco sauce and lemon juice to achieve a tangy flavor.
4. Serve with poached eggs on salad, spooning sauce over the top.
5. Garnish with fresh sprig of chervil.
