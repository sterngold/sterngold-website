Poularde Mediterranean style

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Zutaten für 5 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)

1.5 kg Swiss Poularde
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper)
30 ml Olive oil
100 g Bacon slices
100 g Onions
200 g Assorted bell peppers
20 g Garlic

40 g Tomato paste
500 g Tomatoes

100 g Swiss red wine
400 g Brown veal stock
300 g White chicken stock
100 g Black olives, canned
20 g Brown roux
1 x Seasoning (salt)
6 g Basil, fresh


Cut up as for chicken sauté.


Cut into julienne
Peel and cut into slices.
Remove seeds, wash and cut into julienne. Crush.


Peel, cut in half, remove seeds, and cut into concassé dice.

- SEASON CHICKEN PIECES WITH SALT,PEPPER AND SAUTÉ IN OLIVE OIL. BEFORE CHICKEN PIECES ARE FULLY COOKED, REMOVE FROM PAN. - ADD BACON, ONIONS, PEPPERS, AND GARLIC TO PAN AND SAUTÉ. - ADD DICED TOMATOES, TOMATO PASTE
- DEGLAZE WITH RED WINE AND REDUCE TO SYRUPY CONSISTENCY - ADD STOCK BRING TO A BOIL AND RETURN CHICKEN TO PAN.
COVER AND BRAISE UNTIL ALL CHICKEN PIECES ARE COOKED THROUGH. - ADD OLIVES
- THICKEN WITH ROUX
- ADJUST SEASONING AND KEEP HOT
- SERVE CHICKEN COVERED WITH VEGETABLES.
- Sprinkle and garnish with plugged herbs or fresh pesto
Cut poularde into chicken sauté pieces. Prepare garnish: cut onions into julienne; cut bell peppers into julienne after removing seeds and washing; peel and crush garlic; cut tomatoes into concassé dice after peeling, seeding, and halving.

Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper, sauté in olive oil until partially cooked, then remove from pan. Add bacon, onions, peppers, and garlic to pan and sauté. Add diced tomatoes and tomato paste. Deglaze with red wine and reduce to syrupy consistency. Add stocks and bring to boil. Return chicken to pan, cover, and braise until cooked through. Add olives and thicken with brown roux. Adjust seasoning. Serve chicken covered with vegetables, garnished with fresh basil or pesto.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
