Garde Manger Sauce cold

Italian Dressing

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 3

Ingredients

# 1
11 g Dijon mustard
300 g ml Olive oil
500 g ml Vinegar, red wine
400 g ml Vinegar, white wine
1.0 kg liter Sunflower oil
# 2
200 g ml Italian red wine
23 g Beef broth powder
4 g Oregano dry
6 g Salt
1 g Pepper ground
8 g Garlic
200 g Red bee (cooked)
30 g White roux
4 g Mustard powder

Production

#1
- blend all ingridients
#2
- bring all ingridients to a boil and use bar blender to mix with #1

- strain through china cap and chill
- season to taste

- fill into bottles and label with date and name
