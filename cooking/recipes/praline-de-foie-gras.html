Garde Manger Cold Appetizer

Praline de foie gras

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 4

Ingredients

150 g Terrine de foie gras
75 g ml Heavy cream 36%
2 g Chicken stock base
3 g Gelatine leaves
20 g Aspic powder
130 g Water
40 g Wine, sweet dry

Production

- Scoop out balls from the Foie gras terrine and place on wire rack and chill

- prepare aspic from water and aspic powder and add wine at the end
- chill and cut into dice

- prepare chaud froid with heavy cream, chicken base and gelatin season to taste with salt
- when 40 C warm chaud fraud pour over the cold foie gras
- chill again
- carefully remove praline from wire rack with warm spatula and place on parchment paper
- arange with aspic and décor

Notes

By product - from leftovers Eiswein; Spatlese Coating - can be made with white or braun sauce with gelatine plus cream
