Cafe de paris Butter

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Cafe de paris Butter
Beurre cafe de paris II
Rezept Nr.
Kategorie : Basic preparations
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 11.08.2005
Warenkosten: CHF 14.51 LEUR 9.18

Zutaten fur 1.000 Kilogramm zu 100% [Nettomenge)
600 g Butter, cooking
50 ml Wine. white
25g Shallots
50 g Anchovies
20 g Capers in can, extra fine
20 g Basil. fresh chopped
10 g Thyme, fresh chopped
10 g Rosemary. fresh chopped
40 g Parsley. fresh chopped
5 g Paprika
5 g White pepper. ground
3 g Curry powder
25 g Garlic chopped
2 st Eggs [class extra)
10 ml *Meatglaze
50 g Ketchup. tomato
50 ml Lemon juice. fresh pressed
30 ml Cognac ***
3 g Salt
30 ml Worchestershire sauce
10 ml Tabasco

COMBINE ALL THE CHOPPED INGREDIENTS WITH THE BUTTER AND WHIP UNTIL FOAMY
PIPE ROSETTES ON PREPARED TRAY WITH PARCHMENT PAPER AND CHILL
Serve with grilled meat or fish
