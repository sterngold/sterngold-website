CAFÉ DE PARIS BUTTER
Yield: 1000g | Chef: Patrick Diethelm

Ingredients:
600g butter, cooking
50ml white wine
25g shallots
50g anchovies
20g capers in can, extra fine
20g basil, fresh chopped
10g thyme, fresh chopped
10g rosemary, fresh chopped
40g parsley, fresh chopped
5g paprika
5g white pepper, ground
3g curry powder
25g garlic, chopped
2 eggs (class extra)
10ml meat glaze
50g tomato ketchup
50ml fresh lemon juice
30ml cognac
3g salt
30ml Worcestershire sauce
10ml Tabasco

Method:
Combine all chopped ingredients with butter and whip until foamy. Pipe rosettes onto parchment-lined trays and chill. Serve with grilled meat or fish.
