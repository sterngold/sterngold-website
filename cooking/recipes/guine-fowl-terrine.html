guine fowl terrine

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

VIТZNAU-LUCERNE SWIТZERLAND
GUINEA FOWL TERRINE
Yield: 15 servings
1 Breast of guinea fowl, skinless trimmed, split lengthwise in half
Blanched swiss chard leaves
40 g Seasonal mushroom, sauteed with little olive oil
40 g Cooked ham 2 x 2 mm salpicon
50 g Pistachios
2 g Red pepper corns (dry)

Mousseline Farce
300 g Poultry meat or guinea fowl, cut into emince or salpicon
20 g White Port wine
7g Salt
Saffron disolved with 0.5c warm cream (cool down)
250 g Cream
15g Past. egg whites

Preparation
- For the farce, chill all the ingredients
- Add guinea fowl meat in food processor with 200 gram cream, saffron cream, salt and egg whites and blend to a smooth mousseline farce
- Chill on ice
- Pass through drum sieve, put in a stainless steel bowl and place it back on ice
- Stir in the cold mushrooms, pistachio, ham, port wine and red pepper corns,
- Season to taste
- Wrap the seasoned guinea fowl breast pieces into the swiss chard green
brushed with extra egg white
- Use a tunnel terrine mold, lined out with plastic foil
- Line out terrine mold with blanched prepared dry swiss chard leaves
- Fill prepared terrine mold, with about half of the farce, place wrapped guinea fowl breast pieces on top
- Fin mold with the remaining mousseline farce and smooth out top
- Cover with overlapping swiss chard and plastic foil
- Rap mold on table for a few times to settle the mousseline farce
- Cover terrine and place in Combi steamer at 75° C. with steam until reaches the center temperature of 60°- 62°C
- Remove and cool down on wire rack, then place in refrigerator
- Place some weight on top
- Keep chilled at 1- 3°C until served
