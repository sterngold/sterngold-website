Breakfeast Dessert

Birchermusli

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen

Ingredients

3.0 kg liter Milk
900 g Cream
1.5 kg Yogurt, plain
600 g Sugar
900 g Almond (use very fine once)
1.0 kg Muslli (Haferflocken)
150 g Sulataninen
250 g Lemon juice
300 g Almonds, fine
7 pcs piece Apple
5 pcs piece Pears
6 pcs piece Banana

Production

Mix all ingredients. Appel, Pears and Banana can be double in quantity if at the end the mixture is to runny.
Mix all ingredients together. Note: Apple, pears, and banana quantities can be doubled if the final mixture is too runny.
From Tschuggen · Chef Ulf Wein · 25.03.2006
