Entremétier Soup 🇫🇷 France

Artichoke cream soup

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 0

Ingredients

8 g Butter
20 g Shallots (cut into fine matignon)
11 g Leeks (Wash and trim. Be shure that sand is cleaned. Cut into small matignon)
8 g Knob Celery (celeriac) (Trim and wash and cut into small matignon)
3 pcs piece Artchoke, bottoms (Keep one piece for brunoise garnish and décor)
4 g White roux
5 g Lemon juice
200 g Chicken stock, white
70 g ml Heavy cream 36%
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper

Production

- sweet fine matignon of shallots, leeks, and knob celer in butter.
- add artchoke and continue cooking
- add lemon juice and white chicken stock, bring to boil and simmer until vegetables are soft
- thicken with little roux
- add cream and bring to boil
- season to taste
- blend in bar blender, strain through a china cap
- add extra brunoise of artichoke bottoms
- garnish with some artichoke chips in small amuse bouche dishes for soups
- decorate with small leaves chervil

Notes

- has to be really white - really fast - 20 min ready
