Pancake Batter for Consommé celestine
Pfannkuchenteig Kraftbrühe Pâte à crêpes


Rezept Nr. : 741
Kategorie : ECA - Dough
Koch : P.Diethelm
Datum : 15.02.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 3.75 / EUR 2.37

Culinary FEC . TERM 1
Zutaten für 0.200 Liter zu 100% (Nettomenge)
80 g White wheat flour Or cake flour
100 ml Milk
90 g Egg
2 g Parsley, fresh
1 x Salt
20 g Butter Clarified Melt.
- MIX MILK, EGGS AND FINE CHOPPED PARSLEY WITH A WHISK.
- STIR IN SIFTED FLOUR AND SEASON WITH SALT PEPPER AND NUTMEG AND STIR TO MAKE A SMOOTH BATTER.
- LET BATTER REST FOR 1/2 HOUR BEFORE USE.
- whip out crepe pan and prepare pancakes.
- cool down on wire rack and cut into bigger julienne (see course instruction)
