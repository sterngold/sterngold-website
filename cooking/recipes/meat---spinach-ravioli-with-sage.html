MEAT-SPINACH RAVIOLI WITH SAGE
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Recipe #638 | 10 servings

PREPARATION:
Cut veal and pork shoulder into emincé. Peel and chop shallots and garlic. Wash spinach, blanch, chill, and drain thoroughly. Squeeze out excess moisture. Wash and chop fresh herbs (chervil, chives, parsley).

MEAT FILLING:
Sauté diced veal and pork in peanut oil until golden brown. Add shallots and garlic. Deglaze with white wine, add brown veal stock, bring to boil, cover and simmer over low heat until meat is tender. Cool completely. Add spinach and herbs, season with salt and pepper. Grind mixture through fine plate of meat grinder or purée in food processor. Fold in butter nuggets and egg yolk. Fill ravioli.

RAVIOLI:
Prepare and cook ravioli in salted water. Drain at al dente, refresh, and toss in olive oil-butter mixture.

GARNISH:
Wash sage and cut into chiffonade. Melt glace de viande and pour over arranged ravioli. Sauté sage chiffonade in butter and scatter over hot ravioli.
