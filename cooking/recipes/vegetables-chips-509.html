Vegetables chips

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

1. Slice / on Silk pat 80 C – stem open
2. Deep fat fryer – 130 – 160 C (low sugar ones) – more sugar less temperature – carrots 140 C
3. 60 g water / 40 g sugar / 20 – 30 g vinegar – boil, keep in small bottle - add vegetables to liquid
than on silk pad –oven 80 – 120 – stem open
4. Tomato skin – with sugar / herbs – oven 80 – 120 C
5. Parmezan chips – 80 g cheese + 20 g flour – can make cigars
Slice using mandoline - deep fat fry in 140 to 150 C until crsipy
Carrtos - slice thin and then add to in plate warmer
Fennel. Radis, endive - vegetables with little sugar or any veg with strange taste - 100 g water
40 g any type of vinegar / 60 g sugar / boil / add herb, curry, ginger, strain and chill / Add to bottle /
Label - sugar for vegetablesw chips / cut vegetables on meat slicer - 2 mm thcik lengthwise
/ add in syrop / oven wit steam escape - 100 to 120 C
Tomatoes - skins sprinkle with icing sugar in oven at 80 C or plate warmer
Cherry tomatoes - blanch / peel skin not completely off - shape should look like carrot top / deep fat fry skin
