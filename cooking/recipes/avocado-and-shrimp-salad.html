Avocado and Shrimp Salad

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

3 pcs piece Avocados
10 g ml Lemon juice
500 g Shrimp, canned, small
50 g ml Cognac
250 g ml Cocktail sauce
25 g Sesame seeds (peeled)
20 g Black olives, canned
5 pcs piece Lemon
10 pcs piece Dill, fresh, tops

Production

- avocados - cut in half, remove pits and sprinkle with lemon juice to prevent oxydation
- Shrimps -- marinate in cognac and mix with coctail sauce
- sesame seeds - toast (without adding any fat) in black cast iron pan
- lemon - cut into 10 very thin slices

- FILL AVOCADO HALVES WITH SHRIMP SALAD
- SPRINKLE WHIT SEASAME SEEDS
- GARNISH WITH A LEMON SLICE, BLACK OLIVE AND DILL TWIG
AVOCADO AND SHRIMP SALAD

Cut avocados in half and remove pits. Sprinkle with lemon juice to prevent oxidation.

Marinate shrimps in cognac and mix with cocktail sauce.

Toast sesame seeds in a dry cast-iron pan without added fat.

Slice lemon into 10 very thin slices.

FILL avocado halves with shrimp salad. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. Garnish each with a lemon slice, black olive, and dill twig.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
