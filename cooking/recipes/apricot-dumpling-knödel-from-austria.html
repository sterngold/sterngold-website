Production

Apricot dumpling ( Knödel ) from Austria
Aprikosenknödel
Rezept Nr.
Kategorie : ECA - Entremetier/ side dishes
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 27.01.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 0.66 / EUR 0.42

European Gourmet Cuisine
Zutaten für 10 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)
300 g Apricots
360 g Curd cheese
200 g Semolina (hard wheat] 180 g White wheat flour 150 g Butter
2 x Salt
150 g Mie de pain 250 g Butter
- MAKE DUMPLING DOUGH WITH CURD CHEESE SEMOLINA AND WHEAT FLOUR AND BUTTER AND SEASON WITH PINCH OF SALT.
- LET REST FOR 1/2 HOUR IN REFRIGERATOR.
- FOR KNÖDEL CRUMBS
ROAST FRESH MIE DE PAIN IN THE BUTTER UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN AND DRAIN ON PAPER TOWEL
- FORM THE DUMPLING MIXTURE AROUND THE APRICOT OR OTHER SEASONAL FRUITS
- POACH AT 90°C IN SALTED WATER
AS SOON AS THEY FLOAT ON TOP SIMMER FOR 10 MIN.
- REMOVE CAREFULY WITH SPIDER AND DREDGE IN ROASTED CRUMBS AND SERVE WITH GAME DISHES OR AS AMUSE BOUCHE
