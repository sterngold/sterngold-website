CHOCOLATE MOUSSE

Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 17.11.2005
Category: ECA - Pacojet

Ingredients (10 servings):
- 200 g dark chocolate chips
- 200 g heavy cream
- 200 g pasteurized milk
- 8 g gelatine leaves
- 120 g egg whites
- 50 g Grand Marnier

Method:
1. Heat heavy cream and milk together.
2. Add chocolate chips and melt on low temperature.
3. Soak gelatine sheets in cold water, squeeze out excess, and add to melted chocolate. Chill.
4. Whip egg whites to soft peaks and fold into the cold chocolate mixture with Grand Marnier.
5. Fill into Pacojet beaker and freeze at −24°C.
6. Add a little cream to the frozen chocolate, then Pacotize twice.

Note: Recipe can also be made with white chocolate using only 20 g Grand Marnier.
