Pâtisserie Patisseri Mise en place

Pastry cream (without eggs)

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 0

Ingredients

500 g ml Milk
piece Vanille
100 g Cristal sugar
50 g Vanille cream powder

Production

- split vanilla bean, scrape out marrow and add bean and marrow to a 400 ml milk
- add sugar
- bring milk to boil and remove vanilla bean
- mix vanilla cream powder with remaining 100 ml milk and add to hot milk
- bring to a boil, while stirring
- chill cream in ice water, or pour into shallow pan to cool
