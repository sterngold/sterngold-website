SEMURA JAWA | Tabac | Saucier | Per portion

Ingredients:
- Beef: 250–300 g
- Onion: 200 g
- Garlic: 2 cloves
- Ginger: 4 cm
- Nutmeg: 1 tablespoon
- Clove: 1 teaspoon
- Bay leaves: 8
- Turmeric: 1 teaspoon
- Coconut milk: 70 ml

Method: Braise beef with onion, garlic, and ginger. Toast nutmeg, clove, and turmeric, then add to pot with bay leaves. Finish with coconut milk to deglaze.
