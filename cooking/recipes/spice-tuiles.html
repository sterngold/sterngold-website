SPICE TUILES | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Yield: 10 servings

Ingredients:
25 g white wheat flour
25 g icing sugar
30 ml heavy cream (36%)
30 g egg whites
Seasonal spices for garnish

Method:
Sift flour and icing sugar together. Stir in cream and egg whites. Spread mixture with a spatula on a silpat and sprinkle with seasonal spices. Bake at 170°C for approximately 6 minutes.
