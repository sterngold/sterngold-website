Garde Manger Salad

Green Beans, tomatoes and Mushrooms

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

500 g String beans, fresh
500 g Tomatoes
1 pcs piece Head of lettuce
350 g Mushrooms, fresh
# Dressing
20 g ml Lemon juice
# Sauce
5 g Garlic
5 g Thyme, fresh chopped
5 g Basil, fresh chopped
150 g ml Olive oil cold pressed
100 g ml Oil, sunflower
133 g mll Vinegar, white wine
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
200 g Bell peppers, assorted
100 g Onions

Production

- String beans - trim boil in salted water until still crisp, drain, chill and drain again. Cut into 35 mm lengths
- tomatoes - cut in half, cut each a half into eight wedges
- lettuce - trim and wash, separate leaves
- bell pepper - - trim and wash, cut in half and remove seeds. Cut into fine strips (julienne)
- onions - peel and slice into thin rings

- CHOP GARLIC
- MAKE VINAIGRETE WITH GARLIC, HERBS, OILS, VINEGAR AND SEASONING
- COMBINE GREEN BEANS AND TOMATO WEDGES AND SEASON WITH HALF THE VINAIGRETTE
- ARRANGE SMALL LETTUCE LEAVES ON PLATES. PLACE A BOUGUET OF GREEN BEAN / TOMATO SALAD IN THE CENTER OF EACH PLATE, A TOP OF LETTUCE
- PLACE BOUGUETES OF MUSHROOMS AND PEPPERS AROUND GREEN BEAN /TOMATO SALAD
- DRIZZLE REMAINING VINAIGRETTE OVER THE MUSHROOMS AND THE PEPPERS
-GRANISHWITH ONION RINGS AND FRESH HERBS
**Green Beans, Tomatoes and Mushrooms**

Pre-prep:
Trim and boil green beans in salted water until still crisp. Drain, chill, and drain again. Cut into 35 mm lengths.

Halve tomatoes, then cut each half into eight wedges.

Trim and wash lettuce; separate leaves.

Trim and wash bell peppers, halve, remove seeds, and cut into fine julienne strips.

Peel onions and slice into thin rings.

Chop garlic.

Method:
Make vinaigrette with garlic, herbs, oils, vinegar, and seasoning.

Combine green beans and tomato wedges; season with half the vinaigrette.

Arrange small lettuce leaves on plates. Place a bouquet of green bean and tomato salad in the centre, topped with lettuce.

Arrange bouquets of mushrooms and peppers around the green bean mixture.

Drizzle remaining vinaigrette over mushrooms and peppers. Garnish with onion rings and fresh herbs.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
