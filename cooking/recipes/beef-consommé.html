Saucier Soup 🇫🇷 France

Beef consommé

Kraftbruhe

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 1

Ingredients

1.7999999523162842 kg liter Beef broth
360 g Lean beef (Grind meat through coarse plate of meat grinder)
120 g ml Water
31 g Carrot (Cut into matignon)
35 g Onions
20 g Knob Celery (celeriac) (Cut into matignon)
35 g Leeks (Wash and trim leeks. Clean sand. Cut into matignon)
23 g Tomatoes (Dice)
23 g Egg whites
1 pcs piece Bay leaves
5 pcs piece Peppercorns, white
1 pcs x Seasoning (salt )

Production

- make clarification mixture by combining meat, carrots, knob celery, leeks, tomatoes, egg whites, bay leaf and white peppercorns with water. Mix througly and refrigerate for at least 1/2 hour

- throughly combine meat mixture with cold bouillon
- stirring carefully with frying spatula until 97 C. Once consomme starts to boil, do not stir any longer and add 3 -4 ice cubes to cool.
- lower heat and simmer for at least 1/2 hour.
- season to taste
- strain carefully through napkin
- remove all fat with absorbent paper
- add little sherry wine to parfume
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
