Cream Sauce

Chef: P. Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 0

Ingredients

25 g Butter
25 g Onions
38 g Flour, white wheat
650 g ml Milk
100 g ml Heavy cream 36%
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
1 pcs x Nutmeg (pinch)

Production

- cut onions into small dice and sweet in butter
- add flour and cook briefly
- cool resulting roux
- bring milk to a boiling point and add to roux
- simmer for about 20 minutes
- add cream and bring to boil again and season to taste
- strian through fine china cap
- cover with a lid or use immediately
Dice onions small and sweat in butter. Add flour and cook briefly. Cool the resulting roux. Bring milk to a boil and add to roux. Simmer for approximately 20 minutes. Add cream and return to boil. Season to taste. Strain through a fine china cap. Cover with a lid or use immediately.
