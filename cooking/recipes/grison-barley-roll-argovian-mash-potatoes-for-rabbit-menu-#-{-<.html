Grison Barley roll & Argovian mash potatoes (for rabbit menu) # { <

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Grison Barley roll & Argovian mash potatoes
MOM
Hot Kitchen/ Main course starch dish 110 Pax. Bündner Gerstenrolle und Aargauer Kartoffelpüree
Rezept Nr. : Hot Kitchen 2
Kategorie : Culinary Olympics, Erfurt 2004
Koch : Swiss Junior Team
Datum : 28.02.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 0.70 / EUR 0.45
Zutaten für 10 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)

PRE - PREPARATION:
- Soak the barley in vegetable stock (not salted) # 2.1 Potato chips garnish
- Slice potatoes thin on meat slicer and cut into triangles
- Place on silpat and brush with warm mixture of paprika, butter and egg white
- Dry in oven with open steam escape until dry and crisp
