Osso buco

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Osso Buco (braised veal shanks)
Ossibuchi Cremolata
Osso buco de veau gremolata

Ingredients for 5 servings at 100% [Net quantity)

- BROWN CUT VEAL SHANKS ON BOTH CUT SIDES, IN OLIVE OIL
- REMOVE MEAT AND POUR OFF EXCESS OIL
- SEASON VEAL SHANKS
- ADD CHOPPED ONIONS AND SAUTÉ. ADD BRUNOISE OF CARROTS, KNOB CELERY, AND LEEKS AND CONTINUE COOKING.
- ADD TOMATO PASTE AND CHOPPED GARLIC.
- ADD SAGE, OREGANO, AND WHITE WINE AND REDUCE ALMOST COMPLETELY.
- ADD VEAL SHANKS AND SAUTÉ BRIEFLY.
- ADD SUFFICIENT FOND DE VEAU BRUN TO COVER MEAT.
- COVER RONDEAU AND BRAISE MEAT IN OVEN. TURN MEAT AND BASTE OCCASIONALLY. - ADD TOMATO CONCASSE SHORTLY BEFORE VEAL IS TENDER.
- CHECK SAUCE CONSISTENCY AND REDUCE IF NECESSARY. ADJUST SEASONING.
- SERVE VEAL SHANKS COVERED WITH SAUCE; SPRINKLE WITH CREMOLATA GARNISH.
Note: The marrow in the center of the bone is a delicacy. A marrow spoon or an oyster fork should be provided to customers so the marrow can be eaten.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
