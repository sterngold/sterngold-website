POTATO CAKES WITH PARSLEY
Yield: 10 servings | Chef: Patrick Diethelm

Ingredients:
1.1 kg potatoes
25 g butter
4 egg yolks
100 g white wheat flour
3 g fresh parsley, chopped
100 g clarified butter
Salt, pepper, nutmeg (pinch)

Method:
Wash, peel, and cut potatoes into even chunks. Steam or cook in lightly salted water; drain thoroughly. Dry briefly in oven. Pass through sieve or ricer. Blend hot purée with butter and egg yolks. Chill mixture, then fold in white wheat flour and season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Divide into two equal portions and shape into tubular rolls approximately 35 mm diameter. On floured work surface, cut into 12 mm thick slices. Score surface in crosshatch pattern with back of knife. Flour lightly and sauté in clarified butter until golden brown on both sides.
