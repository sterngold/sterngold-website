Entremétier Soup

Knob celery cream soup with parmesan truffle dumplings and white truffle oil # !

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 8

Ingredients

50 g Butter
50 g Shallots (cut emince)
300 g Knob Celery
600 g ml Chicken bouillon
80 g ml Wine, white, dry
250 g ml Cream
60 g Beurre noisette
100 g ml Cream, whipped
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
40 g Knob Celery (celeriac) (Cut brunoise. Stwed for garnish)
# Dumplings
60 g Parmesan, grated
40 g Mie de pain, fresh
20 g Flour, white wheat
20 g Wine, white, dry
50 g Egg
15 g Black truffles (cut brunoise)
2 g Chervil (fine chopped)
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
Nutmeg (pinch)

Production

- peel and grate the knob celey with rosty grater
- sweat shallots in butter, add celery and stew
- add stock and the cream and bring to boil
- simmer until soft, then pour white wine and blend the soup with the brown butter. Season to taste

# Dumplings
- add all ingredients into a bowl and mix to a dough. Season
- Shape dumplings whte two tea spoons and poach in chicken stock
- If dumplings to soft add more parmesan

- bring soup to a boil, add whipped cream and blend with pole blender until foamy
- serve in soup plate and add the hot dumplings and all the garnish
- drizzle with few drops of white truffle oil

# Garnish
- droplets of white truffle oil
- small sprig of basil
- black truffel brunoise or julliene
KNOB CELERY CREAM SOUP WITH PARMESAN TRUFFLE DUMPLINGS AND WHITE TRUFFLE OIL
Station: Entremétier | Source: DCT

SOUP
Peel and grate knob celery on a box grater. Sweat shallots in butter, add celery, and stew until soft. Add stock and cream, bring to a boil and simmer until celery is tender.

Deglaze with white wine. Blend soup with brown butter until smooth. Season to taste. Just before service, bring to a boil, add whipped cream, and blend with an immersion blender until foamy.

DUMPLINGS
Combine all dumpling ingredients in a bowl and mix to a dough. Season to taste. Using two teaspoons, shape dumplings and poach in chicken stock until cooked. If dumplings are too soft, add more parmesan.

FINISH
Pour soup into plates. Add hot dumplings. Garnish with black truffle brunoise or julienne, small basil sprigs, and drizzle with drops of white truffle oil.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
