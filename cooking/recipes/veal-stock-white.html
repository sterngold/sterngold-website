Veal stock, white

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

1.2000000476837158 kg Veal bones (chop and blanch. Rinse bones first with hot water and then cold water)
2.799999952316284 kg liter Water
10 g Salt
200 g Vegetable Bundle for white stock (white leeks and onion picke)
1 pcs piece Sachet bag (consisting of peppercorns juniper berries, bay leaves, cloves and thyme)

Production

- add cold water to bones and bring to a boil
- add slat and skim frequently
- simmer for 2 to 3 hours
- add sachet bag and bouquet garni after 2 hours
- strain carefully through large hair sieve and again through sheesecloth
VEAL STOCK, WHITE
Station: Saucier | DCT

METHOD
Add cold water to bones and bring to a boil. Add salt and skim frequently. Simmer for 2–3 hours. After 2 hours, add sachet bag and bouquet garni. Strain carefully through large hair sieve, then again through cheesecloth.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
