ALLEMANDE SAUCE | DCT, Saucier | Chef Patrick Diethelm, 18.01.2005
Yield: 500 ml

400 ml veal velouté sauce
200 ml white veal stock
1 egg yolk
100 ml heavy cream (36%)
25 g cold butter
Seasoning: lemon juice, salt, cayenne pepper

Reduce veal stock quickly to a syrupy consistency without browning. Add veal velouté and bring to boiling point. Combine egg yolk with cream and whisk into sauce without allowing it to boil. Season to taste and strain through fine chinois. Mount with cold butter added in small nuggets.

Use as a base for white meat sauce variations, with pasta, or with veal fillings for patty shells.
