Garde Manger Salad

Apples, carrots and sultanas salad

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 6

Ingredients

300 g Apple
30 g ml Lemon juice
300 g Carrot
90 g ml Orange, juice
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
1 pcs pinch Crystal sugar
30 g Dark sultana raisins
Salad leaves
90 g ml Heavy cream 36%
5 g Horseradish, fresh
1 pcs x Salt
30 g Sunflower seeds roasted
5 g Ginger

Production

Mise and place
- peel and core apple and cut into quarters. Dice the apples and blend with lemon juice to prevent oxydation
- peel carrots and grate coarsley
- blanch sulatanas, drain and let cool
- prepare smal sald leaves for decorations
- roast sunflower seeds under the salamander until golden brown
- whip heavy cream
- peel and grate horseradish and fold into shipped cream
- peel and grate ginger and fold into whipped cream

-COMBINE APPLES, CARROTS AND ORANGE JUICE. ADD SULTANAS
- SEASON HORSERADISH CREAM WITH SALT AND PEPPER, SUGAR AND CAYENNE
- ARRANGE BELGIAN ENDIVE LEAVES ON PLATES AND PLACE SALAD IN CENTRE
- TOP WITH HORSERADISH/GINGER CREAM, USING PASTRU BAG OR SPOON
- SPRINKLE WITH SUNFLOWER SEEDS
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
