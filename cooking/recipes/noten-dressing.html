Noten dressing

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

mostard - 2 et l
egg yolk 1 el
sherryazijn 1 dl
normal olie - 3dl
noten olie - 1 dl
slat pepper, cayenneppper
Nut Dressing

Whisk together 2 tablespoons mustard with 1 egg yolk. Add 1 dl sherry vinegar. Slowly incorporate 3 dl neutral oil, then 1 dl nut oil, whisking constantly to emulsify. Season with salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper to taste.
From VandeMarkt · Chef V. I. Sterngold
