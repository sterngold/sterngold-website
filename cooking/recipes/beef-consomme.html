Beef Consomme
Kraftbrtjhe Consomme nature


Recipe 4# : 161
Category : Soups
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 25.10.2005
Cost of goods CHF 4.08 / EUR 2.58


Ingredients for 1.200 liters at 100% (Net quantity)
1,8 l *Beef Broth 360 g Lean Beef 120 ml Water
31 g Carrot
35 g Onions
20 g Knob celery (celeriac)
35 g Leeks
23 g Tomatoes
120 g Egg whites
'I pc Bay leaves
5 pc White peppercorns  1 x Seasoning (salt)
- Grind meat through coarse plate of meat grinder.
- Cut carrots and knob celery into matignon.
- Wash and trim leeks; be sure sand is cleaned from layers. Cut into matignon.
- dice tomatoes.
- Make clarification mixture by combining meat, carrots, knob celery, leeks, tomatoes, egg
whites, bay leaf, and white peppercorns with water, mix thoroughly, and refrigerate for at least 1/2 hour.
- THOROUGHLY COMBINE MEAT MIXTURE WITH COLD BOUILLON.
- STIRRING CAREFULLY WITH FRYING SPATULA UNTIL 97°C, ONCE CONSOMME STARTS TO BOIL, DO NOT STIR ANY LONGER. AND ADD 3-4 ICE CUBES TO COOL.
- LOWER THE HEAT
SIMMER FOR AT LEAST '1/2 HOUR.
- SEASON TO TASTE.
- STRAIN CAREFULLY THROUGH NAPKIN.
- REMOVE ALL FAT WITH ABSORBENT PAPER.
- ADD LITTLE SHERRY WINE TO PARFUME

1 liter broth - 300 - 400 g clarification
If not said - always beef consomme'
