BEEF CONSOMMÉ
Recipe #161 | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 25.10.2005
Yield: 1,200 liters

INGREDIENTS
1.8 l Beef broth
360 g Lean beef
120 ml Water
31 g Carrot
35 g Onions
20 g Knob celery (celeriac)
35 g Leeks
23 g Tomatoes
120 g Egg whites
1 Bay leaf
5 White peppercorns
Salt to taste
Sherry wine for finishing

METHOD
Grind meat through coarse plate of meat grinder. Cut carrots and knob celery into matignon. Wash, trim, and carefully clean leeks of sand; cut into matignon. Dice tomatoes.

Make clarification mixture by combining meat, carrots, knob celery, leeks, tomatoes, egg whites, bay leaf, and white peppercorns with water. Mix thoroughly and refrigerate at least 30 minutes.

Thoroughly combine meat mixture with cold beef bouillon. Place over heat and stir carefully with frying spatula until 97°C is reached. Once consommé begins to boil, stop stirring immediately and add 3–4 ice cubes to cool. Lower heat and simmer at least 30 minutes.

Season to taste. Strain carefully through napkin. Remove all fat with absorbent paper. Add a little sherry wine to perfume before service.

Note: Standard ratio is 1 liter broth to 300–400 g clarification mixture.
