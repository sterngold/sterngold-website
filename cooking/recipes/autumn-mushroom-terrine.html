Autumn mushroom terrine

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

VITZNAU-LUCERNE SWITZERLAND
AUTUMNAL MUSHROOM TERRINE WITH HERBS

Yield: 8 servings
600 g seasonal mushrooms
100 g white wine
40 g cognac or brandy
250 g cream
6 leaves of gelatine
6 g salt, pepper mill ,
20 g mixture of basil, chervil, parsley, chives



Preparation:
- Trim and clean the mushrooms, cut into 10mm x 10mm dice
- Sauté in a non-stick frying pan over high heat
- Season to taste with salt and pepper
- Add cognac and white wine
- Drain now in a hair sieve, keep the stock in a sauteuse and reduce by two-thirds - Pour in the cream and reduce for 2 - 3 minutes
- Soak the gelatine in cold water until soft
- Chill the cream mixture until lukewarm, add the dry squeezed gelatine and stir to dissolve, add the chopped herbs
- Line small terrine dishes with plastic foil, allow a little to overhang
- Fill the dry cold mushrooms into the dish
- Add the warm cream mixture and cover the mushrooms, leave to rest in the refrigerator for 2 - 3 hours
- Unmold the chilled mushroom terrine
- To serve, cut carefully into 2.5 cm thick slices and arrange on a plate with fresh tomato-chives vinaigrette and young spinach leaves or seasonal salad bowl. - Garnish with sprigs of basil and a few short lengths of fresh chives.
