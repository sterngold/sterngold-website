AUTUMNAL MARKET VEGETABLES
Culinary Olympics, Erfurt 2004 | Swiss Junior Team

For 10 portions:
118g Carrots, peeled
118g Black salsify, peeled
118g Pumpkin, peeled
155g Green patisson, small
73g Black-eyed beans
52g Butter
4g Brown sugar
91g Vegetable stock
7g Salt

PRE-PREPARATION:
Cut all vegetables to uniform size. Blanch carrots, black salsify, and pumpkin, then refresh. Boil black-eyed beans until nearly tender.

PREPARATION:
Heat butter in suitable pan, add blanched carrots and black salsify. Sprinkle with sugar and smother lightly. Add vegetable stock, season, cover and stew until two-thirds cooked. Add patisson, pumpkin, and black-eyed beans, cover again. When three-quarters cooked, remove lid and reduce liquid rapidly to a syrupy consistency. Toss all vegetables in syrup to glaze à la minute during service.
