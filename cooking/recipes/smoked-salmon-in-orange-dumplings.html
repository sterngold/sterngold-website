SMOKED SALMON IN ORANGE DUMPLINGS
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Station: Garde Manger | Date: 05.12.2005

Ingredients (8 servings):
300g smoked salmon, trimmed, boneless
250g bread (Bretzel and Weggli)
125g milk paste
125g egg
1g rosemary
3 oranges (juice and grated peel)
Salt, pepper, nutmeg to taste

Method:
- Cut royale fillets from smoked salmon and split lengthwise in half.
- Prepare dumpling mixture according to course instructions. Marinate 1 hour.
- Line a triangle terrine mold with Swiss chard, then dumpling mixture.
- Place salmon fillets in center and cover with remaining dumpling mixture.
- Steam terrine at 75°C for approximately 20 minutes.
- Chill and press with weight.
- Slice carefully into 8–10 portions and rest in warm place for 10 minutes.
- Serve with herb dill, marinated pike perch fillet, sauerkraut, and red cabbage salad with bacon bits.
