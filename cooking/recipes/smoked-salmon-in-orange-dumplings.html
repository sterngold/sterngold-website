Garde Manger Cold Appetizer

Smoked salmon in orange dumplings

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Smoked salmon in orange dumpling
Rauchlachs im Orangen-Knode/mante/
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Garde-manger fish
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 05.12,2005
Cost of goods :CHF 1.83 / EUR 1.15

Eurapean Gourmet Cuisine

Ingredients for 8 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
300 g Smoked salmon, trimmed, boneless #2
250 g Bread Bretzel and Weggli
'125 g Milk past.
125 g Egg
1 g Rosemary
3 pc Orange (juice and grated peel)
Seasoning (salt and pepper) Nutmeg (pinch)
Cut royale filet out of smoked salmon and split lenghtwise in half (see instructions) - Prepare Dumpling mixture (see course instruction) marinate for 1 hour Z
line out triangle terrine mold with swiss chard and with dumpling mixture.
place salmon into center and cover again with dumpling mixture.
- Steam terrine at 75° C for about 20 Minutes chill and press with some weigth. - Slice carefully into 8 -10 Port. and place for 10 Minutes in warm place.
- SERVE WITH HERB DIL AND MARINATED PIKE PERCH FILET AND SAUERKRAUT - RED CABBAGE SALAD WITH BACON BITS
