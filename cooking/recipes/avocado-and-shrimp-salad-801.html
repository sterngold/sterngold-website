Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Avocado and Shrimp Salad
Avocado mit Krevetten Avocat aux crevettes
Rezept Nr. : 198
Kategorie : Cold Appetizers
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 20.07.2005
Warenkosten: CHF 2.26 LEUR 1.43
Zutaten fUr 10 Personen zu 100% [Nettomenge)
3 st Avocados Cut in half, remove pits. and sprinkle lemon juice to prevent oxidation.
10 ml Lemon juice
500 g Shrimp, canned. small Marinate in cognac. and mix with cocktail sauce.
50 ml Cognac or any brandy
250 m/ *Cocktai/ Sauce
25g Sesame seeds [peeled) Toast (without adding any fat) in black cast iron pan.
20 g Black olives, canned
5 st Lemon Cut into 10 very thin slices.
10 st Dill sprigs,tops, fresh
PRESENTATION
- FILL AVOCADO HALVES WITH SHRIMP SALAD.
- SPRINKLE WITH SESAME SEEDS. GARNISH WITH A LEMON SLICE. BLACK OLIVE, AND DILL TWIG.
