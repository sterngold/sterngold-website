AVOCADO AND SHRIMP SALAD
Recipe #198 | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 20.07.2005
Yield: 10 servings

INGREDIENTS
3 Avocados
10 ml Lemon juice
500 g Shrimp, canned, small
50 ml Cognac or brandy
250 ml Cocktail sauce
25 g Sesame seeds, peeled
20 g Black olives, canned
5 Lemons
10 sprigs Fresh dill

METHOD
Cut avocados in half and remove pits. Sprinkle immediately with lemon juice to prevent oxidation.

Marinate canned shrimp in cognac, then mix with cocktail sauce.

Toast sesame seeds in a black cast iron pan without added fat.

Slice lemons into 10 very thin slices.

PRESENTATION
Fill avocado halves with shrimp salad. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds. Garnish each with a lemon slice, black olive, and fresh dill sprig.
