- ADD SALTED WATER AND WORK RAPIDLY INTO A SMOOTH DOUGH. DO NOT KNEAD THE DOUGH TOO LONG SO AS NOT TO DEVELOP THE GLUTEN, WHICH MAKES THE DOUGH TOUGH.
- REFRIGERATE DOUGH FOR AT LEAST 1 HOUR BEFORE USE.
To make whole wheat dough, replace half of the cake flour with whole wheat flour.
SHORT PASTRY / PÂTE BRISÉE (0.4kg yield)

Add cold butter in small nuggets to flour. Rub butter and sifted flour together between fingertips into fine granules.

Add salted water and work rapidly into a smooth dough. Do not overwork the dough, as excessive kneading develops gluten and makes the pastry tough.

Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before use.

Variation: For whole wheat dough, replace half the white flour with whole wheat flour.
