Artichoke sous vide

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

- PLACE CLEANED ARTICHOKE BOTTOMS TIGHTLY, SIDE BY SIDE INTO VACUUM BAG - SEASON WITH SALT ROSEMARY AND THYME
- ADD BUTTER AND VACUUM PACK
- STEAM AT 91°C FOR APPROX. 40 MIN IN COMBI STEAMER
UNTIL ARTICHOKES ARE TENDER.
- COOL DOWN IN VACUUM BAG IN ICE WATER AS QUICK AS POSSIBLE
- WHEN 2°C REMOVE FROM ICE WATER
- LABEL AND YOU CAN STORE SOUS VIDE COOKED VEGETABLES AT LEAST 5-10 DAYS
- FOR REHEATING ARTICHOKES ADD VACUUM BAG INTO STEAM FOR 10 -15 MIN.
- READY FOR SERVING
- PLACE REMAINING LIQUID INTO SAUTEUSE AND REDUCE BRUSH VEGETABLE AND SERVE.
APPROX. COOKING TIME LIST FOR SOUS VIDE VEGETABLES AT 91-94°C
POTATOES NATURE 45 - 55 MIN CARROTS BATONNET 45 MIN FENNEL HALF 90 MIN. ASPARAGUS WHITE 50 MIN. ASPARAGUS GREEN 35 MIN.
CELERY ROOT 80 MIN.
BELGIAN ENDIVE 35 MIN,
Arrange cleaned artichoke bottoms tightly in a vacuum bag. Season with salt, rosemary, and thyme; add butter and vacuum seal. Cook in a combi steamer at 91°C for approximately 40 minutes until tender. Cool in ice water as quickly as possible to 2°C. Label and store sous vide for 5–10 days. To reheat, add vacuum bag to steam for 10–15 minutes. For service, reduce the remaining cooking liquid in a sautoir, brush vegetables, and serve. (Reference cooking times for other vegetables at 91–94°C: potatoes 45–55 min, carrots batonnet 45 min, fennel half 90 min, asparagus white 50 min, asparagus green 35 min, celery root 80 min, Belgian endive 35 min.)
