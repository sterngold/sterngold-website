Brownies

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

60 g couverture - black
125 g butter - melt and mix together - let cool down little
250 g suggar + 2 eggs - mix togther very well
60 g flour
Salt, 1 tl. Vanilla sugar
60 g crème fraiche
100 g walnoten - small pieces
Mix sugar/egg wit chocolate, add walnoten, add rest
160 C for 25 min, 130 C for 10 min
BROWNIES | VandeMarkt Pâtisserie

Melt 60 g dark couverture with 125 g butter together. Cool slightly.

Whisk 250 g sugar with 2 eggs until very well combined.

Fold chocolate-butter mixture into sugar-egg mixture.

Add 100 g walnuts (cut into small pieces).

Fold in 60 g flour, salt, and 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar. Fold in 60 g crème fraîche.

Bake at 160°C for 25 minutes, then reduce to 130°C for 10 minutes.
From VandeMarkt · Chef V. I. Sterngold
