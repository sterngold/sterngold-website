Brownies

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

60 g couverture - black
125 g butter - melt and mix together - let cool down little
250 g suggar + 2 eggs - mix togther very well
60 g flour
Salt, 1 tl. Vanilla sugar
60 g crème fraiche
100 g walnoten - small pieces
Mix sugar/egg wit chocolate, add walnoten, add rest
160 C for 25 min, 130 C for 10 min
From VandeMarkt · Chef V. I. Sterngold
