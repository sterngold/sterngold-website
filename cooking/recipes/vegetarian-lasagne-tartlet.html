Vegetarian Lasagne Tartlet

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Vegetarian Lasagne Tartlet
Vegetarisches Lasagnetarte/e to
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Entremetier/ side dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 03.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 1.75 / EUR 1.11

Ingredients for 4 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
320 g Noddle dough
120 g Zucchinis
120 g Eggplants
200 g Mushroom, fresh
48 g Parmesan cheese fine slices 40 g Butter
48 ml Olive oil
40 g White wheat flour
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper)
160 ml *Cream Sauce [with cream] 160 ml *Tomato Concassé
- PREPARE NOODLE DOUGH ( SEE RECIPE )
- CUT ZUCCHINI AND EGGPLANT INTO THIN SLICES AND SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER - DREGDE IN WHITE WHEAT FLOUR AND PAN FRY QUICKLY IN OLIVE OIL UNTIL GOLDEN. - PLACE SHEET PAN WITH PAPER AND COOL DOWN
- SAUTE SLICED CEPES OR OTHER SEASONAL MUSHROOMS IN OLIVE OIL AND SEASON TO TASTE. COOL DOWN
- PREPARE TOMATO CONCASSÉ AND CREAM SAUCE ( SEE RECIPES j - Lasagne tartelette preparation see course instruction
- BAKE AT 180°C FOR 10-30 MINUTES, DEFENSE ON SIZE OF TARTLET MOLD
