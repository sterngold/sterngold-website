SPICE OLIVE-PISTACHIO BISCOTTI
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 07.11.2005

Ingredients (5 servings):
125g white wheat flour
3g baking powder
3g salt
43g butter
8ml olive oil
13g dried tomatoes in oil, brunoise
38g black olives (canned), brunoise
25g pistachios
30g egg
25ml water

Method:
1. Knead all ingredients quickly to form a dough.
2. Form into two rolls of 25cm length and press into oval loaf shapes.
3. Bake at 180°C for approximately 20 minutes, then cool.
4. Slice into 1cm pieces and bake at 150°C until crispy.
5. Serve as Italian appetizer.

Cost: CHF 0.34 / EUR 0.21 per serving
