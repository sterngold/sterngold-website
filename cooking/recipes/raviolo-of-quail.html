RAVIOLO OF QUAIL
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 15.12.2005
Yield: 5 servings
Cost of goods: CHF 2.75 / EUR 1.74

Method:
Stew dried tomato concassé with olive oil and season to taste. Arrange spring onion circle on the plate. Place raviolo on top and position the two quail legs on top with concassé. Brush raviolo with olive oil. Drizzle sauce around the spring onion circle and serve garnished with young basil leaves.

Note: Good timing is important for successful result.
