- Combine millk, butter, salt, and nutmeg in small sauteuse and bring to a boil - Add semolina, stirring with a whisk.
- Cover and place into 120°C hot oven and cook for 10 minutes
- Remove from oven, mix in egg yolks and half of the grated cheese, using a plastic spatula. - Spread mixture. about 15 - 20 mm thick on oiled parchment paper;
and let cam.
- CUT CHILLED SEMOLINA PASTE INTO ATTRACTIVE SHAPES.
- THE TRADITIONAL SHAPES ARE HALF-MOONS AND CIRCLES.
- BUTTER AN OVENPROOF DISH (GRATIN DISH).
- PLACE GNOCCHIS ON BLACK BAKING TRAY
- SPRINKLE WITH REMAINING CHEESE AND BAKE IN HOT OVEN AT 220° C UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN.
Note: Semolina must cook the full time. The semolina must completely absorb all moisture, or the gnocchis will not hold together during baking.
