Pâtisserie Bread

Brioche

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

1 kg flour - 0,500 g
1.25 dl milk -0,62 dl
1.25 dl water - 0,62 dl
125 g egg - 1 egg
75 gist - 40 g always ut more gist (if its old)
100 sugar - 50 g
17 g zout - 8,5
250 g botter - 125 g(soft)
Briochebrod - 30 g piece in balck form; 180 C 14 - 16 min preheated
Brioche

Mix 500 g flour with 0.62 dl milk, 0.62 dl water, 1 egg, 40 g yeast (increase if old), and 50 g sugar. Add 8.5 g salt and 125 g soft butter, mixing until fully incorporated. Let dough develop.

Portion 30 g pieces into black molds. Proof until risen. Bake at 180°C for 14–16 minutes in a preheated oven.
From VandeMarkt · Chef V. I. Sterngold
