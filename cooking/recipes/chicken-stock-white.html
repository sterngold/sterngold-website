Chicken stock, white

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

1.2000000476837158 kg Chicken necks and backs (rinse (DEGORGER) for 30 min with some salt and drain)
2.4000000953674316 kg liter Water
10 g Salt
200 g Vegetable bundle for white stock
1 pcs piece Sachet bag (consisting of bay leaves, cloves, peppercorns and thyme)

Production

- add cold water to carcasse and bring to a slow boil
- add slat and skim frequently
- simmer for one hour
- add sachet bag and bouquet garni after 1/2 hour
- strain carefully through double layer of cheesecloth or china cap
Add cold water to carcass and bring to a slow boil. Add salt and skim frequently. Simmer for one hour. Add sachet bag and bouquet garni after 30 minutes. Strain carefully through a double layer of cheesecloth or china cap.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
