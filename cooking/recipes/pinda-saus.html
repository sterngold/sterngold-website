Limoen blad - handje
Limoen - 2
Peanut Sauce

Ingredients:
- Peanut butter: 2 kg
- Milk: 3 L
- Sambal: 2 tablespoons
- Sweet soy sauce: 80 ml
- Cinnamon: 20 g
- Lime leaves: handful
- Lime: 2

Method:
Whisk peanut butter with milk to combine smoothly. Season with sambal, sweet soy sauce, and cinnamon. Add lime leaves. Finish with lime juice to taste. Adjust consistency as needed for service.
From Tabac · Chef V. I. Sterngold
