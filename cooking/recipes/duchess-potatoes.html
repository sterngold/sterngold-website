**Duchess Potatoes** | Chef P. Diethelm | August 4, 2005 | Cost: CHF 0.20

**Mise en place:**
900 g potatoes, 60 g egg yolk, 30 g butter, salt and nutmeg for seasoning.

**Method:**
Wash, peel, and cut potatoes into even-sized chunks. Steam until tender and drain thoroughly. Pass through a food mill to create a smooth purée.

Butter a baking tray. Blend the puréed potatoes with egg yolks and butter. Season to taste with salt and nutmeg.

Using a pastry bag fitted with a star nozzle, pipe rosettes or nest shapes onto the prepared sheet pan. Transfer to the freezer and chill quickly.

Mix egg yolks with a little cream to create an egg wash. Brush the chilled potato shapes with this mixture.

Bake at 200°C until golden brown. Serve immediately.
