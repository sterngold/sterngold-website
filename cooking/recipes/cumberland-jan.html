Combine red wine, red wine vinegar, jam, mustard, sugar, salt, and pepper. Add star anise, raisins, cloves, and juniper berries (contained in a tea infuser ball). Prepare orange and lemon zest by peeling thinly with a channel knife, blanch in water for 5–6 minutes, then add to sauce along with a bay leaf. Thicken sauce slowly with cornstarch dissolved in water, stirring constantly until desired consistency is reached.
