Production

Venison Medallions Mirza
Sautierte Rehnüsschen Mirza Noisettes de chevreuil sautes Mirza

Recipe # : 478
Category : Game Dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 07.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 5.59 / EUR 3.54

European Gourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 2 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
300 g Venison meat Boneless, trimmed Cut into small escalopes, 50 g each.
2 g Salt
1 x Seasoning (black pepper)
20 g Butter Clarified 40 ml Italian red wine
100 g Fond de veau brun lié
50 ml Heavy cream (36%)
80 mg Cayenne pepper, ground
2 ml Lemon juice Garnish :
140 g Apple
8 g Butter
8 g Crystal sugar
For Garnish
- Peel apples, cut into small wedges
à la minute
- Melt butter add apples sprinkle with sugar and saute until golden.
- Keep warm
- SAUTÉ QUICKLY IN CLARIFIED BUTTER, TO MEDIUM RARE.
- SEASON VENISON MEDALLIONS WITH SALT AND WHITE PEPPER
- REMOVE ON A WIRE RACK AND KEEP WARM.
- POUR OFF EXCESS BUTTER. ADD RED WINE AND REDUCE.
- ADD FOND AND BOIL THOROUGHLY.
- STIR IN HEAVY CREAM. SEASON WITH CAYENNE AND LEMON JUICE.
- ARRANGE MEDALLIONS ADD SAUCE AND SERVE ON SAUTEED CARAMELIZED APPLES
- Garnish with sprig of herbs and sweet and sour cranberries
DCT - Swiss Culinary School - Vitznau / Luzern CALCMENU
