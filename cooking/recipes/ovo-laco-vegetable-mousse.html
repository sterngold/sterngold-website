OVO-LACTO VEGETABLE MOUSSE
Category: FPP - Vegetarian Cooking | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 10.05.2005

Ingredients (6 servings):
- 150 g vegetable purée
- 3 g salt
- Pepper to taste
- 30 g egg yolk
- 80 ml heavy cream

Method:
1. Steam vegetables until tender; purée finely.
2. Reduce purée over heat, stirring constantly to remove excess moisture.
3. Fold in egg yolk; whisk and season with salt and pepper.
4. Fold in whipped cream.
5. Chill before service.
