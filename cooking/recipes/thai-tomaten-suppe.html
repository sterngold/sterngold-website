THAI TOMATO SOUP

Sweat 1 head white onion (finely diced) in a pot. Add fresh ginger (finely diced), 10 lime leaves (finely sliced), coriander seeds (crushed), garlic paste, and sambal oelek paste. Season with salt and pepper. Add tomato purée and simmer 40–45 minutes.

Add 10 stalks lemongrass (split lengthwise) and 1 can coconut milk. Continue simmering 30 minutes. Remove lemongrass stalks. Finish with 2 tablespoons vegetable stock. Pass through fine sieve. Adjust consistency with water as needed.
From Franken · Chef V. I. Sterngold
