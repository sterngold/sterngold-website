Garde Manger Cold Appetizer

Cauliflower panacotta

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen

Ingredients

200 g Onions
2 g Cauliflower
5 g Wine, white, dry
2 g Milk
2 g Cream
8 pcs to 10 Gelatine leaves

Production

onions sweet
cauliflower – add
white wine - abloschen
milk
cream – add ½ / aufkochen / abschmeken / add restliche miclh und rahm
blat gelatin
Passieren

Notes

Or any kind of panacotta Per liter 4 to 6 gelatin
