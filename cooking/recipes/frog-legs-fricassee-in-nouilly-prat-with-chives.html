Clean and dry 1200 g frog legs. Season with nutmeg, lemon juice, and freshly milled pepper; marinate 30 minutes.

Salt the legs, dust lightly with flour, and sauté in 50 g clarified butter until light golden. Keep warm.

In the same pan, add 200 g emincéed button mushrooms and 50 g chopped shallots. Deglaze with 100 g Nouilly Prat and 250 g white veal stock; bring to a boil.

Stir in 200 g heavy cream and incorporate into the sauce. Adjust consistency with 20 g white roux if needed, then pass through a china cap. Check seasoning.

Return frog legs to the sauce. Temper the liaison (1/3 egg yolk + 2/3 cream) and add to the pan, keeping it hot without boiling. Finish with 30 g chives cut into 1 cm slivers and serve immediately.
