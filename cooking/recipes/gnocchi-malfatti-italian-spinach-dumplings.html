GNOCCHI MALFATTI ITALIAN SPINACH DUMPLINGS

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

GNOCCHI MALFATTI ( ITALIAN SPINACH DUMPLINGS I
Yield: 5 servings
200 g fresh spinach or 70 g frozen blanched
30 g fine chopped shallots
1 clove of garlic, fine crushed
20 g unsalted butter
75 g ricotta cheese
50 g past. eggs
70 g white wheat flour
15 g buck wheat flour
60 g freshly grated parmesan salt, pepper mill, nutmeg
2 g chopped basil and marjoram
Garnish:
25 g butter
15 g chopped basil
freshly grated parmesan  150 g tomato coulis
Preparation:
Wash and drain the spinach carefully in cold deep water for several times. Remove the stalks of the spinach. Blanche the leaves very quickly and refresh. Squeeze out all the water. Chop the spinach very fine or blend in food processor with the eggs
Sweat the chopped shallots and garlic in butter for about 1-2 min. and cool down. Now mix all the ingredients for the dumplings.
Season the dough with salt, pepper and nutmeg. Let the dough rest for about 20 min. Scoop out dumplings from the dough like quenelles shape.
Simmer the gnocchi in a large amount of lightly salted boiling water. As soon as they float to the top, simmer them for 2 - 4 min. Remove and drain the cooked gnocchis carefully. Sweat fresh roughly chopped sage in hazelnut butter and pour over the hot dumplings and arrange on plate or serving dish.
Serve on tomato coulis.
Sprinkle with fresh parmesan cheese, grated or fine sliced.
