PARMENTIER POTATOES
Source: DCT | Station: Entremétier
Yield: 6 servings (720 g net)

Ingredients:
- 720 g potatoes
- 12 g salt
- 60 ml peanut oil
- 30 g butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

Method:
Wash and peel potatoes. Cut into even dice approximately 10 mm thick. Blanch in salted water until tender. Drain thoroughly and spread on a sheet pan to cool quickly and arrest cooking.

Heat peanut oil in a roasting pan and add potatoes. Begin roasting on the stovetop, then transfer to a hot oven at 220°C. Roast, turning frequently, until golden brown. Season with salt and pepper.

Drain off oil and replace with butter. Roast briefly, turning potatoes to coat evenly with the butter.
