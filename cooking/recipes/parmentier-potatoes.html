Parmentier Potatoes

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Zutaten für 6 720 g


12 g
60 ml
1 x 30 g
Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge) Potatoes


Salt
Peanut oil
Seasoning (salt and pepper) Butter
Wash and peel. Cut into, even parmentier dice, about 10 mm thick. Blanch in salted water. Drain and spread out to cool quickly and to prevent further cooking.

- HEAT PEANUT OIL IN ROASTING PAN, ADD POTATOES
- START ROASTING ON TOP OF STOVE AND CONTINUE IN A HOT OVEN AT 220°C. - ROAST POTATOES, TURNING FREQUENTLY, UNTIL THEY ARE GOLDEN BROWN. AND SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER.
- POUR OFF OIL, REPLACE WITH BUTTER, AND ROAST POTATOES BRIEFLY, TURNING TO COAT EVENLY.
