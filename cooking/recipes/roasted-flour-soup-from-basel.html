Roasted Flour soup from Basel
Basler Mehlsuppe
Potage bälois
Basler Fasnachtssuppe

Recipe # : 111
Category : ECA - Soups
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 20.10.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 2.07 / EUR 1.31


Ingredients for 2.500 liters at 100% (Net quantity)
100
g
Clarified butter

630
g
Onions
Peel, slice and chop.
200
g
Whole wheat flour
Roast flour in cast iron pan in slow oven until evenly
brown. Let flour cool.180 - 190 C
3
l
*Beef Broth

200
ml
Red cooking wine

1
x
Seasoning (salt)

100
g
Sbrinz Grated, or Parmesan


- SAUTE ONIONS IN BUTTER.
- DUST WITH THE ROASTED FLOUR AND MIX WELL UNTIL SOUP STARTS TO BOIL. - ADD HOT BOUILLON; STIR WELL UNTIL SOUP STARTS TO BOIL.
- ADD RED WINE, SKIM, AND SIMMER FOR AT LEAST 1 HOUR.
- STRAIN THROUGH FINE MESH CHINA CAP.
- SEASON TO TASTE.
- SERVE WITH GRATED CHEESE ON THE SIDE.
