Creation of Rabbit leg from central
Switzerland (Fortsetzung)
Hot Kitchen/Main course meat & sauce 110 Pax. Kreation vom Innerschweizer Kaninchenschenkel\

Rezept Nr. : Hot kitchen 1
Kategorie : Culinary Olympics, Erfurt 2004
Koch : Swiss Junior Team
Datum : 28.02.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 3.43 / EUR 2.17
PREPARATION:
1 Rabbit leg ballotine part 1
- Debone the remaining part of the rabbit leg
- Remove some bigger parts of meat, trim of fat and darker parts of meat
- The trimmed rabbit legs should be placed between two plastic foil to a rectangular piece and flatten - Place on prepared air dried ham and season with satt and pepper mill
- Place dry savoy cabbage leaves on top of rabbit meat, season with salt and chill
- Reserve removed meat and chill
1.1 Rabbit mousseline farce
- Grind chilled meat of rabbit with meat grinder
- Combine meat with chilled cream and salt
- Add into food processor and blend to fine, smooth fine farce - Remove from cutter and chill in bowl on ice
-Strain through drum sieve and place back on ice
- Add all remaining ingredients and season to taste - Fill into piping bag and keep chilled
1 Rabbit leg ballotine part 2
- Pipe farce evenly in center of prepared seasoned rectangular
- Carefull roll up tightly, tie with butchers twine and chill
- Heat up rotissoir add clarified butter and brown on all sides
- Place in oven and roast briefly in hot oven
- Lower heat and continue roasting
- Baste occasionally
- Check the internal meat temperatur
- When the desired degree of doneness is reached, remove meat to wire rack and keep warm - Carefully drain of all fat from rotissoir, add matignon and saute briefly
- Add wine and reduce completely
- Add brown veal stock and bring to a boil
- Skim, and strain sauce through fine mesh china cap
- Return stock to stove and reduce to desired consistency or add little brown roux
- Adjust seasoning
- Slice the roasted rabbit ballotine and brush slices with melted butter to give a nice sheen and arrange with some sauce
