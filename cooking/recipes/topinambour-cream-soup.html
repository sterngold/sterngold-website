Entremétier Soup

Topinambour cream soup

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 1

Ingredients

30 g Butter
25 g Onions (Cut into medium dice)
25 g Leeks (white leaves only. Wash and trim. Cut into medium dice)
5 g Knob Celery (celeriac) (Cut into matignon)
10 g Flour, white wheat
300 g Topinambour (greated with rosti grater)
600 g ml Chicken stock, white
150 g ml Heavy cream 36%
1 pcs x Salt
Chevil, fresh chopped

Production

- sweet leeks , onions and knob celery in butter
- add diced topinambour and continue cooking
- dust with flour and stir
- add stock and bring to boil, stirring
- boil for at least 10 min. skim occasionally
- stri in cream. Bring to a boil again. Season to taste
- mix with bar blender
- strain through china cap and chill
- for serving bring to a boil and foam up with pole blender

- garnish with freshly baked topinabour clavotis (extra recipe pauli) sprinkle with chervil
Sweat sweet leeks, onions, and knob celery in butter. Add diced topinambour and continue cooking. Dust with flour and stir to combine. Add stock and bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Boil for at least 10 minutes, skimming occasionally. Stir in cream and bring to a boil again. Season to taste. Blend with an immersion blender until smooth. Strain through a china cap and chill. For service, bring to a boil and aerate with an immersion blender to create foam. Garnish with freshly baked topinambour clavotis (see Pauli recipe) and sprinkle with chervil.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
