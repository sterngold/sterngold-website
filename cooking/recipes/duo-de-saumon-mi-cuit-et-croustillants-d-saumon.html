**Duo de Saumon mi-cuit et croustillants de Saumon** | Chef Patrick Diethelm | December 15, 2005 | Cost: CHF 1.91

**Part One: Poached Salmon**
Cut 300 g salmon trout fillet into long thick sticks. Marinate with 4 g sea salt and 3 g crystal sugar. Wrap tightly in plastic foil, then aluminum foil, shaping into a roll.

Poach in a 50°C oven for approximately 30 minutes. Chill, unwrap carefully, and slice. Coat with chopped fresh dill.

**Part Two: Crispy Brick-wrapped Salmon**
Cut 300 g salmon trout fillet into long sticks and season with 1 g smoking salt.

Brush feuilles de brick with melted clarified butter and wrap salmon tightly within. Sauté quickly in 40 g clarified butter until the brick dough is crispy. Slice carefully; the salmon should be medium-cooked.

**Service:**
Arrange both preparations on the plate. Serve with Granny Smith apple reduction and shallot confit.
