#1
- Cut Salmon in long thick sticks and marinate with salt and sugar - wrap in plastic foil and then tightly in alu foil and shape into roll
- Add into Ofen at 50°C for about 30 Minutes and then chill. - unwrap and slice carefully and dredge in chopped dill
#2
- Cut Salmon in long sticks and then season with smoking salt
- brush feuilles de brick with melted butter and then wrap the salmon in tightly. [See course Instruction)
- Saute quickly in clarified butter until brick dough crispy - Slice carefully (salmon is medium cooked)
- Serve with Granny smith reduction and shallot confit
