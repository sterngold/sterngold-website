BLACK TRUFFLE SABAYON

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

VITZNAU-lUCERNE SWITZERLAND
BLACK TRUFFLE SABAYON
Ingredients for 5 - 1 0 servings:
Sabavon:
Garnish:
30 g white veal stock
20 g truffle jus
50 g white wine
40 g egg
20 g butter melted
salt
3 g truffle brunoise

Garnish - lam de truffe or julienne de truffe

Sabayon:
- Add the white veal stock, truffle jus and white wine into a sauteuse
- Add the egg yolk and whisk until foamy on moderate heat.
- Season with salt, then pour in slowly the warm melted butter
- add truffle brunoise and serve.
