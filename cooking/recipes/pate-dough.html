Pate dough

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Раtê Dough ECA
Pastetenteig Pâte à pâté


Recipe #247
Category ECA - Dough
Chef Patrick Diethelm
Date28.11.2005
Cost of goods CHF 2.97 / EUR 1.88

Ingredients for 0.900 kilogram. at 100% (Net quantity)


504 g white wheat flour
174 g Butter fresh 140 g water
11 g Salt
60 g Egg Past
12 g Malt powdered
IN A PASTRY BOWL, COMBINE FLOUR AND BUTTER (RUB TOGETHER).
MAKE HOLE IN CENTER.
- DISSOLVE SALT AND MALT IN WATER.
- ADD EGG AND COLD WATER IN CENTER.
- COMBINE (KNEAD) QUICKLY INTO DOUGH.
- SHAPE INTO A RECTANGLE. COVER AND REFRIGERATE FOR 1 TO 2 HOURS.

Notes: The addition of malt will brown the dough evenly during baking.
All ingredients should be worked together rapidly; otherwise the dough can become crumbly, which is know in the trade as "burnt".
