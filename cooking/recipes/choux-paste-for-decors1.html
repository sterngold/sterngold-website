CHOUX PASTE FOR DECORS

Yield: 200g | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Recipe #678

INGREDIENTS
35ml pasteurized milk
35ml water
30g butter
1g salt
45g white wheat flour
80g eggs

METHOD
1. Combine milk, water, butter, and salt in a saucepan. Bring to a boil.
2. Remove from heat and add flour all at once, stirring vigorously with a plastic spatula until the mixture is smooth and no longer clings to the sides of the pot.
3. Cool briefly, then incorporate eggs in small increments, mixing until fully incorporated.
4. Use for appetizer décors.
