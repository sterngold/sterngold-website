Oranges oil with vanilla und cardamom o.300 l.

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen

Production

0.300 l oranges juice – passiert
8 g Cardamom kerns – 4 stuck - zerdrucken
1 st Garlic – scheiben schneiden
Reduzieren bis dickflussig ist
0.5 st vanilla geben
250 g Olive oil cold pressed montieren
Orange Oil with Vanilla and Cardamom (0.3 L)

Ingredients:
300 ml fresh orange juice, strained
8 g cardamom seeds (4 pods), crushed
1 garlic clove, sliced
½ vanilla pod
250 g cold-pressed olive oil

Method:
Combine strained orange juice with crushed cardamom and garlic slices. Reduce over heat until the mixture becomes thick and syrupy. Split the vanilla pod and scrape the seeds into the reduction; steep briefly. Remove from heat and cool slightly. Slowly mount the cold-pressed olive oil into the reduction, emulsifying gently. Strain through fine sieve if desired, and store in a clean bottle.
From Tschuggen · Chef Ulf Wein
