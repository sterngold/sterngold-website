Entremétier Soup

WONTON SOEP

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: Tabac

Production

per portie
Gemalen Gember - 1 thee lepel
Soyasaus - 1 thee lep.
Asperges - 2
Glasnoodels - 15 gr.
Spaanse peper - 1/4
Wontons - 3
Bosui
Selderie/Koreander

Kipboullion - 75 gr.
Ui - 1 kg
Knof - 50 gr.
Gember - 50 gr
Sereh - 4
Soepkip 1
Stock preparation: sweat sliced onions with ginger, garlic, and lemongrass. Add chicken bouillon and simmer. Strain.

Per portion: Bring stock to boil. Add ground ginger, soy sauce, and sliced asparagus. Add glass noodles and diced Spanish pepper. Place 3 wontons in bowl and pour over hot soup. Garnish with sliced scallion and cilantro or celery.

Stock ingredients:
- 1 kg onions
- 50 g ginger
- 50 g garlic
- 4 lemongrass stalks
- 1 chicken (for stock)

Notes

voor 10 lt.
