Hollandaise Sauce
Hollandische Sauce Sauce hollandaise
Rezept Nr. : 60
Kategorie : Sauces
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 16.08.2005
Warenkosten: CHF 13.-45/ EUR 8.51


Zutaten fOr 0.300 Liter zu 100% (Nettomenge)
15 g Shallots Chop shallots.
3 st white peppercorns Crush peppercorns.
5 ml White wine vinegar
90 ml Dry white wine
50 ml Water
2 9 Salt
100 g Egg yolk Pasteurized
350 g Butter Clarify butter – make buree noisete
1 x Seasoning (salt, cayenne pepper)
1 x Seasoning (lemon juice)
- BOIL VINEGAR, \IVINE. 1/3 OF THE WATER, SHALLOTS, PEPPERCORNS, AND SALT UNTIL ALMOST COMPLETELY REDUCED.
- ADD REMAINING WATER TO DILUTE REDUCTION.
- STRAIN THROUGH HAIR SIEVE
- ADD EGG YOLKS TO REDUCTION AND BEAT OVER 80·C WATER UNTIL THICK AND FOAMY.
- ADD FRESHLY CLARIFIED STRAINED BUITER IN A THIN STREAM, 'NHIPPING VIGOROUSLY.
- SEASON \IVITH SALT, CAYENNE, AND LEMON JUICE. .
- KEEP SAUCE IN COVERED CONTAINER IN A WARM PLACE. FOR SANITARY REASONS, THE
SAUCE SHOULD NOT BE KEPT LONGER THAN 1 HOUR.
Note: If the sauce curdles, beat together 1 egg yolk with little water over hot water, and beat in sauce in small amounts. As soon as the sauce starts to thicken, the remaining curdled sauce can be added in larger amounts.
