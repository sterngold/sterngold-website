Garde Manger Cold Appetizer

Zucchini custard

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Zucchini custard
Zucchetti flan
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Garde-manger special
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 07.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.64 / EUR 0.41

European Gourmet Cuisine

Ingredients for 6 servings at 100% (Net quantity)

6 g Butter
12 g Shallots
200 g Zucchinis Cut into emince or smaller diced
10 g White wheat flour
20 g Peccorino Grated
50 g Parmesan grated
70 ml Heavy cream [36%)
150 g Egg
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper)


- SWEAT THE SHALLOTS IN BUTTER
- ADD THE EMINCE FROM ZUCCHINIS AND SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER
- STEW ON LOW HEAT FOR 2-3 MINUTES - DUST WITH WHITE WHEAT FLOUR - REMOVE FROM HEAT
- BLEND IN THE FOOD PROCESSOR UNTIL FINE PUREE WITH THE CREAM, EGGS
AND GRATED PARMESAN
- ONLY STRAIN THROUGH HAIR SIEVE IF NOT FINE PUREE.
- SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER
- LINE OUT I EHRINE MOLD WITH PLASTIC FOIL
- FILL UP WITH ZUCCHINI CUSTARD AND POACH AT 90°C IN CONVECTION OVEN
- COOL DOWN AND SERVE COLD WITH LUKEWARM VEGETABLE VINAIGRETTE
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
