Stuffed Onions with vegetables
Gefüllte Zwiebeln Oignous farcies

Recipe # : 539
Category : Vegetables
Chef : P.Diethelm
Date : 19.10.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.51 / EUR 0.32

Ingredients for 6 servings at 100% [Net quantity)
300 g Onions 3 medium, same size BO ml *Beef Broth
20 g Sbrinz Grated
1 x Salt
40 g Butter
60 g Smoked bacon sliced
70 g Vegetables jardiniere
60 g Tomato concasse
4 g Parsley, Italian, fresh
50 g White bread [Pullman loaf)
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper]
1 g Marjoram fresh or basil
- Peel onions, cut off tops, and leave root end in tact. Blanch briefly in salted water or steam onions lightly.
- Carefully scoop out centers to form cups.
Be sure onions are held together at the root ends.
The cups should be at least 2 layers thick.
- Chop scooped out onion and reserve.
For Stuffing
- Cut bacon into small brunoise.
- Cut seasonal vegetables into small jardiniere
- Cut peeled seedless tomato into concasse
- Chop parsley and marjoram leaves.
- Remove crust from bread and slice into small croutons
Toast cubes under the salamander until golden brown.
- Sauté diced bacon in butter,add vegetables
- Add parsley, marjoram, croutons, and reserved chopped onions and concasse. - Season to taste with salt and pepper.
- ARRANGE ONION CUPS IN AN OVENPROOF DISH (GRATIN PLATTER).
- FILL ONION CUPS WITH STUFFING. POUR BOUILLON AROUND ONIONS, AND COVER WITH BUTTERED ALUMINUM FOIL.
- BAKE IN OVEN AT 165°C UNTIL ONIONS ARE BROWN.
- REMOVE FOIL AND SPRINKLE WITH CHEESE.
- BROWN IN HOT OVEN OR UNDER THE SALAMANDER.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
