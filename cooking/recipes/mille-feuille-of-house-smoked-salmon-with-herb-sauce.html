MILLE FEUILLE OF HOUSE SMOKED SALMON WITH HERB SAUCE
Vitznau-Lucerne, Switzerland | 6-10 servings

Ingredients:
300 g puff pastry
450 g smoked salmon, diced 2×2 mm
50 g sour cream
30 g fine chopped herbs
Fresh pepper, lemon juice, coriander

Garnish:
Herb sprigs, herb sauce

Method:
Bake puff pastry until golden and crisp. Layer with smoked salmon and sour cream mixture. Finish with chopped herbs, fresh pepper, and lemon juice. Garnish with herb sprigs and herb sauce.
