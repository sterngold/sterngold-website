Garde Manger Sauce cold

Cocktail sauce

Chef: P. Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 0

Ingredients

400 g ml Mayonnaise
200 g ml Ketchup, tomato
100 g ml Sour cream 35%
17 g Horseradish, fresh
17 g ml Cognac
1 pcs x Tabasco
1 pcs x Lemon juice

Production

- peel and grate horseradish
- combine all ingridients
- serve with tabasco and lemon juice
- add spices according to taste to give sauce a tangy flavour

Notes

- arrange the hot egg on the salad - spoon the sauce to cover the poached egg - garnish with fresh sprig of chervil
