CREAM SAUCE FEC

Yield: 500ml | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Recipe #40

INGREDIENTS
25g butter
25g onions
38g white wheat flour
650ml milk
100ml heavy cream (36%)
White pepper and salt, to taste
Pinch of nutmeg

METHOD
1. Cut onions into small dice and sweat in butter until translucent.
2. Add flour and cook briefly to form a roux.
3. Cool the roux slightly.
4. Bring milk to a boil and add to the roux, whisking to combine smoothly.
5. Simmer for approximately 20 minutes.
6. Add cream and bring to a boil again.
7. Season to taste with white pepper, salt, and nutmeg.
8. Strain through a fine chinois.
9. Cover with a lid or use immediately.
