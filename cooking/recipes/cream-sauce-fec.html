Production

Cream Sauce FEC
Cremesauce Sauce creme

Rezept Nr. : 40
Kategorie : Sauces
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 16.08.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 3.60 lEUR 2.27

Zutaten tar 0.500 Liter zu 100% (Nettomenge)
25 g Butter
25g Onions
38 g White wheat flour
650 ml Milk
100 ml Heavy cream (36%)
1 x Seasoning (white pepper, salt)
1 x Nutmeg (pinch)

- CUT ONIONS INTO SMAll DICE AND SWEAT IN BUITER.
- ADD 'FlOUR AND COOK BRIEFLY.
- COOL RESULTING ROUX.
- BRING MilK TO A BOILING POINT AND ADD TO ROUX.
- SIMMER FOR ABOUT 20 MINUTES.
- AQD CREAM\AND BRING TO A BOil AGAIN AND SEASON TO TASTE.
- STRAIN THROUGH FINE CHINA CAP.
- Cover with a lid or use immediately
