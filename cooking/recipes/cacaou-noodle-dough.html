Cacao noodle dough
Hausmachernudeln mit Kakao Nouilles maison au cacao
Recipe # : 631
Category : ECA - Entremetier/ side dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 01.12.2005
Cost of goods : CH F 0.43 / EUR 0.27

EUROPEAN CULINARY CENTER
Ingredients for 5 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
185g White wheat flour
50q Semolina flour, fine 50q Cacao powder
190q Egg yolk past.
15ml Grape seed oil
20g Water
METHOD BY HAND
Combine all ingredients
Knead mixture thoroughly, by hand or machine into a dense, smooth, elastic dough. - Let dough rest for at least 1/2 hour before use.
(Handmade dough can also be made by kneading the mixture, letting it rest briefly, and then rolling the dough a number of times through a pasta rolling machine.)
Notes: The purpose of kneading the dough thoroughly is to develop the gluten in the flour, which makes the dough elastic.
Noodle and ravioli doughs are not salted, because salt would make the dough brittle.
