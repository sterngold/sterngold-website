Delice de volaille homardine
Geflügeldelikatesse mit Hummer
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Terrine/Paté/Galantine / Mart
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 15.12.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 4.66 / EUR 2.95

Ingredients for 5 servings at 100% (Net quantity)

125 g Chicken breast, skinless
45 g Chicken mousseline farce
50 g Spinach, frozen, chopped
50 ml *Chicken Stock
75 g Vegetable batonet mixture
1 g Truffle, canned, drained
250 g Lobster Rock lobster



- Add dry fine chopped spinach into raw mousseline farce

- butterfly chicken breast and flatten between plastic foil. - season with salt and pepper and truffle brunoise. - spread farce on chicken breast.
- place rock lobster tail
- roll in tightly [see tours instruction)
- poach in chicken stock until 58°C
- chill in stock and slice cold.
