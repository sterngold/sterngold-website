**Delice de Volaille Homardine** | Chef Patrick Diethelm, 15.12.2005

Add dry fine-chopped spinach into raw mousseline farce. Butterfly 125 g chicken breast and flatten between plastic foil. Season with salt, pepper, and 1 g truffle brunoise. Spread 45 g farce on breast. Place rock lobster tail and roll tightly.

Poach in 50 ml chicken stock until internal temperature reaches 58°C. Chill in stock and slice cold.

**Service:**
Arrange sliced ballotine on top of cooked fine marinated vegetable batonet. Prepare whipped cream, fold in coral and lemon juice, season with salt and pepper, and form quenelle alongside.
