Blinis

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

VITZNAU-LUCERNE SWITZERLAND
BLINIS (BUCKWHEAT PANCAKES)
Yield: 8 servings
220 g milk
20 g fresh yeast
5 g honey
70 g buck wheat flour
70 g white wheat flour
30 g egg yolks
15 g melted butter
90 g egg whites salt
20 g clarified butter
Preparation:
- Dissolve the fresh yeast in lukewarm milk with the honey
- Stir in the flour and allow to ferment for 20 - 30 min.
- Mix with the melted butter and past. egg yolk
- Fold the stiff egg white into the batter
- Heat a non stick pan and bake the blinis with clarified butter on both sides until golden brown [see course instruction)
- Keep warm on kitchen paper and serve immediately
With a cold appetizer or caviar also as soup garnish
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
