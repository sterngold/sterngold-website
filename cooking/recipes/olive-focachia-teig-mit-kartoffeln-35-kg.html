Olive Focaccia Dough with Potatoes (3.5 kg)

Ingredients:
1 kg potatoes
1.5 kg type 400 wheat flour
1 L milk
70 g yeast
50 g salt
120 g olives, coarsely chopped

Method:
Cook potatoes in salted water until tender. Drain well and pass through a ricer while warm. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm milk and let it activate briefly. Combine flour, passed potatoes, and salt. Pour the milk-yeast mixture into the dry ingredients and mix until a cohesive dough forms. Fold in the coarsely chopped olives evenly throughout. Place in a lightly oiled container, cover, and allow to rise at room temperature until doubled in volume (approximately 1.5–2 hours). Dough is ready for shaping and final proof before baking.

Notes

180 – 200 C Mit Tomaten – 120 g Marinierte Tomaten 1750 g Teig auf 1/1 Blech Wie Pizataig
