Bramata Polenta with Mascarpone
Polenta mit Mascarpone Polenta aux mascarpone


Recipe # : 653
Category : Rice and Grain Dishes
Chef : P.Diethelm
Date : 03.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.40 / EUR 0.25

European Gourmet Cuisine'
Ingredients for 5 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
30 ml Olive oil
50 g Onions Peel and chop.
5 g Garlic Peel and chop. 125 ml Milk
500 m/ *Veal broth
1 pc Bay leaves
135 g Cornmeal, coarse Bramata
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper)
100 g Mascarpone

SWEAT ONIONS AND GARLIC IN OLIVE OIL WITHOUT GETTING COLOR. - ADD BROTH AND MILK AND BRING TO A BOIL.
- ADD CORNMEAL GRADUALLY, IN A THIN STREAM,
STIRRING CONTINUOUSLY WITH A STRONG WIRE WHISK.
- SIMMER FOR 5 MINUTES, STIRRING WITH A PLASTIC SPATULA.
- ADD BAY LEAF AND SALT AND PEPPER, COVER, AND COOK AT 120°C IN OVEN, FOR 75 MINUTES.
DO NOT STIR DURING THIS TIME.
- REMOVE BAY LEAF, STIR IN MASCARPONE AND SERVE
Peel and chop onions and garlic. Sweat onions and garlic in olive oil without coloring. Add veal broth and milk, bring to a boil. Add cornmeal gradually in a thin stream, stirring continuously with a strong wire whisk. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring with a plastic spatula. Add bay leaf, season with salt and pepper, cover, and cook at 120°C in oven for 75 minutes without stirring. Remove bay leaf, stir in mascarpone, and serve.

**Ingredients (5 servings):** 30 ml olive oil, 50 g onions, 5 g garlic, 125 ml milk, 500 ml veal broth, 1 bay leaf, 135 g bramata cornmeal, salt and pepper, 100 g mascarpone.
