Berry-Vanilla confit

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Berry - Vanilla confit
Beeren-Vanille Confit
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Sauces Garde-manger
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 14.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.43 / EUR 0.27

Ingredients for 5 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
20 g Crystal sugar 40 ml Water
0.5 pc Vanilla bean
14 ml Sherry vinegar  200. g Blueberries, fresh 10 ml Kirsch
2 g Cornstarch
1 x Nutmeg (pinch) 100 mg Cinnamon, ground
1 x Ceyenne pepper
- SPLIT THE VANILLA BEAN IN HALF AND SCRAPE OUT THE FLESH WITH PARING KNIFE - ADD SUGAR, WATER, VANILLA BEAN, SHERRY VINEGAR AND REDUCE TO 1/3.
- ADD BLUEBERRIES AND SIMMER ON LOW HEAT FOR 5 MIN. !
- MAKE SLURY WITH KIRSCH AND CORN STARCH AND ADD TO BERRIES
- Bring to a boil and add into bowl
- Season with nutmeg cinnamon and cayenne pepper. - Fresh Mint chiffonade can be added extra flavour. - Chill down and serve
