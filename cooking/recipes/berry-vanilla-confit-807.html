BERRY-VANILLA CONFIT

Source: DCT | Station: Garde Manger
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 14.11.2005
Yield: 5 servings

Ingredients:
20 g crystal sugar
40 ml water
0.5 vanilla bean
14 ml sherry vinegar
200 g fresh blueberries
10 ml kirsch
2 g cornstarch
1 pinch nutmeg
100 mg ground cinnamon
1 pinch cayenne pepper
Fresh mint chiffonade (optional)

Method:
1. Split vanilla bean lengthwise and scrape out flesh with paring knife.
2. Combine sugar, water, vanilla bean, and sherry vinegar in saucepan. Reduce to one-third.
3. Add blueberries and simmer on low heat for 5 minutes.
4. Make slurry with kirsch and cornstarch; add to berries.
5. Bring to a boil and transfer to bowl.
6. Season with nutmeg, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper. Add fresh mint chiffonade if desired.
7. Chill and serve.
