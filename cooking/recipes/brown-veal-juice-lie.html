Brown veal juice lie

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 1

Ingredients

2.0 kg liter Veal juice
30 g Brown roux
15 g Cornstarch
30 g ml Wine, white, dry
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
50 g Butter, cold

Production

- reduce veal stock to desired consistency
- use instant brown roux powder or mix cornstarch with wine and add to boiling stock to thicken
- bring to a boil
- simmer 15 minutes
- season to taste
- strain through fine china cap
- remove from heat and add cold butter in small nuggets

Notes

- use with roaast veal - use as ingredient in other brown sauces - brown roux - fro 1 liter - butter 20 g / flour 30 g
