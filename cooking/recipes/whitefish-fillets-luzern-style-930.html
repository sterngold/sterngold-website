Poissonnier

Whitefish Fillets Luzern. Style

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

- SALT FISH, DREDGE LIGHTLY IN FLOUR, AND SAUTÉ IN CLARIFIED BUTTER. KEEP FISH FILLETS WARM. - IN ANOTHER PAN , SAUTÉ CHOPPED ONIONS IN BUTTER, ADD TOMATOES AND CAPERS, AND COOK BRIEFLY.

- TOP FISH FILLETS WITH SAUTEED ONIONS, TOMATO CONCASSE, AND CAPERS.

#2

- PREPARE FRESH BEURRE NOISETTE ADD GLAZE DE VIANDE AND DRIZZLE HOT OVER DISH
- SPRINKLE WITH CHOPPED PARSLEY
WHITEFISH FILLETS LUZERN STYLE
Chef: DCT Poissonnier Station

METHOD
1. Salt fish fillets, dredge lightly in flour, and sauté in clarified butter. Keep warm.
2. In separate pan, sauté chopped onions in butter. Add tomatoes and capers, cook briefly.
3. Top fish fillets with sautéed onions, tomato concassé, and capers.
4. Prepare fresh beurre noisette, add glace de viande, and drizzle hot over dish.
5. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.
