Garde Manger Cold Appetizer

French poultry terrine

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

FRENCH POULTRY TERRIN
Yield: 15 servings
300 g Chicken meat
60 g Pork meat
60 g Fatback
20 g Port wine
20 g Madeira wine
1 Sprig of rosemary, thyme, bay leaf, clove


40 g Veal or chicken liver
10 g Shallots fine chopped
15g Butter
50 g Seasonal mushrooms or horn of plenty [5 x 5 mm]

100 g Sliced fatback in thin slices to line out terrine mold
30 g Pistachios
30 g Cooked vegetables 3 mm x 3 mm jardiniere [carrot, green/red bell pepper (skinless)
110 g Cream
8 g Rock Salt

Chicken breast, skinless, trimmed
Preparation
Marinate the chicken meat, pork meat, fresh fatback and liver
- Pour in the port wine, madere and all the herbs (whole stalks)
- Cover and marinate for 1 hour
- Remove the meat and drain in a wire sieve and reserve the marinade
Grind the meat mixture through a medium die of meat grinder.
- Place in a bowl on ice
- Sweat the mus rooms and fine chopped shallots quickly in butter and cool down
- Add the pistachios, blanched vegetables, and cold mushrooms to the meat and stir in carefully.
- Stir in the cold cream, season to taste and chill.
- pour in the remaining marinade
Line terrine mold first with plastic foil and then with thinly sliced fatback, reserve some slices for the top or use the modern way air dried ham
- Fill prepared terrine mold by half
Place the chicken breasts on top
Fill mold with the remaining farce, smooth out top
- Cover top with fatback and then with foil
- Place in convection oven at 75°C with steam.
Center temperature of 62° C - Remove terrine and cool down on wire rack in refrigerator
Place some weight on top and press terrine lightly over night.
- Take terrine out of the mold, wrap in fresh plastic foil and allow to rest for one day.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
