Saucier Sauce hot

Black truffle sabayon

Chef: P. Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 5

Ingredients

# Sabayon
30 g White veal stock
20 g Truffle jus
50 g Wine, white, dry
40 g Egg yolk
20 g Butter, melted
Salt
3 g Truffle brunoise

Production

Sabayon:
- add white veal stock, truffle jus and white wine into sauteuse
- add egg yolk and whisk until foamy on moderate heat
- season with salt, then pour in slowly the warm melted butter
- add truffle brunoise and serve

Garnish:
- lam de truffle or julliene de truffle

Notes

Servings - 5 to 10

From DCT · Chef P. Diethelm
