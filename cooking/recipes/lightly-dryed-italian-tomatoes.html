**LIGHTLY DRIED ITALIAN TOMATOES**

Yield: 5 servings | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 07.11.2005

**INGREDIENTS:**
- 500 g fresh ripe Italian tomatoes
- 50 ml olive oil
- Sea salt and pepper

**METHOD:**
- Peel tomatoes and cut into quarters.
- Remove all seeds.
- Place on parchment-lined sheet pan.
- Drizzle lightly with olive oil and season with sea salt and fresh cracked pepper.
- Dry in convection oven at 80°C until half-dry (1–2 hours with steam vent open), or overnight in regular oven with steam escape.
- Serve with mushroom terrine and drizzle with olive oil or tomato oil, or as Italian antipasti.
