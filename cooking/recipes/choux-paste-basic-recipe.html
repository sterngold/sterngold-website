CHOUX PASTE

Yield: 400g | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Recipe #677

INGREDIENTS
64g pasteurized milk
64g water
64g butter
8g crystal sugar
2g salt
78g white wheat flour
126g eggs, shelled

METHOD
1. Combine milk, water, butter, sugar, and salt in a saucepan. Bring to a boil.
2. Remove from heat and add flour all at once, stirring vigorously with a paddle until the mixture is smooth and no longer clings to the sides of the pot.
3. Cool briefly, then incorporate eggs in small increments, mixing until fully incorporated.
4. Pipe with star nozzle onto prepared parchment paper.
5. Bake at 180°C with steam escape open until golden brown.

NOTE: The paste should be smooth and silky for proper piping. Use for éclairs, profiteroles, and swan pastries.
