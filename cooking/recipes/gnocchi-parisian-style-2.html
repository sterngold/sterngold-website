Gnocchi Parisian style 2

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Gnocchi Parisian Style
Brandteignocken Gnocchi parisienne


Recipe ft : 627
Category : Pasta Dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 25.10.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.58 / EUR 0.37


Ingredients for 6 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
135 ml Milk
48 g Butter
3 g Salt
1 x Nutmeg (pinch)
140 g White wheat flour
150 g Egg Shelled & broken up
12 g Butter
150 ml *Cream Sauce [with cream)
60 g Sbrinz grated
36 g Butter melted
- Combine milk, butter, salt, and nutmeg and bring to a boil.
- Remove from heat and add sifted flour all at once.
- Return to heat and stir vigorously with a plastic spatula until the resulting stiff paste no longer clings to the bottom of the pot.
- Cool mixture slightly; stir in eggs in small increments.
- BRING AMPLE AMOUNT OF WATER TO SIMMER.
- PUT Pate choux IN PASTRY BAG WITH A LARGE, SMOOTH TIP. PIPE DUMPLINGS DIRECTLY INTO THE WATER 25- mm to make DUMPLINGS, OR USE TWO TABLE SPOONS
- REMOVE DUMPLINGS WITH SKIMMER AS SOON AS THEY FLOAT TO THE TOP, .AND PUT THEM INTO COLD WATER.
- WHEN ALL PASTE IS USED UP, LIFT DUMPLINGS FROM COLD WATER AND LEt THEM DRAIN OFF. - BUTTER A DEEP, OVENPROOF DISH (GRATIN DISH).
- BRING CREAM SAUCE TO A BOIL.
- SPOON A SMALL AMOUNT OF CREAM SAUCE INTO THE GRATIN DISH AND PLACE DUMPLINGS ON TOP. DO NOT CROWD DUMPLINGS; THEY WILL EXPAND DURING BAKING.
- COVER WITH REMAINING CREAM SAUCE AND SPRINKLE WITH CHEESE AND MELTED BUTTER. - BAKE FOR 10 -15 MINUTES, STARTING AT 180°C, AND FINISHING AT 220°C .
Note: The dumplings should be golden brown, and should have risen like a souffle. If not served at once, they will collapse.
