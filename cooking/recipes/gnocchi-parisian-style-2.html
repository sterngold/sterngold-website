GNOCCHI PARISIAN STYLE
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | 25.10.2005

INGREDIENTS (6 servings)
135 ml milk
48 g butter
3 g salt
Pinch nutmeg
140 g white wheat flour
150 g eggs (shelled and broken)
12 g butter
150 ml cream sauce
60 g Sbrinz, grated
36 g butter, melted

METHOD
Combine milk, butter, salt, and nutmeg; bring to a boil. Remove from heat and add sifted flour all at once. Return to heat and stir vigorously with a plastic spatula until the paste no longer clings to the pot bottom. Cool slightly, then stir in eggs in small increments.

Bring an ample pot of water to a simmer. Transfer pâte à choux to a pastry bag fitted with a large smooth tip and pipe 25 mm dumplings directly into the water, or use two tablespoons. Remove dumplings with a skimmer as soon as they float and transfer to cold water. Drain thoroughly.

Butter a deep ovenproof gratin dish. Bring cream sauce to a boil. Spoon a small amount of sauce into the dish, arrange dumplings without crowding (they expand during baking), and cover with remaining sauce. Sprinkle with cheese and melted butter.

Bake at 180°C for 10–15 minutes, finishing at 220°C until golden brown and risen like a soufflé. Serve immediately—they will collapse if held.
