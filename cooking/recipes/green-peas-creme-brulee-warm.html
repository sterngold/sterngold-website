Green peas creme brulee (warm)

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Green peas creme brulee (warm)
Caremelisierte Zuckererbsencreme (warm)
Recipe #
Category : Amuse Bouche
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 12.12.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.37 / EUR 0.23

European Gourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 8 servings at 100% (Net quantity)

#1 Pre-preparation:
Prepare light golden caramel sugar with crystal sugar add rosemary and pour on parchment paper and chill
Add small pieces into food prozessor until powdered.
Preparation:

Remove from heat, add egg and egg yolk
Strain and fill into prepared smaller special dishes.
Steam shortly before serving at 85°C with steam for about 20 minutes. Remove from oven and keep warm
Sprinkle rosemary sugar on of wârm green peas creme and caramelize with torch.
Serve with carved royal fillet of pepper salmon and few stewed vegetable julienne Decorate with sprig of herbs
