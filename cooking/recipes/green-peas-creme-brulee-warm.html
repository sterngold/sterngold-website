GREEN PEAS CRÈME BRÛLÉE (WARM)
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | 12.12.2005
Category: Amuse Bouche

PRE-PREPARATION
Prepare a light golden caramel with crystal sugar, add rosemary, and pour onto parchment paper to chill. Once set, process into powder.

PREPARATION
[Note: Original text is incomplete/damaged OCR. The following steps are reconstructed from context:]
Remove caramel from heat, add egg and egg yolk. Strain mixture and fill into prepared small serving dishes. Steam in combi at 85°C with steam for approximately 20 minutes. Remove and keep warm.

Sprinkle rosemary sugar over the warm green pea crème and caramelize with a culinary torch. Serve alongside carved royal fillet of pepper salmon and a small julienne of stewed vegetables. Garnish with herb sprigs.
