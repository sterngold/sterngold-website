Braised Red Cabbage Part 1
Geschmortes Rotkraut
Choux rouges braisé


Rezept Nr, : 543
Kategorie : ECA - Entremetier/ side dishes
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 27.01.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 0.49 / EUR 0.31

European Gourmet Cuisine
Zutaten für 5 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)
600 g Red cabbage
100 g Apple
200 ml Red cooking wine
70 g Onions
20 ml Raspberry vinegar
1 st Sachet bag
Preparation:
- Trim cabbage, cut into quarters, and remove core.
Cut cabbage into thin slices or on a meat slicer or with a mandolin. - Peel apples,core,and cut into thin slices.
- Peel and slice onions.
- Make sachet bag with bay leaf, cloves, juniper berries and peppercorns.
- Combine cabbage and apples and Marinate with all ingredients for 3 - 5 days
