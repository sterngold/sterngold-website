Quiche Lorraine

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Quiche Lorraine
Lothringer Kasekuchen Quiche lorraine


Rezept Nr. : 313
Kategorie : Cheese Dishes
Koch : P.Diethelm
Datum : 27.07.2005
Warenkosten :CHF 0.77 / EUR 0.49

EUROPEAN CULINARY CENTER

Zutaten film 8 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)
300 g *Short Pastry [sugarless] 130 g Smoked bacon
100 g Onions
200 g Gruyere cheese
16 g Butter 160 ml Milk
40 g Butter
80 ml Heavy cream (36%)
100 g Egg
16 g White wheat flour
1 x Seasoning (salt and pepper)
1 x Nutmeg (pinch)
- Butter a tart mold and dust with flour.
- Roll out short pastry and line mold.
- Blind baking for 10 Minutes at 180°C.
Peel onions and cut into julienne. Cut bacon into julienne. - Saute onions and bacon in butter and let cool.
- Grate cheese.
- Combine milk, cream, eggs, and flour. Strain through china cap. - Season with salt and pepper and nutmeg, and add cheese.
SPREAD ONION AND BACON MIXTURE EVENLY IN TART MOLD. - FILL MOLD WITH CUSTARD.
- BAKE AT 200°C FOR ABOUT 20-30 MINUTES WITH OPEN STEAM ESCAPE.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
