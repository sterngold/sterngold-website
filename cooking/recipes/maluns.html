Maluns
Long-fried Potatoes, Graubunden Style
Serves 6 - 1 0
Preparation: 1 day
1 kg Potatoes
300 g White wheat flour salt, Nutmeg
300 g Butter
Serve with Bundner alp cheese
PREPARATION
The day before, boil potatoes in their skins.
Peel while warm.
Grate half of the potatoes, thinly slice the other half. Mix salt with flour.
Mix in loosely with sliced and grated potatoes.
Heat 50 g butter in frying pan and add mixture.
Fry potatoes on medium to low flame while stirring constantly. Add butter from time to time.
Small, crispy potato "crumbs" will form in 30 - 45 minutes.
Add remaining butter and serve with large piece of Bundnerkase and apple sauce.
Serves 6–10 | Preparation: 1 day ahead

Day Before:
Boil 1 kg potatoes in their skins. Peel while warm.

Service Day:
Grate half the potatoes; thinly slice the remaining half. Mix salt and 300 g wheat flour together. Loosely combine flour mixture with sliced and grated potatoes.

Cooking:
Heat 50 g butter in frying pan, add potato mixture. Fry over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, 30–45 minutes until small crispy potato crumbles form. Add remaining 250 g butter gradually during cooking.

Finish and Serve:
Serve with large piece of Bündner Alp cheese and apple sauce.
