Tuna mayonnaise for Vitallo tonnato

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Mayonnaise

Mayonnaise-Sauce Sauce mayonnaise

Rezept Nr. : 71

Kategorie : Sauces

Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 01.03.2004

Warenkosten: CHF 5.63 / EUR 3.56


Zutaten fOr 0.500 Liter zu 100'1. (Nettomenge)

Egg yolk past. 100 g
Prepared mustard, mild 25 g
Sunflower oil 400 ml
White wine vinegar 15 ml
Seasoning (salt and pepper) 1 x

Seasoning (lemon juice) 1 x
Water 30 g

- COMBINE EGG YOLKS AND MUSTARD; WHISK UNTIL FOAMY.
- ADD OIL IN A FINE AND STEADY STREAM, WHILE WHIPPING VIGOROUSLY.
- ADD VINEGAR WHEN ALL OIL IS INCORPORATED.
- SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER AND LEMON JUICE.
