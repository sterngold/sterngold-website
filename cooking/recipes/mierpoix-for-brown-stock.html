Mierpoix for Brown stock

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 1

Ingredients

450 g Onions
400 g Carrot
175 g Knob Celery (celeriac)
30 g Parsley, steams, fresh
1 pcs piece Bay leaves
1 pcs piece Cloves
10 pcs piece Peppercorns, white
1 pcs piece Thyme, fresh
5 g Marjoram

Production

- peel onions
- wash and trim into coarse cubes
- wash parsley
- combine all ingredients
Peel onions and wash. Trim into coarse cubes. Wash parsley. Combine all ingredients.

Notes

the componenents and amount can vary according to taste and use. If desered for a particular recipe, chopped leeks and crushed garlic can be added after the vegetables have been browned

From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm · 25.03.2006
