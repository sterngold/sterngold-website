Pâtisserie Dessert

Baklava

Баклава

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: Toni

Production

1. Walnuts Plus lemon, vanilla and sugar
2. Fillo - put one and brush with butter, add Mie de pin
3. In the middle add the walnuts
4. Syrup - boil 15 min - plus lemon juice
5. Cold baklava - add syrup hot- put in warm place

400 - 600 gr. Fillo dough
Caneel
Mie de pain
lemon zest
3 tbl Sugar
600 gr walnuts
butter 500 g
Syrup - 600 gr. Water / 600 gr sugar
From Toni · Chef V. I. Sterngold
