Rabbit ballotine with prunes and almonds

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

RABBIT BALLOTINE WITH PRUNES AND ALMONDS
Yield: 4 - 6 servings 
1 fresh rabbit
50 ml olive oil
120 g dry prunes
1 dl marsala wine
40 g almonds toasted
250 g rabbit mousseline farce - leg shoulder
1 clove garlic
2 sprigs of rosemry
10 g croutons 2 x 2 mm (toasted under salamander) 20 g egg yolk
20 g parmesan cheese
salt, pepper
100 g mirepoix
150 ml white wine
200 ml brown veal stock

Preparation:
- Sweat the garlic (chopped) in olive oil, add the almonds
- Add fine chopped rosmary, cool down.
- Mix the rest o e ingredients and season to taste with salt and pepper - Stew the whole dry prunes with marsala wine and cool down.
- Bone out rabbit carefuly
- Remove the hind legs and fore legs
- debone the meat from the rib bones
- Put the stuffing on the loins (see course instruction) and roll up tightly . - Tie with butcher's twine
- Season with salt, pepper and brown lightly on all sides, remove from pan add the mirepoix, Tomato paste and keep roasting
- Deglaze with white wine and reduce, add the brown stock and bring to a boil place the rabbit back into the pan.
- Cover and glaze at 180° C, basting frequently
- When rabbit is well done, remove and keep warm
add extra brown stock to glaze and bring to a boil
Reduce sauce to desired consistency and season to taste (40ml per person) - Strain sauce through china cap
- Remove carefully the twine and slice meat.
- Arrange on a plate and pour some sauce around the meat


















RAW RABBIT MOUSSEUNE FARCE
Yield: 8 servings
130 g lean rabbit meat
10 g egg white
120 g cream
3 g salt
plus different added ingredients, depense on purpose of use
Preparation:
- Mince the lean rabbit meat very fine ( cool down)
- Blend in a food processor with cream, salt and egg white. - Place the bowl on ice and cool down
- Strain through drum sieve and keep on ice
- Allow to rest in the refrigerator and use for your purposes
