- MIX MUSTARDS WITH EGG YOLK AND SUGAR.
- ADD OIL IN THIN STREAM WHILE STIRRING VIGOROUSLY.
- REMOVE STEMS FROM DILL; CHOP LEAVES; AND ADD TO SAUCE.
- SEASON WITH HONEY, LEMON JUICE, SALT AND PEPPER
.- ADD WATER TO GET THE CONSISTENCY
APPLICATION
served with Gravad Lax.
