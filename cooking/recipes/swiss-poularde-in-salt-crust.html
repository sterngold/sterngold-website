Preparation
Mix coarse sea salt with flour, whole eggs and a little water roll out and chill.
Prepare one poularde
Season insides with salt and pepper, insert one twig of rosemary, one bay leaf.
Tie appropriately with string.
Unroll salt dough, line out the bottom of a cocotte. Place poularde on it, cover with remaining salt dough. Place a cover over to close.
Bake in a hot oven at 170 °C for about 50 – 60 minutes
Smash salt-crust, remove poularde. Carve and serve with prepared autumnal salad, artichokes and hazelnut oil vinaigrette
