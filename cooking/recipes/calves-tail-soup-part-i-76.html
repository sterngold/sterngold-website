Calves tail Soup Part I

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

800 g Clavestail or Oxtail
120 g Mirepoix for Brown stock
16 g Tomato paste
1600 g ml Wine, white, dry (Sylvaner or Riesling)
2.5 kg Water

Production

- roast calvestail pieces in oven until browned evenly at 200 C
- add mirepoix and continue roasting
- add tomato paste and continue roasting
- deglaze with wine and reduce
- put all above ingredients in a rondeau, add water or 1/2 brown veal stock
- simmer under the boiling point until calves are tender - about 2 to 3 hours
- remove calves tails
- strain stock through cheesecloth, save and let cool
- remove meat from tial, add to terrine mold , press and chill
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
