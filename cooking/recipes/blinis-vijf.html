Garde Manger Side dish 🇸🇪 Sweden

Blinis Vijf

Chef: Jan Wasch Source: Vijf

Ingredients

200 g Flour, white wheat
200 g Flour, rogge (dark weed ????)
20 g Yeast, fresh
200 g Milk
250 g Egg (or 6)
150 g Butter
125 g Egg whites
200 g Heavy cream 36%
1 pcs x Salt

Production

Bloem, rogge, eiern en zachte boter mengen. Melk licht verwarmen (37) gist toevoegen dit toevoegen aan mengsel en op warme plek laten rijzen.
Eiwitten opslaan, room met zout opslaan en beiden erdoor spateln
**Blinis Vijf**

**Method:**
1. Combine flour (wheat and rye), eggs, and soft butter.
2. Gently warm milk to 37°C. Add yeast to warm milk, then incorporate into flour mixture.
3. Allow to rise in a warm location.
4. Whip egg whites to stiff peaks. Whip cream with salt to soft peaks.
5. Fold both into batter.
6. Bake and serve immediately.
From Vijf · Chef Jan Wasch · 29.03.2006
