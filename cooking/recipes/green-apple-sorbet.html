Green apple sorbet

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Green Apple sorbet
Apfelsorbet
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Paco jet
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date :17.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.11 / EUR 0.07
Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% (Net quantity)

375 g Apples Granny smith
250 ml Apple juice
50 g Crystal sugar
30 g Glucose syrup
20 ml Lemon juice

- WASH APPLES, CUT INTO QUARTER REMOVE CORE, CUT EMINCE AND FILL INTO BEAKER - ADD THE LEMON JUICE AND MIX WELL
- BRING THE APPLE JUICE, SUGAR AND GLUCOSE TO A BOIL
- COOL DOWN
- ADD TO THE APPLES AND FREEZE at minus 20° C FOR 24 HOURS

METHOD PACOTISE:
- Pacotise twice will enhance the flavour and texture of the recipe
