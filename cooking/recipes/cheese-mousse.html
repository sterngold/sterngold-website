Cheese mousse

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

VITZNAU-LUCERNE SWITZERLAND
DALLENWILER GOAT CHEESE MOUSSE TERRINE WITH HERBS
Yield: 8 servings
150 g goat cheese from Dallenwil
50 g sour cream or mascarpone
fine chopped herbs as basil, parsley, chervil, chive
4 sheets gelatine
50 g chicken stock
90 g whipped cream salt, pepper mill
Garnish: Sprigs of herbs
Preparation:
- Press the goat cheese through a fine drum sieve
- Add the sour cream and half of the herbs in a bowl
Dissolve the soaked squeezed gelatine in the lukewarm chicken stock and stir in with a whisk
- Fold in the whipped cream carefully, season to taste with salt and pepper, fill in a prepared small terrine mold (prepared with plastic foil) and cover with the overhang foil - Place in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
- Unmold the terrine and remove the plastic.
Sprinkle and dredge the goat terrine bavarois in the remaining herbs
- Serve with a little rucola salad and a marinated dry tomato olive vinaigrette:

2 Marinated dried tomatoes
8 Black olives
10 g Chopped shallots
50 g Olive oil
20 g Balsamic vinegar Salt, pepper mill
10 g Chicken stock


- Cut all the tomato and black olives into small brunoise, add the fine chopped shallot, oil and vinegar
- Season to taste with salt and pepper
- Warm up the vinaigrette in a bain marie before serving to the sliced terrine - Garnish with some sprigs of herbs.
