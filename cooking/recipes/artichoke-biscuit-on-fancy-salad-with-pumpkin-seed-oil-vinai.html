LUKEWARM ARTICHOKE BISCUIT ON FANCY SALAD WITH PUMPKIN-SEED OIL VINAIGRETTE | Vitznau-Lucerne, Switzerland
Yield: 4 servings

4 cooked artichoke bottoms | 50 g butter | 100 g egg yolk | 120 g egg white | 50 ml cream | salt, pepper, nutmeg | 50 g white wheat flour | 50 g grated pecorino vecchio or parmesan | 5 g chervil

Clean, peel, and trim artichoke bottoms. Boil in salted water with lemon juice and white wine. Butter soufflé molds or cocettes and dust with flour, or use non-stick muffin molds. Using a machine, cream butter with egg yolks one by one. Pour in cream. Whip egg whites to soft peak with salt. Fold whipped whites, sifted flour, and cheese carefully into cream mixture. Slice cooked artichoke bottom and place in prepared mold. Pour batter in and bake at 190°C for 10–15 minutes à la minute. Check doneness with skewer. Unmold and slice. Arrange with seasonal leaf salads. Sauté pumpkin seeds in olive oil until they pop; add artichoke bottom salpicons and scatter over salad. Drizzle with vinaigrette. Garnish with chervil sprigs and parmesan slices.
