Artichoke biscuit on fancy salad with pumpkin seed oil vinai

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

VITZNAU-LUCERNE SWITZERLAND
LUKEWARM ARTICHOKE BISCUIT ON FANCY SALAD WITH PUMKIN-SEED OIL VINAIGRETTE

Yield: 4 servings

Biscuit:
4 cooked artichokes bottoms
50 g butter
100g egg yolk / 120g egg white
50 ml cream

salt, pepper, nutmeg
50 g white wheat flоwer
50 g grated pecorino vecchio (italian sheep-cheese) or parmesan
5 g chervil









Preparation:
- Clean and peel artichokes (see course instruction]
Trim artichoke bottoms and boil in salted water with little lemon juice and white wine
Butter souffé mold or cocotte and dust with flour or use non stick muffin mold

BISCUIT:
Use machine and foam up the butter with the egg yolks one by one
Pour in the cream
Whip egg whites to soft peak with a little salt
Fold the whipped egg whites with the sifted flour and grated cheese carefully into the cream mixture
Slice the cooked artichoke. bottom and place in the prepared mold
Pour batter into móld
bake approx. 30minutesbefore serving (a la minute)
Bake the biscuit at 190° C. for 10-15 min.
Check doneness with a skewer
Unmold the biscuit and arrange it sliced with seasonal leaf salades
Sauté the pumkin seeds with olive oil until they pop. Add artichoke bottom salpicons,
and sprinkle all this over the salad
Drizzle with the vinaigrette
Sprigs of chervil and parmesan slices
