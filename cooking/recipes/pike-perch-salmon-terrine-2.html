MISE EN PLACE
Line terrine mold with plastic foil, then with blanched carrot slices. Skin pike perch fillets, dice, and refrigerate. Blanch spinach leaves and dry on absorbent paper. Cut salmon trout into long strips. Slice carrot thin on mandoline, blanch in salted water, and refresh. Line carrot slices in terrine mold.

MOUSSELINE
Blend diced pike perch with 20g egg white, heavy cream, and salt until smooth. Pass through drum sieve. Place in bowl over ice and season to taste. Fold in chopped dill and sautéed horn of plenty.

ASSEMBLY
Fill terrine mold with approximately one-third of the fish mousseline. Wrap salmon strips in blanched spinach leaves and arrange down the center of the terrine. Fill remaining space with mousse, ensuring no air bubbles. Cover with overlapping carrot slices and plastic foil.

COOKING AND FINISH
Poach in convection oven at 75°C until center reaches 55-58°C. Weigh down slightly and cool in mold before turning out.
