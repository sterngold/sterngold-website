Onion Sauce

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 1

Ingredients

405 g Onions
80 g Butter
100 g ml Wine, white, dry
50 g ml Vinegar, white wine
800 g Fond de veau brun lie
10 g Parsley, fresh, chopped
1 pcs Salt

Production

- onions peel cut in half and slice emince
- saute onions in butter until about three qurters cooked
- add wine and vineagr and boil until almost evaporated
- add brown veal stock and simmer for 5 minutes
- season to taste and add parsley
Onion Sauce

1. Peel onions, cut in half, and slice into fine emincé.
2. Sauté onions in butter until approximately three-quarters cooked.
3. Deglaze with wine and vinegar; reduce until almost completely evaporated.
4. Add brown veal stock and simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Season to taste and finish with fresh parsley.

Notes

APPLICATION - serve with grilled sausages (bratwurst) or boiled beef gratine (miroton de boeuf)
