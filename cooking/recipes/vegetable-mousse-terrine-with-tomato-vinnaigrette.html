VEGETABLE MOUSSE TERRINE WITH TOMATO VINAIGRETTE | Chef P. Diethelm | DCT, Garde Manger Station | Yield: 20 servings

Ingredients:
620 g carrots
620 g broccoli
620 g knob celery
180 g vegetable stock
27 sheets gelatine
240 g cream
7 g whipped cream
Salt and pinch of sugar per mousse

Tomato Vinaigrette:
200 g tomato concassé
100 g olive oil
50 g sherry vinegar
10 g shallots, finely chopped
2 g chopped basil

Garnish:
Chervil and basil sprigs, basil oil
Chopped chives

Method:
1. Wash, trim vegetables, cut into even pieces. Boil in vegetable stock until tender. Drain in hair sieve.
2. Blend each vegetable separately with 60 ml liquid cream in food processor, strain through drum sieve. Yield should be 420 g purée per vegetable.
3. Soak gelatine in cold water (9 sheets per mousse). Dissolve with warm vegetable stock, stir into carrot purée.
4. Season to taste with salt and fold in 80 ml whipped cream.
5. Line terrine mold with blanched zucchini or carrot slices. Fill to one-third with carrot mousse, refrigerate until firm.
6. Layer celery mousse to two-thirds, refrigerate until firm.
7. Top with broccoli mousse, cover with plastic, chill thoroughly.
8. Unmold, remove plastic, slice carefully into tranches. Arrange on plate with vinaigrette and herb garnish.
