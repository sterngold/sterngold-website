Preparation:
Wash and trim the vegetables, cut into even sized pieces
Boil in vegetable stock until soft and tender
Drain in a hair sieve and blend each vegetable with the 60 ml liquid cream in food processor or blender, and strain through drum sieve.
You should have now 420 g puree of each vegetable

Soak the gelatine in cold water only 9 pieces per mousse
Dissolve with a tsp. warm vegetable stock and stir in one of the puree (carrot)
Season to taste with salt and also fold 80 ml whipped cream into puree.

Fill into prepared terrine mold lined out with blanched Zucchetti or carrot to one third
Let it cool down in the refrigerator until firm.
Now start with the second layer (celery) and fill to second part of the mold and refrigerate
Then start with the third layer (broccoli) and fill up the terrine, cover
with the overhanging plastic foil,
chill and keep in refrigerator
Unmold the vegetable terrine, remove the plastic foil and cut carefully into tranches
- Arrange on a plate with the vinaigrette and garnish with sprigs of herbs.
Р. Diethelm 05 - EGc
