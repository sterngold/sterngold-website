Tomato mousse

Cost of goods : CHF 0.42 / EUR 0.27

European Gourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% (Net quantity)

150 g Concassé of tomatoes big chunks
100 ml Tomato Ketchup
40 ml Tomato juice
60 g Tomato paste
5 pc Gelatin, leaves
7 g Sea salt
15 ml Balsamic vinegar 340 mI Heavy cream (36%)
3 pc Basil leaves, fresh
40 ml *Chicken broth Strong or
consomme
60 g Concassé of tomatoes 3x3mm



- BLEND THE FIRST TOMATO CONCASSE, PASTE, JUICE AND KETCHUP IN BAR BLENDER - SOAK THE GELATIN IN COLD WATER
- MIX THE VINEGAR AND CHICKEN BROTH AND BRING TO A BOIL WITH THE SALT - DISSOLVE GELATIN IN WARM MIXTURE
- STIR INTO TOMATO MIX
- USE WHISK AND FOLD 1/4 OF WHIPPED CREAM INTO COOL MIXTURE
- SEASON TO TASTE WITH SALT AND PEPPER, TABASCO
- FOLD IN REMAINING CREAM CHOPPED BASIL LEAVES AND SECOND TOMATO CONCASSE - FILL INTO BOWLS AND LET REST FOR AT LEAST 1 HOUR.
- FORM QUENELLES AND DRIZZLE WITH HERB OIL OR CHILI OIL
