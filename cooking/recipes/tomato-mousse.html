TOMATO MOUSSE

Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 07.11.2005
Yield: 10 servings

Ingredients:
150 g tomato concassé (large chunks)
100 ml tomato ketchup
40 ml tomato juice
60 g tomato paste
5 gelatine leaves
7 g sea salt
15 ml balsamic vinegar
340 ml heavy cream (36%)
3 fresh basil leaves
40 ml chicken broth or strong consommé
60 g tomato concassé (3 × 3 mm)
Salt, pepper, Tabasco to taste

Method:
Blend first tomato concassé, paste, juice, and ketchup in bar blender. Soak gelatine in cold water. Heat vinegar and chicken broth together, bring to boil with salt. Dissolve soaked gelatine in warm mixture. Stir into tomato mix. Whip cream until light, then fold one quarter into cooled tomato mixture. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and Tabasco. Fold in remaining whipped cream, chopped basil leaves, and fine tomato concassé. Fill into bowls and rest for at least 1 hour. Form quenelles and drizzle with herb oil or chili oil.
