Cream Puffs

Chef: Urs Meichtry Source: DCT

Production

Cream Puffs
Windbeutel
Choux à la crème


Rezept Nr. 784
Kategorie : Desserts
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 28.07.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 0.46 / EUR 029

EUROPEAN CULINARY CENTER

Zutaten für 10 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)

400 g *Puff Paste [basic sweet recipe)
10 g Butter
10 g White wheat flour
250 m/ *Vanilla Mousseline Cream 250 ml Heavy cream (36%) Whipped
20 g Powdered sugar


- Butter a baking sheet pan and dust with flour.
- WITH PASTRY BAG, DRESS 20 g ROSETTES, USING MEDIUM-SIZE STAR TUBE. - BAKE AT 180 - 200°C), WITH OPEN STEAM ESCAPE UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN. - COOL AND SPLIT PASTRY PUFFS IN HALF HORIZONTALLY.
- FILL BOTTOM HALVES WITH VANILLA MOUSSELINE CREAM.
- WITH A PASTRY BAG, DRESS WHIPPED CREAM ON TOP OF THE MOUSSELINE. - REPLACE TOP LAYER AND DUST WITH POWDERED SUGAR.
VARIATIONS
Eclairs with Chocolate Cream: Dress puff paste into 100 mm long tubes.
Flavor vanilla mousseline cream with couverture. Brush puffs with apricot glaze and cover with chocolate fondant.
Eclairs with Kirsch Cream: Dress puff paste into 100 mm long tubes. Flavor vanilla mousseline cream with kirsch. Cover puffs with pink fondant.
Small Cream Puffs (Profiteroles) with Chocolate Sauce: Make small cream puffs.Instead of cutting them in half, make a small opening in the bottom and fill them with whipped cream, using a pastry bag. Serve on top of chocolate sauce.
Butter a baking sheet and dust with flour. Using a pastry bag fitted with a medium star tube, pipe 20 g rosettes of puff paste. Bake at 180–200°C with steam vents open until golden brown. Cool and split puffs horizontally. Fill bottom halves with vanilla mousseline cream. Pipe whipped cream on top using a pastry bag. Replace top layer and dust with powdered sugar.

Variations: For éclairs with chocolate cream, pipe 100 mm tubes, flavor mousseline with couverture, brush with apricot glaze, and cover with chocolate fondant. For kirsch cream éclairs, flavor mousseline with kirsch and cover with pink fondant. For profiteroles, make small puffs, pierce the bottom, fill with whipped cream using a pastry bag, and serve over chocolate sauce.
