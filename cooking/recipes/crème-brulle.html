Crème brulle

Chef: V. I. Sterngold

Production

1.5 l cream
150 ml milk
170 g sugar
400 g egg yolk
1.5 vanilla
salt
Boil slowly cream with vanilla and salt
Dissolve sugar in milk add to cream
Put trough hair sieve
Boil again - add egg yolks
Leave for 30 min till foam is gone
Add to Bain Marie
In oven 120 C for - 20 - 25 min
Add lot's of sugar burn
Repeat the burning one more time
· Chef V. I. Sterngold
