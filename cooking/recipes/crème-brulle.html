Crème brulle

Chef: V. I. Sterngold

Production

1.5 l cream
150 ml milk
170 g sugar
400 g egg yolk
1.5 vanilla
salt
Boil slowly cream with vanilla and salt
Dissolve sugar in milk add to cream
Put trough hair sieve
Boil again - add egg yolks
Leave for 30 min till foam is gone
Add to Bain Marie
In oven 120 C for - 20 - 25 min
Add lot's of sugar burn
Repeat the burning one more time
Heat 1.5l cream slowly with 1.5 vanilla pods and salt. Dissolve 170g sugar in 150ml milk and add to cream. Pass through fine sieve. Return to heat and bring to boil. Remove from heat and temper in 400g egg yolk, whisking constantly. Let stand 30 minutes until foam subsides.

Pour through sieve again into baking vessels set in a bain-marie. Bake at 120°C for 20–25 minutes until just set. Cool completely. Before service, coat generously with sugar and caramelize with torch. Repeat caramelizing once more for double crust.
· Chef V. I. Sterngold
