RACK OF LAMB WITH OLIVE CRUST AND PESTO (DCT | Saucier)
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 13.12.2005
Yield: 3 servings

MISE EN PLACE:
Prepare tapenade and pesto (see separate recipes). Clean, trim, and cut vegetables into modern shape. Blanch and refresh. Season and prepare for glazing à la minute.

PREPARATION:
Roast rack of lamb (500 g) and let rest. Spread tapenade on top and sprinkle with breadcrumbs. Gratinate under salamander.

Glaze blanched vegetables (baby carrots 150 g, green beans 100 g, celeriac 60 g, snow peas 60 g, rosemary 15 g) à la minute in sauteuse with 40 ml olive oil.

Plate lamb on glazed vegetables with suitable starch. Arrange with strong red wine sauce and pesto.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
