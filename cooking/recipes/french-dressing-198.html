French Dressing

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 3

Ingredients

#1
15 g Salt
1 g Pepper ground
375 g Egg past.
30 g Dijon mustard
1 g Beef broth powder
8 g Garlic
# 2
1.75 kg liter Oil, sunflower
500 g ml Vinegar, herbs
175 g ml Water

Production

1#
- add all the ingridients into mixing bowl and stir until creamy
#2
- add all ingridients into bar blender and mix with #1 until creamy white

- season to taste
- strain throufh china cap and fill into bottles
Part 1: Add all ingredients to mixing bowl and stir until creamy.

Part 2: Add all ingredients to bar blender and mix with Part 1 until creamy white.

Season to taste. Strain through china cap and fill into bottles.

Notes

Label with date and name (expire date + 10 days)
