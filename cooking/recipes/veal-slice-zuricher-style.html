VEAL SLICE ZURICH STYLE (Kalbsgeschnetzeltes Zurcher Art) | Chef Patrick Diethelm | DCT, European Culinary Center | Yield: 3 servings

Ingredients:
20 g clarified butter
250 g veal top round, sliced thin (emincé)
20 g shallots, finely chopped
120 g button mushrooms
60 g white wine
100 g brown veal stock, thickened
100 g white instant roux (as needed)
80 g whipped cream
Salt and pepper mill
Fresh chopped parsley and lemon juice
Additional butter for sautéing

Method:
1. Slice veal and clean mushrooms, cutting both into emincé.
2. Heat a medium sauteuse, add clarified butter. Brown meat on all sides and remove to a sieve; season with salt and pepper, keep warm.
3. Add fresh butter to pan, sauté mushrooms with shallots.
4. Deglaze with white wine, reduce to syrupy consistency.
5. Add thickened brown veal stock, reduce again. Add cream and reduce to desired consistency; thicken further with white instant roux if needed.
6. Return meat to pan and reheat gently without boiling.
7. Season to taste with salt, pepper mill, and lemon juice. Fold in whipped cream.
8. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve immediately with golden brown Swiss Rösti.
