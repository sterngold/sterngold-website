SOTO AJAM

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: Tabac Servings: 10

Stock preparation: sweat sliced onions with ginger, garlic, lemongrass, turmeric, and sambal. Pour in chicken bouillon and simmer. Add lime juice and lemongrass. Season with pepper.

Per portion: Blanch bean sprouts. Cook chicken breast fillet. Crack egg into soup bowl. Layer with noodles and fried onions. Pour hot stock over. Garnish with sprouts and chicken.

Stock ingredients:
- 10 kg onions
- 100 g ginger
- 40 g garlic
- Handful lemongrass
- 150 g chicken bouillon
- 4 limes
- 4 lemongrass stalks
- 2 tbsp sambal
- 40 g turmeric
- Pepper

voor 10 lt.
