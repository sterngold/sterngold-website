Garde Manger Salad

Tomato Salad

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

1.1399999856948853 kg Tomatoes (10)
1 pcs pinch Salt
1 pcs x Seasoning, black pepper
90 g Onions
# Dressing
100 g ml Vinegar, red wine
150 g ml Olive oil cold pressed
20 g Basil, fresh chopped
20 g Chives, fresh

Production

- tomatoes - wahs, remove steam end. Cut into slices
- Onions - peel and chop

- ARRANGE TOMATO SLICES IN SEQUENCE ON PLATES
- SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER
- SPRINKLE WITH CHOPPED ONIONS
- DRIZZLE WITH VINEGAR AND OIL
- SPRINKLE WITH CHOPPED BASIL AND CHIVES

Notes

- The classic method calls for peeled tomatoes. This is no longer recommended in commercial establishments for health resons - Another suitable dressing is herb vinaigrette. Use vinaigrette dressing and add kitchen herbs
