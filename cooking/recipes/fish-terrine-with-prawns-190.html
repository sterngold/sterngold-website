- CUT RAW FISH IN DICES
- CUT CHIVES AND CHOP DILL
- MIX ICE COLD CREAM, EGG WHITE, SALT AND FISH
- ADD THE MIXTURE INTO FOOD PROCESSOR AND BLEND UNTIL SMOOTH MOUSSELINE FARCE
- REMOVE AND PLACE IN BOWL ON ICE
- ADD THE POACHED PRAWNS (see course infos)
CHIVES AND CHERVIL AND SEASON WITH SALT,
LEMON JUICE AND NOUILLY PRAT, ADD CHOPPED PISTACCHIOS, BLACK TRUFFLE BRUNOISE.
- LINE OUT TERRINE MOLD WITH THIN WELL DONE COOKED CARROT SLICES FILL IN THE MOUSSELINE FARCE AND COVER
- COVER UP WITH PLASTIC FOIL.
- POACH AT 75°C IN STEAMER FOR APPROX.20-25 MIN. UNTIL CENTER TEMP. OF 55°C. - COOL THE TERRINE IN THE REFRIGERATOR FOR 1 HOUR.
Dice raw pike perch fillet. Cut and mince chives and dill. Combine ice-cold cream, egg whites, salt, and fish; process in food processor until smooth mousseline farce forms. Remove to a bowl set on ice. Fold in poached prawns, chives, chervil, and black truffle brunoise. Season with salt, lemon juice, and Noilly Prat. Add chopped pistachios. Line a terrine mold with thin, well-cooked carrot slices. Fill with mousseline farce and cover. Cover with plastic film. Poach at 75°C in a steamer for 20–25 minutes until center temperature reaches 55°C. Cool in refrigerator for 1 hour before serving. Note: Prawns should be poached according to course instructions.
