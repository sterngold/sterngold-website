Saucier Sauce hot 🇫🇷 France

Hollandaise Sauce

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 0

Ingredients

15 g Shallots
3 pcs piece Peppercorns, white
5 g ml Vinegar, white wine
90 g ml Wine, white, dry
50 g ml Water
2 g Salt
100 g Egg yolk
350 g Butter
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
1 pcs x Lemon juice

Production

- clarifiy butter (best taste when is made a beurre noisset) - strain

- boil vinegar, wine, 1/3 of the water, shallots, peppercorns, and salt until almost completely reduced
- add remaining water to dilute reduction
- strain through hair sieve
- add egg yolk to reduction and beat over 80 C water until thick and foamy
- add freshly clarified butter in a thin stream, whipping vigorously
- season with salt, cayenne and lemon juice

- keep sauce in covered conatiner in a warm place. For sanitary reasons, the sauce should not be kept longer than 1 hour

Notes

If the suace curdles, beat together 1 egg yolk with little water over hot water, and beat in sauce in small amounts. As soon as the suace strats to thicken, the remaining curdled sauce can be added in larger amount.
