SESAME SEEDS TUILES
Chef Patrick Diethelm | Cost: CHF 0.12 / EUR 0.07
Yield: 5 servings

INGREDIENTS
40 g White wheat flour
15 g Egg
25 g Peeled sesame seeds
5 g Poppy seed
10 g Butter
3 g Crystal sugar
0.1 pc Lemon juice
8 g Honey

METHOD
Mix all ingredients to a dough. Cool and roll out thin between two sheets of plastic film with minimal flour. Freeze. Peel off top film and cut into desired garnish shapes. Bake at 180°C until golden brown. Use for garnish and decoration.
