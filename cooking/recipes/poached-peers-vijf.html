Poached Peers Vijf

Chef: Jan Wasch Source: Vijf

Production

Обелват се крушите
Десертно вино – мускатово
Подправки – Stern, Kruid nagel, Chinese papper, Vanile –половинкар, Koriander – топчета
Мед
Лимон – сок и цяла обвивка
Портокал – кората се обелва /каротофобелачка/ и сока
Вода
Захар
На бавен огън
Peel pears and set aside.

Combine muscatel dessert wine, honey, lemon juice, lemon zest, orange zest (peeled with a peeler), orange juice, and water in a pot. Add sugar and spices: star anise, cloves, Sichuan pepper, half a vanilla pod, and coriander seeds.

Bring to a gentle simmer and poach pears slowly until tender. Cook on low heat until desired doneness is achieved.

Allow to cool in the poaching liquid before service.
From Vijf · Chef Jan Wasch
