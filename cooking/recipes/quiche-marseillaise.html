**Quiche Marseillaise**
*Chef: Patrick Diethelm | 10 servings | Recipe #294*

**Crust:**
Brush tart mold with butter and dust with flour. Roll out dark rye dough and line mold, poking holes randomly for steam escape. Blind bake at 180°C for 12 minutes.

**Filling:**
Wash, trim, and cut leeks and fennel into fine julienne. Peel and emince shallots. Scrape, wash, and braise mussels in dry white wine. Drain mussels from shells and remove strings, reserving stock.

Sweat shallots, fennel, and leeks in olive oil. Add cooked mussels, a pinch of saffron, and some mussel stock with Pernod. Heat through, then drain, reserving stock. Cool completely.

Reduce reserved mussel stock and cool slightly. Blend heavy cream and eggs, mix with reduced stock, and strain through fine sieve. Season to taste and add chopped fennel green.

**Assembly and Baking:**
Arrange cooled mussels and vegetables evenly in lined mold. Pour custard over seafood. Bake at 180–200°C for 20–30 minutes until center is firm.
