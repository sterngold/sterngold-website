- Brush tart mold with butter and dust with flour.
- Roll out dark rye dough and line tart mold.
Poke holes at random to let steam escape. Blind baking for 12 minutes at 180°C - Wash and trim leeks and fennel; Cut into fine julienne.
- Peel arid clean shallots and cut into emince
- Scrape mussels, wash and braise in white wine.
- Drain mussels out of shells; remove strings.
- SWEAT SHALLOTS, FENNEL AND LEEKS IN OLIVE OIL; ADD COOKED MUSSELS, SAFFRON AND HEAT WITH SOME MUSSEL STOCK AND PASTE.
DRAIN OFF AND RESERVE STOCK.
- LET DRAINED, MUSSELS, AND VEGETABLE COOL.
- WHEN COOL, PLACE SEAFOOD WITH VEGETABLES EVENLY IN MOLD LINED WITH DOUGH. - REDUCE RESERVED FISH STOCK AND LET COOL SLIGHTLY.
- BLEND CREAM, AND EGGS. MIX WITH REDUCED FISH STOCK. STRAIN THROUGH CHINA CAP. SEASON TO TASTE. ADD CHOPPED FENNEL GREEN
- POUR CUSTARD OVER SEAFOOD; BAKE AT LOW HEAT FOR ABOUT 20 - 30 MINUTES, AT 180 - 200° C UNTIL CENTER IS FIRM.
