RED BEET CRISSINIS WITH ANIS
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Date: 12.12.2005
Yield: 10 servings
Cost of goods: CHF 0.04 / EUR 0.02

Ingredients:
5 g fresh yeast
10 ml milk
80 g white wheat flour
1 g powdered malt
1 g anis seeds
5 ml grape seed oil
30 g red beet juice
2 g sea salt

Method:
Prepare crissini dough with all ingredients and knead until smooth and dense. Follow basic bake lesson recipes and tips & tricks for further instruction. Bake at 180°C.
