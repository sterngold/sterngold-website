Chop.
Crush into fine paste.

Dip tomatoes in boiling water for 10 seconds and chill immediately in ice water. Peel, cut in half, remove seeds and chop into concassé dice.
1 x Seasoning (salt)
- SWEAT SHALLOTS IN OIL.
- ADD GARLIC AND TOMATO PASTE AND CONTINUE COOKING.
- ADD DICED TOMATOES AND STEW CAREFULLY FOR ABOUT 2 MINUTE. - SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER MILL.
