Fondue Vacherin Mont-d'Or
Serves 4
An exquisite delight in just 25 minutes,
1 Vacherin Mont-d'Or Switzerland
1 loaf semi-white bread, cubed
1 small glass of white wine
Wrap in aluminum foil wooden box with Vacherin (do not cover cheese).
Use fork to puncture the vacherin crust.
Pour glass of white wine over cheese.
Bake 25 minutes in oven at 1702 С.
