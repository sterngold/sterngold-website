**Fish Terrine with Prawns** (Pacojet) | Chef Patrick Diethelm | November 17, 2005 | Cost: CHF 1.94

**Mise en place:**
400 g pike perch fillet (raw, diced), 250 ml heavy cream (36%), 15 g egg whites, 10 g salt. Garnish: 200 g large raw prawns (poached and cleaned), 30 g fresh chives (chopped), 12 g fresh chervil, 20 g black truffles (brunoise), carrot strips for lining.

**Method:**
Cut raw fish into dice. Combine cream, egg whites, salt, and diced fish in Pacojet bowl. Blend twice using the 4-blade fresh Pacojet knife. Transfer to ice.

Fold in the poached prawns, chives, chervil, and black truffle brunoise. Season with salt, lemon juice, and Noilly Prat.

Line a terrine mold with thin carrot strips. Fill with the mousseline farce and cover with remaining vegetables. Seal with plastic wrap.

Poach in a 75°C steamer for approximately 20 minutes until the center reaches 55°C. Cool in the refrigerator for 1 hour before service.
