Garde Manger Salad

Carrot raw salad

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

960 g Carrot
# Dressing
50 g ml Lemon juice
50 g ml Orange, juice
1 pcs pinch Crystal sugar
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
10 g Oregano, fresh
150 g ml Oil, sunflower

Production

- carrots - peel and grate coarsely or cut into fine strips (julliene)
- oregano - chop

- BLEND DRESSING INGREDIENTS AND MIX THE CARROTS

Notes

Other suitable salad dressings are Yogurt, Cottage cheese dressing and Creamy salad dressing
