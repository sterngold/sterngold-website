Fenel

Chef: Jan Wasch Source: Vijf

Production

Samll cubes
With orange zest and juce
tyme and rozmerain
white wine
on slow fire
**Fennel** | Vijf, Entremétier

**Ingredients:**
- Fennel, cut into small cubes
- Orange zest and juice
- Thyme and rosemary
- White wine

**Method:**
1. Combine fennel cubes with orange zest, orange juice, thyme, and rosemary.
2. Add white wine.
3. Cook slowly over low heat until tender.
From Vijf · Chef Jan Wasch
