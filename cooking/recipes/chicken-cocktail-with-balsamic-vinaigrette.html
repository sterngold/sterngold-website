Garde Manger Cold Appetizer

Chicken cocktail with balsamic vinaigrette

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

1.0 kg Chicken
80 g ml Olive oil cold pressed
10 g Garlic
1 pcs piece Head of lettuce
237 g Tomatoes
90 g Onions
200 g ml Olive oil cold pressed
50 g ml Vinegar, Balsamic
36 g Shallots
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
10 g Basil, fresh chopped
20 g Parsley, fresh, chopped

Production

- lettuce - clean
- tomatoes - peel, remove seeds and cut into 5 mm concasse

#Dressing
- mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt and pepper
- chop shallots and basil. Add to mixture. Blend throughly

-SAUTEE CHICKEN MEAT WITH OLIVE OIL AND GARLIC. SEASON AND CUT THEN INTO BIGGER SIZE JULLIENNE
- BLEND TOMATOES AND ONIONS WITH SOME DRESSING
- PLACE SMALL LETTUCE LEAVES INTO GLASS
- PLACE BOUQUETS OF TOMATO SALAD AND CHICKEN SALAD ON LEAVES
DRIZZLE WITH DRESSING. SPRINKLE WITH CHOPPED PARSLEY
- GARNISH EACH GLAS WITH FRESH BASIL
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
