Garde Manger Salad

Vegetable cocktail with yogurt

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

300 g Cauliflower
200 g Fennel bulb (anise)
285 g Tomatoes
143 g Mushrooms, fresh
1 pcs piece Avocados
1 pcs piece Artichoke, bottoms large
25 g ml Vinegar, white wine
60 g ml Oil, sunflower
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
10 g ml Lemon juice
10 g Chives, fresh
# Dressing
300 g Yogurt, plain
60 g ml Olive oil cold pressed
60 g ml Ketchup, tomato
9 g Horseradish, fresh
140 g Oranges
1 pcs x Tabasco
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper

Production

- cauliflower - clean and trim. Cut into florets. Steam until cooked but still frim
- fennel bulb - clean and trim. Cut into wedges. Steam until cooked but still firm
- tomatoes - peel, remove seeds and cube
- mushrooms - cut into quarters. Braise briefly with lemon jucie
- avocados - peel, remove pit, cut into small wedges and drizzle with lemon juice
- artichoke - boil, cut bottom into 10 slices

# Dressing
- peel oranges and cut into smal cubes. Blend with yogurt, olive oil, ketchup, grated horseradish, tabasco, salt and pepper.

Presentation
- fill large cocktail glasses about halfway with dressing
- add marinated vegetables and top with remaining dressing
- garnish with avocado wedges and atrichoke slices
