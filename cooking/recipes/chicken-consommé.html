Saucier Soup

Chicken consommé

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

2.0 kg liter Chicken broth
100 g Chicken meat
620 g Lean beef
160 g ml Water
51 g Carrot (Cut into matignon)
34 g Knob Celery (celeriac) (Cut into matignon)
50 g Tomatoes (Cut into matignon)
60 g Leeks (Wash, trim and Cut into matignon)
75 g piece Egg whites
1 g Rosemary twig, fresh
4 pcs piece Peppercorns, white
1 pcs x Salt
50 g ml Sherry wine, dry

Production

- grind meat clarification and vegetables through coarse plate of meat grinder
- make clarification by throughly combining ground chicken and beef meat, leek, carrots, celery, tomatoes, egg whites, rosemary, white peppercorns and water.

- combine clarification with cold chicken broth and stir well
- bring to boil while stirring carefully with a frying spatula
- as soon as consomme starts to boil, do not stir any longer
- simmer for 1 hour
- strain carefully through kitchen cloths or napkin with help of hair sieve
- remove all fat from surface with absorbent paper
- season to taste
- add sherry to parfume the consomme

Notes

- If too cloudy not enough egg whites. - add onion brulle for color - clarification needs to sit for a while for meat protein to appear
