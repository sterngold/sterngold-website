Entremétier Soup

Calves tail Soup Part I

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 2

Ingredients

360 g Lean beef (Grind meat through coarse plate of meat grinder. Make clarification - water, maet, egg white, tomaotes. Mix well and refrigarete 1 h.)
120 g Flavouring vegetables for meat
160 g ml Water
75 g piece Egg whites
38 g Tomatoes (cut into dice)
40 g ml Sherry wine, dry
1 pcs x Salt
80 g Leeks (Wash, trim and dice. Boil in water or briase together with carrots and celey until just done)
96 g Carrot (cut into dice)
56 g Knob Celery (celeriac) (cut into dice)
Ravioli dough

Production

- prepare ravioli dough and fill with oxx tail meat stuffing
- - combine cold stock with clarification; stir with frying spatula until mixture starts to boil
- do not stir any longer and simmer for 1 hour
- strain carefully through a napkin (2 china caps technique)
- remove all fat from surface with absorbent paper
- season to taste
- add sherry wine
- garnish with ravioli of calvestail meat and vegetables

Raviolo
- sweet shallots + stufiing (mushrooms, herbs, meat, glace de viand and sherry to cook)
- boil in broth or water with no FAT - add to soup
Prepare ravioli dough and fill with oxtail meat stuffing.

Combine cold stock with clarification mixture. Stir with frying spatula until the mixture begins to boil, then stop stirring. Simmer for 1 hour without further disturbance.

Strain carefully through a napkin using the double china cap technique. Remove all fat from the surface using absorbent paper. Season to taste and add sherry wine.

For the ravioli: combine sweet shallots with filling made from mushrooms, herbs, meat, glace de viande, and sherry. Cook the ravioli in broth or water with no added fat.

Finish the soup by adding the ravioli and vegetable garnish just before service.
