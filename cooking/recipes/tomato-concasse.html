Tomato Concasse

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Zutaten für 1.000 Liter zu 100% (Nettomenge)

35 ml 33 g
30 g
75 g
1 kg
Olive oil
Shallots
Garlic
Tomato paste Tomatoes
Peeled

Dip tomatoes in boiling water for 15 seconds and chill immediately in ice water. Peel tomatoes, cut in half, remove seeds and chop into 7 x 7 mm dices.
1 x Seasoning (salt)
- SWEAT FINE CHOPPED SHALLOTS AND GARLIC IN OLIVE OIL. - ADD TOMATO PUREE AND CONTINUE COOKING.
- ADD TOMATO CONCASSE AND STEW FOR ABOUT 2-3 MINUTES. - SEASON TO TASTE
DCT - Swiss Culinary School - Vitznau / Luzern
