CALCMENU
TOMATO CONCASSE

Yield: 1000ml (net quantity)

INGREDIENTS
35ml Olive oil
33g Shallots, finely chopped
30g Garlic
75g Tomato paste
1kg Tomatoes, peeled
Salt to taste

METHOD
1. Blanch tomatoes in boiling water for 15 seconds, then chill immediately in ice water. Peel, halve, remove seeds, and chop into 7×7mm dice.
2. Sweat finely chopped shallots and garlic in olive oil.
3. Add tomato paste and continue cooking.
4. Add tomato concasse and stew for approximately 2–3 minutes.
5. Season to taste with salt.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
