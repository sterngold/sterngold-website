**Crispy Vegetable Dough Decors** | Chef P. Diethelm, 07.11.2005

Mix 40 g egg whites with 20 g cold vegetable purée, 50 g white wheat flour, 30 g melted butter, and salt (do not beat). Cover and chill for 20 minutes.

Spoon onto sheet and sprinkle with dry spices, poppy seeds, or sesame seeds as desired. Bake at 180°C for 2–5 minutes until crisp.
