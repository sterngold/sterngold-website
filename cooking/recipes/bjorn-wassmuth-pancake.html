Bjorn Wassmuth Pancake

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen

Production

125 g Flour
150 ml milk
3 stk. Egg yolk – all mix well
Salt
Backpulver – thee spoon – give to
5 g Sucker
3 pc. Egg white – beat / folds in the milk masse
Masse halt 2 day
Mix 125 g flour, 150 ml milk, and 3 egg yolks until smooth. Season with salt. Add 1 teaspoon baking powder and 5 g sugar. Whip 3 egg whites to stiff peaks and fold into batter. Batter may be held for 2 days.
From Tschuggen · Chef Ulf Wein
