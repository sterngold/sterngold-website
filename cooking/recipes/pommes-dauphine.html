Pommes Dauphine

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Pommes dauphine
Dauphine-Kartoffeln Pommes dauphine


Recipe # : 582
Category : ECA - Entremetier/ side dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 18.102005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.12 / EUR 0.08

European Gourmet Cuisine
Ingredients for 6 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
420 g Potatoes Bintje B/C, peeled
4 g Salt
160 g *Choux Paste
1 x Seasoning (salt, nutmeg) 15 ml Peanut oil


- Cut peeled potatoes into large even sized chunks.
- Steam potatoes in pressure steamer with 1 bar, drain well.
- When cooked, dry potatoes briefly in the oven at 150°C with open steam escape. - Pass potatoes through food mill
- Cut parchment paper into 50 mm wide strips and oil strips lightly.
- Blend hot puréed potatoes with choux paste; season to taste with salt and nutmeg.
- Shape into oval dumplings, using two oily tablespoons, and line up dumplings along the parchment paper strips or use piping bag for special suitable shape.


- AT SERVICE TIME, HEAT OIL IN DEEP FRYER AT 175°C.
- DIP PAPER STRIPS IN HOT OIL; THE POTATO DUMPLINGS WILL SLIDE OFF.
- FRY DUMPLINGS UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN AND DRAIN ON ABSORBENT PAPER. - SERVE ON PAPER DOILIES, AS A SIDE DISH OR GARNISH.


Note: The paper strips should be long enough for one or two portions. Fry portions to order.
