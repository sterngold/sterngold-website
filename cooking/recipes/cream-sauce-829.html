Ingredients for 0.500 liters at 100% (Net quantity)
31
g
Butter
23
g
Onions
31
g
White wheat flour
625
ml
Milk
125
ml
Heavy cream (36%)
1
x
Seasoning [white pepper, salt)
1
x
Nutmeg (pinch)

- CUT ONIONS INTO SMALL DICE AND SWEAT IN BUTTER. - ADD FLOUR AND COOK BRIEFLY.
- COOL RESULTING ROUX.
- BRING MILK TO A BOILING POINT AND ADD TO ROUX.
- SIMMER FOR ABOUT 20 MINUTES.
- ADD CREAM AND BRING TO A BOIL AGAIN AND SEASON TO TASTE. - STRAIN THROUGH FINE CHINA CAP.
- Cover with a lid or use immediately
