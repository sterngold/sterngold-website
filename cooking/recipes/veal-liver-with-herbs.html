- SAUTE LIVER IN CLARIFIED BUTTER OVER HIGH HEAT UNTIL STILL RARE. DO NOT OVERCOOK.
- AND PUT THE LIVER IN A COLANDER OR HAIR SIEVE TO DRAIN. - SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER
- ADD BUTTER TO THE PAN AND SWEAT SHALLOTS
- ADD FRESH CHOPPED HERBS AND EXTRA BUTTER
- REMOVE FROM HEAT, ADD LIVER JUST TO WARM IT, AND COMBINE
- SERVE IMMEDIATELY
Sauté sliced calves' liver in clarified butter over high heat until still rare; do not overcook. Drain liver in a colander or hair sieve and season with salt and pepper. Add butter to the pan and sweat shallots. Stir in fresh chopped herbs (thyme, rosemary, parsley) and additional butter. Remove from heat, add liver to warm it through, and combine gently. Serve immediately. For 3 servings.
