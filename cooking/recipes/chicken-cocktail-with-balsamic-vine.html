CHICKEN COCKTAIL WITH BALSAMIC VINEGAR
Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Recipe for 10 servings | Cost: CHF 1.91

Ingredients:
1 kg chicken breast
80 ml cold-pressed olive oil (for cooking)
10 g garlic
1 head lettuce, cleaned
237 g tomatoes (peeled, seeded, 5 mm concasse)
90 g onions
200 ml cold-pressed olive oil (for dressing)
50 ml balsamic vinegar
36 g shallots
10 g fresh basil
20 g fresh Italian parsley, chopped
Salt and pepper

Method:
Prepare dressing: Mix 200 ml olive oil with balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Finely chop shallots and basil, add to dressing, and blend thoroughly.

Sauté chicken breast with 80 ml olive oil and garlic until cooked. Season, cool, and cut into large julienne.

Toss tomatoes and onions with some dressing.

Line serving glasses with small lettuce leaves. Layer tomato salad and chicken salad on top.

Drizzle with remaining dressing and sprinkle with chopped parsley. Garnish each glass with fresh basil leaf.
