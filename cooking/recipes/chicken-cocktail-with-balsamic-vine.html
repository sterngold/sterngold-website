Chicken Cocktail with Balsamic Vine
GentOgelcocktail mit Ba/samico
Cocktail de pou/et au vinaigre ba/samique
Rezept Nr. :240
Kategorie : Cold Appetizers
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 01.03.2004
Warenkosten : CHF 1.91 / EUR 1.21
Zuaten fUr 10 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)
Chicken 1kg
Olive oil Cold pressed 80 ml
Garlic 10 g
Head of lettuce1 st Clean.
Tomatoes 237 g Peel, remove seeds, and cut into 5mm concasse. Chop.
Onions 90 g
Olive oil Cold pressed 200 ml
Balsamic vinegar 50 ml
Shallots 36 g
Seasoning (salt and pepper) 1 x
Basil, fresh 10 g
Parsley, Italian, fresh 20 g Chop.

Method for Dressing
- Mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt and pepper.
- Chop shallots and basil. Add to oil mixture. Blend thoroughly.
- SAUTE CHICKEN MEAT WITH OLIVE OIL AND GARLlC.SEASON AND CUT THEN INTO BIGGER SIZE JULIENNE
- BLEND TOMATOES AND ONIONS WITH SOME DRESSING.
- PLACE SMALL LETTUCE LEAVES INTO GLASS
- PLACE BOUQUETS OF TOMATO SALAD AND CHICKEN SALAD ON LEAVES.
- DRIZZLE WITH DRESSING. SPRINKLE WITH CHOPPED PARSLEY.
- GARNISH EACH GLAS WITH FRESH BASIL
