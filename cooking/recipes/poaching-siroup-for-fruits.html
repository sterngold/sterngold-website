Pâtisserie Patisseri Mise en place

Poaching siroup for fruits

Chef: Jan Wasch Source: Vijf

Production

1 lt. water
½ lt. witte wijn
300 gr. Suiker
Sap en schil van 1 citroen en 1 sinasappel
1 Vanille
2 Kaneel
3 Steranijs
Beijtje peperkorrels
POACHING SIRUP FOR FRUITS
Source: Vijf | Pâtisserie Station

1. Combine 1 lt. water and ½ lt. white wine in pot.
2. Add 300 gr. sugar, juice and zest of 1 lemon and 1 orange.
3. Add 1 vanilla pod, 2 cinnamon sticks, 3 star anise, and a pinch of peppercorns.
4. Bring to boil, then simmer 15 minutes.
5. Strain and cool before poaching fruit.
From Vijf · Chef Jan Wasch · 25.03.2006
