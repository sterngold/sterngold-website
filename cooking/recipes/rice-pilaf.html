Rice Pilaf

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Rice Pilaf
Pilaw-Reis
Riz pilau


Rezept Nr. : 646
Kategorie : Rice and Grain Dishes
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 11.08.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 0.11 / EUR 0.07

EUROPEAN CULINARY CENTER

Zutaten für 2 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)
12 g Butter
15 g Onions
180 g Siam Patna rice
280 ml Water
0.5 st Bay leaves
12 g Butter cold


Peel and fine chopped.

- SWEAT ONIONS LIGHTLY IN BUTTER, WITHOUT GETTING COLOR.
- ADD RICE, AND SAUTÉ BRIEFLY WITH ONIONS.
- ADD WATER AND BAY LEAF AND BRING TO A BOIL.
- SEASON WITH SALT
- COVER AND COOK IN THE OVEN AT 200°C FOR ABOUT 10 MINUTES.
- REMOVE FROM THE OVEN AND KEEP COVERED FOR ABOUT 5 MINUTES.
- REMOVE BAY LEAF. CAREFULLY STIR RICE WITH A FORK TO LOOSEN THE KERNELS. - ADD THE COLD BUTTER IN SMALL NUGGETS.
Notes: The amount of liquid varies depending on rice variety. Generally, larger amounts require less liquid.
