**Rice Pilaf** | Recipe 646 | Chef Patrick Diethelm

**Ingredients (2 servings):**
- 12 g butter
- 15 g onions, peeled and finely chopped
- 180 g Siam Patna rice
- 280 ml water
- 0.5 bay leaf
- 12 g cold butter

**Method:**
1. Sweat onions lightly in butter without allowing them to color.
2. Add rice and sauté briefly with the onions.
3. Add water and bay leaf, bring to a boil.
4. Season with salt.
5. Cover and cook in the oven at 200°C for approximately 10 minutes.
6. Remove from oven and keep covered for about 5 minutes.
7. Remove bay leaf. Using a fork, carefully stir the rice to loosen the kernels.
8. Add cold butter in small nuggets.

**Note:** Liquid requirements vary by rice variety; larger quantities require proportionally less liquid.
