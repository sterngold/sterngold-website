Marbre de foie de canard

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 4

Ingredients

200 g Stuffed duck liver
70 g Water
40 g ml Port wine, red
5 g Aspic powder
5 g Chives, fresh
2 g Green peppercorns (canned, drained)
4 g Butter, clarified
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
45 g Pumpernickel

- suate the pieces in hot pan and place in hair sieve
- chill quickly

- crush green peppercorns
- cut chives into very fine slivers
- add into aspic
- line out terrine mold with plastic foil and fill terrine with liver and aspic

- slice carefully the marbre
- serve with liver truffel (trimmings with butter) breaded in pumpernickel bread crumbs.
- with cognac marinated sultans and orange sections
Clean the chilled duck liver. Sauté the pieces in a hot pan, then transfer to a hair sieve and chill quickly.

Prepare aspic with water, port wine, and aspic powder. Crush the green peppercorns and cut chives into fine slivers, then add both to the aspic.

Line a terrine mold with plastic film. Fill with alternating layers of liver and aspic. Chill for 1 hour before serving.

Slice the marbre carefully. Serve with liver truffles (trimmings mixed with butter) breaded in pumpernickel crumbs, cognac-marinated sultanas, and orange sections.
