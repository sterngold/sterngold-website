Allemande - with veal veloute + white veal stock

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 0

Ingredients

400 g ml Veal Velute Sauce
200 g ml White veal sauce
25 g st Egg yolk
100 g ml Heavy cream 36%
25 g Butter, cold
1 pcs x Lemon juice
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper

Production

- reduce veal stock quickly to a syrupy consistency, but do not brown
- add veloute veau; bring to boiling point
- combine egg yolks with cream and mix into sauce. Do not alow to boil
- season to taste and strain through fine china cap
- add cold butter in small nuggets

APPLICATIONS
- use as an ingridient in white meat suace variations
- use as sauce with pasta
- use with veal fillings for patty shells
ALLEMANDE SAUCE

Reduce veal stock quickly to a syrupy consistency without browning. Add veal velouté and bring to boiling point. Combine egg yolks with cream, then temper into the sauce off heat—do not allow to boil. Season to taste and pass through fine china cap. Mount with cold butter in small pieces.

Applications: Use as a base for white meat sauce variations, as a sauce for pasta, or with veal fillings for patty shells.
