Roast Duckling with Oranges
Gebratene junge Ente mit Orangen Caneton rôti à l'orange


Rezept Nr. : 458
Kategorie : Poultry Dishes
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 27.01.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 4.38 / EUR 2.77

European Gourmet Cuisine
Zutaten für 5 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge]
1.75 kg Duckling, young oven-ready
75 g *Seasoning Salt for Poultry
38 ml Peanut oil
150 g *Flavoring Vegetables for Meat
50 ml Dry white wine
150 ml Orange juice
50 ml Red Port wine
300 g Fond de veau brun lié
1 mg Cayenne pepper, ground

1 x Seasoning (satt)
3 st Orange
25 g Butter
Truss to retain its shape during roasting.










Peel and seam out wedges.

- SEASON DUCKLING WITH SEASONING SALT, POUR PEANUT OIL OVER DUCKLING, AND ROAST IN HOT OVEN AT 200°C UNTIL TENDER CRISP, BASTING OCCASIONALLY. REMOVE DUCKLING AND KEEP WARM.
- POUR OFF EXCESS FAT.
- ADD MATIGNON AND ROAST BRIEFLY.
- ADD FOND DE VEAU BRUN LIE AND BRING TO A BOIL TO DISSOLVE PAN DRIPPINGS.
- ADD ORANGE JUICE AND PORT WINE. REDUCE STOCK.
- SEASON WITH CAYENNE.
- REDUCE SAUCE TO A DESIRED CONSISTENCY AND SKIM.
- STRAIN SAUCE THROUGH A FINE WIRE CHINA CAP AND SKIM AGAIN.
ADJUST SEASONING.
- HEAT ORANGE SECTIONS IN BUTTER.
- CARVE DUCKLING AND SERVE WITH ORANGE SECTIONS AND SOME SAUCE.
- SERVE REMAINING SAUCE ON THE SIDE.
