Roast Duckling with Oranges
Recipe 458 | Chef: Patrick Diethelm | Serves 5

INGREDIENTS
1.75 kg young oven-ready duckling
75 g seasoning salt for poultry
38 ml peanut oil
150 g flavoring vegetables (matignon)
50 ml dry white wine
150 ml orange juice
50 ml red port wine
300 g brown veal stock (fond de veau brun lié)
1 mg cayenne pepper, ground
3 oranges
25 g butter

METHOD
Truss duckling to retain shape. Season with poultry seasoning salt and coat with peanut oil. Roast in hot oven at 200°C until tender and crisp, basting occasionally. Remove and keep warm.

Pour off excess fat from roasting pan. Add matignon and roast briefly. Add brown veal stock and bring to boil, scraping pan drippings to dissolve. Add orange juice and port wine, then reduce stock. Season with cayenne pepper and reduce sauce to desired consistency, skimming as needed. Strain through fine china cap and skim again. Adjust seasoning.

Peel oranges and segment out wedges. Heat orange sections in butter.

Carve duckling and present with orange sections and sauce. Serve remaining sauce on the side.
