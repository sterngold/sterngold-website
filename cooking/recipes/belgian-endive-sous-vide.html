Belgian Endive sous vide

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

Belgian Endive Sous vide style
Brüsseler Endivie sous vide Endives sous vide


Recipe # : 541
Category : ECA - Vegetables
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 25.10.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.33 EUR 0.21

European Gourmet Cuisine

Ingredients for 6 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
400 g Belgian endive
3 g Salt
30 g Butter
120 ml Orange juice And zest
Wash endives, trim if necessary. split length wise in half remove the bitter core. Be sure endive leaves do not separate.

- Reduce orange juice to 30 ml.

- PLACE ENDIVE TIGHTLY, SIDE BY SIDE INTO VACUUM BAG
- SEASON WITH SALT
- ADD REDUCED ORANGE JUICE AND BUTTER PIECES AND VACUUM WITH 95%
- STEAM AT 91°C FOR APPROX. 35-40 MIN IN COMB! STEAMER
UNTIL BELGIAN ENDIVE ARE TENDER.
- COOL DOWN IN VACUUM BAG IN ICE WATER AS QIUCK AS POSSIBLE
- WHEN 2°C REMOVE FROM ICE WATER
- LABEL AND YOU CAN STORE SOUS VIDE COOKED VEGETABLES AT LEAST 5-10 DAYS
- FOR REHEATING ENDIVE BELGE ADD VACUUM BAG INTO STEAM FOR 10 -15 MIN.
- READY FOR SERVING
- PLACE REMAINING LIQUID INTO SAUTEUSE AND REDUCE BRUSH ENDIVE AND SERVE.
APPROX. COOKING TIME LIST FOR SOUS VIDE VEGETABLES AT 91-94°C
POTATOES NATURE 45 - 55 MIN CARROTS BATONNET 45 MIN FENNEL HALF 90 MIN. ASPARAGUS WHITE 50 MIN. ASPARAGUS GREEN 35 MIN.
CELERY ROOT 8O MIN.
