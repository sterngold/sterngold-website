**Belgian Endive Sous Vide**

Yield: 6 servings | Chef: Patrick Diethelm (25.10.2005)

**Ingredients:**
400 g Belgian endive
3 g salt
30 g butter
120 ml orange juice and zest

**Method:**
1. Wash endives and trim if necessary. Split lengthwise in half and remove the bitter core, keeping leaves intact.
2. Reduce orange juice to 30 ml.
3. Arrange endive halves tightly, side by side, in a vacuum bag. Season with salt.
4. Add reduced orange juice and butter pieces. Vacuum seal at 95%.
5. Steam at 91°C for 35–40 minutes in combi steamer until endives are tender.
6. Cool immediately in ice water while still in vacuum bag until internal temperature reaches 2°C.
7. Label and store sous vide for 5–10 days.

**To reheat:** Return vacuum bag to steam for 10–15 minutes.

**To serve:** Remove endives from bag. Pour remaining cooking liquid into sauteuse and reduce. Brush endives with reduced liquid and serve.

**Sous vide cooking times at 91–94°C reference:**
Potatoes (nature) 45–55 min | Carrots (batonnet) 45 min | Fennel (half) 90 min | White asparagus 50 min | Green asparagus 35 min | Celery root 80 min
