CALVES LIVER WITH MADEIRA SAUCE
Yield: 5 servings | Chef: Patrick Diethelm

Ingredients:
750g calves' liver
40g clarified butter
Salt and pepper
25g butter
100g shallots
75ml Madeira wine
400g fond de veau brun lié
15g parsley, fresh
5g fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary)
40g butter

Method:
Sauté liver in clarified butter over high heat until still rare—do not overcook. Drain in colander or hair sieve. Season with salt and pepper.

Add butter to pan and sweat shallots. Deglaze with Madeira wine, bring to boil, and reduce. Add fond de veau brun lié and reduce again.

Stir in fresh chopped herbs and extra butter. Remove from heat, add liver just to warm through, and combine with sauce. Do not allow to boil.

Serve immediately.
