Calves Liver with Madeira Sauce
Geschnetzelte Kalbsleber an Madeirasauce Emincë de Foie de veau au mac/ere

Category : Hot Meat Dishes
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 25.10.2005

- SAUTE LIVER IN CLARIFIED BUTTER OVER HIGH HEAT UNTIL STILL RARE. DO NOT OVERCOOK.
- AND PUT THE LIVER IN A COLANDER OR HAIR SIEVE TO DRAIN.
- SEASON WITH SALT AND PEPPER
- ADD BUTTER TO THE PAN AND SWEAT SHALLOTS
- DEGLAZE WITH MADEIRA WINE AND BRING TO A BOIL AND REDUCE. - ADD FOND DE VEAU BRUN LIE AND REDUCE
- ADD FRESH CHOPPED HERBS AND EXTRA BUTTER
- REMOVE FROM HEAT, ADD LIVER JUST TO WARM IT, AND COMBINE WITH SAUCE. DO NOT ALLOW TO BOIL.
- SERVE IMMEDIATELY
