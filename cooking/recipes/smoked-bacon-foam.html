Saucier Sauce hot

Smoked bacon foam

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 5

Ingredients

50 g Smoked bacon
40 g Consomme
75 g ml Heavy cream 36%

Production

- sweat the bacon, add the cream and consomme and bring to boil
- for serving strain through china cap and blend with pole blender to produce foam and spoon only foam on dish

Notes

Consomme or White veal stock
