- Combine white wheat flour, egg, cream and almonds to tuiles batter. - Do not beat !!!

- Brush tuiles 3cm x 12cm or spread with spatula on silpat
- Sprinkle with fine chopped fennel seeds and crushed almond slices and bake at 180°C. until golden brown.
