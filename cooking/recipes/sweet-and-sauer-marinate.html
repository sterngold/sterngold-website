Sweet and sauer Marinate

Suss and sauer Marinade

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen Servings: 2

Ingredients

1.0 kg liter Vinegar, white wine
1.0 kg liter Water
1.0 kg Sugar
50 g Ginger (grated)
2 g piece Bay leaves
5 g Mustard, kerns
5 g Cloves
5 g Peppercorns, white
5 g Koriander, kerns

Production

Boil all

Notes

For sweet and sauer mariande with Balsamico + 750 g Balsamico + 750 g Sugar
