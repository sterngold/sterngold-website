**iSi Whip Recipes**

Prepared by Peter Lindemann and Erich Zgraggsen

**HOLLANDAISE**
Warm clarified butter to approximately 60°C. Combine with pasteurized egg yolk and white wine reduction (made by reducing 1 l white wine with a splash of vinegar, crushed peppercorns, chopped onion, and 1 bay leaf to one-third volume, then straining). Blend all ingredients at room temperature until smooth. Strain through a sieve and fill iSi Thermo Whip. Charge with one cartridge. Shake before use.

**WARM CHOCOLATE SABAYON**
Melt dark chocolate couverture in a water bath. Add heavy cream and QimiQ, stirring until homogeneous. Add cognac. Fill iSi Thermo Whip and charge with one cartridge. Shake well and serve.

**SALMON MOUSSE**
Mix lightly frozen smoked salmon, onion, heavy cream, QimiQ, fish stock, and Pernod (all chilled) until a farce forms. Fill iSi Gourmet Whip and charge with one cartridge. Shake well and refrigerate. Rest 1 hour before plating. Garnish with fresh dill.

**LINDI'S CHEESE MOUSSE**
*Cold version:* Mix quark, QimiQ, grated Glarner Schabziger, grated Parmesan, and good olive oil at room temperature until lump-free. Fill iSi Thermo Whip, charge, shake, and refrigerate 1 hour.
*Warm version:* Warm all ingredients in a sautoir, then blend. Fill iSi Thermo Whip, charge, and shake. Shake before each use.

**EXOTIC FRUIT MOUSSE**
Smooth QimiQ, add plain yogurt, Boiron exotic fruit purée, heavy cream, and fine sugar. Mix and taste. Fill iSi Gourmet Whip and charge. Shake until desired consistency; use second cartridge if needed. Stays fresh 5 days refrigerated.

**ORANGE-CHOCOLATE MOUSSE**
Smooth QimiQ at room temperature. Add liquid Callebaut orange drops (40°C), yogurt, and half-cream. Mix well, strain through sieve, and fill iSi Gourmet Whip. Charge with first cartridge and shake lightly. Add second cartridge and shake again. Test and adjust consistency. Stays fresh 5 days refrigerated.

**AVOCADO MOUSSE**
Smooth QimiQ, add finely diced avocado and blend finely. Add liquid heavy cream and taste. Strain through sieve before filling iSi whip.
