Parmesan - Sablés
Parmesan - Sablés
Recipe #
Category : ECA - Mediterranean cuisine
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date :07.112005
Cost of goods : CHF 0.14 / EUR 0.09

European Gourmet Cuisine

Ingredients for 10 servings at 100% (Net quantity)
20 g Butter Soft 15 ml Olive oil
40 g Parmesan grated
65 g White wheat flour
1 mg Baking powder Pinch
35 g Egg
- KNEAD ALL INGREDIENTS TO A DOUGH.
LET REST FOR 1 HOUR IN REFRIGERATOR - ROLL OUT DOUGH 5 MM AND LET REST CUT OR STAMP OUT DOUGH AND PLACE ON SHEET PAN.
BAKE AT 1 BO°C UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN.
- OR ROLL OUT DOUGH 3 mm AND CUT INTO MODERN SHAPES BRUSH WITH EGG WASH AND BAKE
SERVING IDEAS
- SERVE FOR AMUSE BOUCHE WITH PARMA HAM, TOMATO AND OLIVE OIL OR GRILLED FISH MIGNON
