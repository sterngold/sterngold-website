Lemon - olive olie dressing

Chef: V. I. Sterngold

Production

limoensap - 1dl
olifolie - 3 dl
normal olie 1 dl
salt , pepper, cayennpepper
anijszaad
Lemon-Olive Oil Dressing

Combine 1 dl lemon juice with 3 dl olive oil and 1 dl neutral oil. Season with salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper to taste. Add aniseed. Whisk until emulsified.
· Chef V. I. Sterngold
