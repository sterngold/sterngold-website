Garde Manger Salad

Mediterranean Vegetable Salad with Saffron

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 8

Ingredients

2 pcs piece Artichoke, large
192 g Stalk celery
240 g Cauliflower
256 g Carrot
272 g Bell peppers, assorted
152 g Mushrooms, fresh
216 g Zucchini
160 g ml Olive oil cold pressed
60 g ml Lemon juice
1 pcs piece Bay leaves
4 g Fennel leaves
2 g Thyme, fresh chopped
160 g ml Water
1 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
1 pcs pinch Saffron
# Garnish
16 g Black olives, canned
16 g Green olives, canned

Production

- artichokes - clean and trim. Save 10 leaves for garnish. Blanch all. Set leaves aside. Remove centre fuzz from bottoms and cut into 20 wedges
- celery - clean and trim. Cut into attractive pieces of equal size. Blanch
-cauliflower - clean and trim. Cut into attractive pieces of equal size. Blanch
- carrot - clean and trim. Cut into attractive pieces of equal size. Blanch
- Peppers - clean and trim. Cut into attractive pieces of equal size.
- Mushrooms - clean and trim. Cut into attractive pieces of equal size.
- Zucchini - clean and trim. Cut into attractive pieces of equal size.

-SAUTE ALL VEGETABLES IN OLIVE OIL
- ADD LEMON JUICE AND WATER
- SEASON WITH BAY LEAVES, FENNEL LEAVES, THYME, SAFFRON AND SALT/PEPPER
- COOK VEGETABLES UNTIL TENDER BUT STILL FIRM. LET COOL IN THEIR OWN JUICE
- PLATE SALAD. GARNISH EACH PLATE WITH ONE ARTICHOKE LEAF AND BLACK AND GREEN OLIVES
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
