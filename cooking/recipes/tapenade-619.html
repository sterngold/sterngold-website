Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

VITZNAU-LUCERNE SWITZERLAND
TAPENADE / Olive paste
Olive dip or paste from the Provence for cold and hot dishes.
250 g black olives [TOP quality olives)
20 g anchovies
6 g dryed tomatoes blanched
40 g capers 20 g garlic 50 g olive oil
5 g chopped basil
pepper mill
Preparation:
- Add olives, anchovies, capers, garlic, tomatoes into in food processor and purée with olive oil
- Season with chopped basil and pepper mill
