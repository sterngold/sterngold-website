WHITE WINE SAUCE (FOR FISH)
Chef: P. Diethelm | DCT Saucier Station | 11.08.2005
Yield: 1,000 ml | Cost: CHF 4.74

INGREDIENTS
700 ml fish velouté sauce
300 ml fish stock poaching liquid
250 ml heavy cream (36%)
Salt, cayenne, and pepper to taste
Lemon juice to taste

METHOD
1. Reduce fish stock to a glaze.
2. Add glaze and cream to fish velouté. Boil for 5 minutes.
3. Strain through cheesecloth.
4. Season with salt, cayenne, pepper, and lemon juice.

USE: Basic ingredient for white fish sauces.
