White Wine Sauce (for fish)

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

White Wine Sauce (for fish)
Weissweinsauce Sauce au vin blanc
Rezept Nr. : 103
Kategorie : Sauces
Koch : P.Diethelm
Detum : 11.08.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 4.74 / EUR 3.00

Zutaten fUr 1.000 Liter zu 100% [Nettomenge)
700 ml *Fish Veloute Sauce
300 ml Fish stock poaching liquid
250 ml Heavy cream (36%)

1 x Seasoning [salt.\cayenne .pepper)
1 x Seasoning lemon juice)

'- REDUCE FISH STOCK TO A GLAZE.
- ADD GLAZE AND CREAM TO FISH VELOUTE; BOIL FOR 5 MINUTES.
- STRAIN THROUGH CHEESECLOTH.
-SEASON WITH SALT. CAYENNE. AND LEMON JUICE.
APPLICATION
Use as a basic ingredient in white fish sauce.
