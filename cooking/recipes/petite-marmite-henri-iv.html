MISE EN PLACE
Blanche beef and chicken separately. Drain, rinse first in hot water then cold water. Prepare sachet bag with parsley, bay leaf, thyme, cloves, and peppercorns. Wash and trim all vegetables (leeks, carrots, knob celery, savoy cabbage). Cut vegetables into losanges. Blanch marrow bones and cut into slices. Chop chives.

COOKING
Bring veal broth to boil. Add beef, bouquet garni, and sachet bag. Simmer 2 hours, skimming and removing impurities regularly. Add chicken and continue simmering. Boil leeks, carrots, knob celery, and savoy cabbage separately in bouillon until tender. Remove beef and chicken from stock and cut into attractive pieces.

FINISH AND SERVE
Arrange beef, chicken, vegetables, and marrow bones in marmite. Pour hot bouillon over. Sprinkle with fresh chives and serve.
