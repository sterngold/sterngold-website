Critical
CUT BACON INTO VERY FINE BRUNOISE
FINE CHOPPED SHALLOTS
DICE THE BREAD INTO 1 CM x 1 CM
CHOP THE PARSLEY
- SAUTE THE SHALLOTS AND BACON IN BUTTER, THEN ADD CHOPPED HERBS AND PLACE INTO BOWL
TOAST THE BREAD WITH THE REMAINING BUTTER IN THE OVEN AT 180°C.
ADD TO SHALLOTS AND BACON
- SEASON WITH SALT, PEPPER MILL AND NUTMEG
- BRING MILK TO A BOIL AND ADD INTO BOWL. DO NOT STIR AND COOL DOWN
- ADD EGGS AND MIE DE PAIN AND ROLL INTO PLASTIC FOIL
- POACH AT 85 - 90° HOT SALTED WATER FOR 1 HOUR.
- REMOVE AND COOL DOWN
- SLICE ON MEAT SLICER INTO 2-3 mm THICK SLICES AND PLACE ON PARCHMENT PAPER INTO 80°C OVEN FOR 20 MINUTES OR OVER NIGHT IN PLATE WARMER TO CRISP UP.
- SERVE AS GARNISH FOR AMUSE BOUCHE OR APPETIZER
