Garde Manger Sauce cold

Carrot oil

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT

Production

-         cut on the grater – birken
-         dry in the oven – not brown – open steam escape
-         blend in the mixer with oil – sun flower oil
-         put in the filter – coffee filter
-         keep in deep freezer for long time
Grate the carrots using a birken grater. Dry in the oven without browning, leaving the steam escape open. Blend in the mixer with sunflower oil. Pass through a coffee filter. Store in the deep freezer.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
