Glazed Carrots

Chef: DCT Source: DCT

Production

Glazed Carrots
Glasierte Karotten
Carottes glacées

Rezept Nr. : 547
Kategorie : Vegetables
Koch : Patrick Diethelm
Datum : 17.08.2005
Warenkosten : CHF 0.13 / EUR 0.08

EUROPEAN CULINARY CENTER

Zutaten für 12 Personen zu 100% (Nettomenge)
900 g Carrot


45 g Butter
1 x Crystal sugar (pinch)
150 ml Water Or vegetable stock
6 g Salt
Wash, peel cut into modern shape Blanch if very large
(or they wil be tough).

- ADD BUTTER INTO RONDEAU.
- ADD CARROTS SUGAR AND SALT. SWEAT CARROTS LIGHTLY. - ADD STOCK TO CARROTS.
- COVER, AND STEW UNTIL CARROTS ARE TENDER.
-REMOVE LID AND REDUCE LIQUID RAPIDLY TO A SYRUPY CONSISTENCY.
- TossCARROTS IN SYRUP TO GLAZE.
For glazed summer vegetables use several different vegetables cut into uniform shape and blanch.
Glaze together like carrots
