Bonbons

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

Ganache
550 butter
500 sugar
500 ml double cream
Vanilla
Mix very well and add to freezer
Mix again and add to piping bag with nozzle …..
Pipe on alum folio small pieces and freeze
Melt chocolate couverture
With fork add the ganche to chocolate
Place on cacao
