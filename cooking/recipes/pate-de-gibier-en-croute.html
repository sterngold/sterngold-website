Paté de gibier en croute (Continuation)
Wildpastete mit gratin farce Pâté de gibier
Recipe # : 269
Category : ECA - Terrine/Paté/Galantine / Marbré
Chef : Patrick Diethelm
Date : 28.11.2005
Cost of goods : CHF 1.06 / EUR 0.67



FOOD PROCESSOR AND BLEND UNTIL SMOOTH FARCE WITH 2/3 OF CREAM AND MEAT GLAZE.

SEASON TO TASTE.
ADD SAUTEED HORN OF PLENTY OR CHANTERELLES
ADD ROAS TED NUTS
ADD HAM OR TONGUE

FILL PREPARED LINED PATÉ MOLD ABOUT 1/3 HIGH WITH FARCE.
PLACE PREPARED VENISON TENDERLOIN IN CENTER AND COVER WITH REMAINING FARCE.
BRUSH DOUGH RIM WITH EGG WASH CREAM MIXTURE.
COVER WITH DOUGH AND PREPARE CHIMNEYS TO LET STEAM ESCAPE.
DECORATE TOP WITH DOUGH PIECES.
REFRIGERATE FOR 1 hour
BRUSH TOP WITH EGG WASH MIXTURE.

BAKE IN OVEN AT 250°C FOR 10 MINUTES
CUT OUT OPENING OF CHMNEY HOLES AND CONTINUE BAKING AT 180°C, WITH OPEN VENT,UNTIL CENTER TEMPERATURE HAS REACHED 55°C.
LET PATÉ COOL 25°C POUR HOT ASPIC JELLY THROUGH CHIMNEY HOLES AND REFRIGERATE UNTIL COMPLETELY CHILLED.

RECIPE FOR TWO SMALLER PATÉ MOLDS
