- GRATE THE POTATOES WITH BIRCHER GRATER , —
- SQUEEZE OUT ALL THE JUICE AND ADD WHITE WHEAT FLOUR EGGS,CURD CHEESE BAKING POWDER AND SEASON TO TASTE
- BRING WATER TO A BOIL
- FORM DUMPLINGS WITH TEA SPOON AND POACH IN SALTED WATER
- REMOVE THE DUMPLINGS WITH A SPIDER AS SOON AS THEY RISE TO THE TOP AND PUT INTO COLD WATER.
DRAIN IMMEDIATELY.
- REHEAT AS NEEDED BY STEAMING IN CONVECTION OVEN OR PLACING INTO 90°C SALTED WATER FOR 5 MIN.
- DRAIN FOR SERVING AND PLACE INTO OVENPROOF DISH OR GASTRO NORM SPRINKLE WITH GRATED CHEESE AND BAKE FOR 10 MINUTES AT 200°C.
- ADD BUTTER INTO FRYING PAN ADD BACON AND SAUTE GOLDEN BROWN WITH ONION EMINCE AND SPRINKLE OVER THE PIZOKEL AND SERVE.
- SPRINKLE WITH CHOPPED PARSLEY
- SERVE WITH STEWED SWEET AND SOUR PRUNES OR CRANBERRY COMPOT ON THE SIDE
