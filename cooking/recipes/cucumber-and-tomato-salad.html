Cucumber and tomato Salad

Chef: P.Diethelm Source: DCT Servings: 10

Ingredients

1.0 kg Cucumber
600 g Tomatoes
# Dressing
25 g Dill, fresh, chopped
20 g Mustard, mild
2 pcs x Seasoning, salt and pepper
100 g ml Vinegar, red wine
140 g ml Olive oil cold pressed
# Garnish
25 g Chives, fresh
Oil, sunflower
Vinegar, herbs

Production

- wash cucumbers and peel partially. Split, remove seeds if necessary and cut into julliene
- peel tomatoes (optional), halve and cut into slices
- combine dill, mustard, salt and pepper, herb vinegar and sunflower oil

-JUST BEFORE SERVING BLEND CUCUMBERS WITH THE DILL MIXTURE
- MAKE MOUNFS OF CUCUMBER SALAD IN THE CENTRES OF PLATES
- PLACE TOMATO SLICES AROUND
- COMBINE RED VINEGAR WITH SALT AND PEPPER AND OLIVE OIL. DRIZZLE RED WINE VINEAGR OLIVE OIL DRESSING OVER TOMATOES
- SRPINKLE WITH CHIVES
**Cucumber and Tomato Salad**

Wash cucumbers and peel partially in strips. Split lengthwise, remove seeds if necessary, and cut into julienne. Peel tomatoes (optional), halve, and cut into slices.

Prepare dill vinaigrette: combine dill, mustard, salt, pepper, herb vinegar, and sunflower oil.

Just before service, fold cucumbers with dill mixture. Mound cucumber salad in center of plates and arrange tomato slices around.

Prepare red wine dressing: whisk red vinegar, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Drizzle over tomato slices. Garnish with sliced chives.
From DCT · Chef P.Diethelm
