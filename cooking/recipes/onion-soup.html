**Onion Soup** | Vijf, Entremétier

**Ingredients:**
- Onions
- Olive oil
- Bouquet garni
- White wine
- Madeira or white port
- Chicken stock

**Method:**
1. Glaze onions in olive oil over very low heat until deeply caramelized.
2. Add bouquet garni, white wine, madeira (or white port), and chicken stock.
3. Simmer until flavors meld.
From Vijf · Chef Jan Wasch
