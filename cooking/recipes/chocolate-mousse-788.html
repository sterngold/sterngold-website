Chocolate mousse

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

Sabayon - 200 g egg yolk + 50 sugar + 1 muscat wine + 1 glas (big) strong coffie
Maybe not so much koffie but add water
1/ 4 Chocolate couverture - melt
Mix Sabayon with couverture
500 g Cream - whip fold to the mix
Egg white - fold to the mix (300 g )
Sabayon: Whisk together 200g egg yolk, 50g sugar, 1 measure Muscat wine, and 1 large glass strong coffee (reduce coffee quantity if needed; add water to adjust). Set aside.

Melt 250g chocolate couverture. Fold cooled couverture into sabayon mixture. Whip 500g cream to soft peaks and fold into mousse. Whip 300g egg whites and gently fold into the mixture until just combined.
From VandeMarkt · Chef V. I. Sterngold
