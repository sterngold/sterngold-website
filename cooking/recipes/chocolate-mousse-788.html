Pâtisserie Dessert

Chocolate mousse

Chef: V. I. Sterngold Source: VandeMarkt

Production

Sabayon - 200 g egg yolk + 50 sugar + 1 muscat wine + 1 glas (big) strong coffie
Maybe not so much koffie but add water
1/ 4 Chocolate couverture - melt
Mix Sabayon with couverture
500 g Cream - whip fold to the mix
Egg white - fold to the mix (300 g )
From VandeMarkt · Chef V. I. Sterngold
