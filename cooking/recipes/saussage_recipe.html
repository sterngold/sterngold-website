SWISS BRATWURST RECIPES

1. SCHWEINS BRATWURST (Swiss German Style)
Composition per kg:
850 g pork meat (P III, 5 mm grind)
150 g veal sausage paste
Seasonings per kg: 19 g cooking salt, 1 g pepper, 1 g nutmeg, 0.5 g marjoram, 0.5 g mace, 1 g seasoning condiment, 5 g steamed onions

Method:
Mince pork with onions. Mix thoroughly with sausage paste, salt, and seasonings. Stuff into pork casings (28/30 mm). Sell raw.

Variation: Form 100 g portions, wrap in pork caul, garnish with parsley.

2. ST. GALLER KALBSBRATWURST (Veal Bratwurst)
Composition per kg:
350 g veal (K I)
100 g pork (P II)
250 g neck lard
300 ml milk
Seasonings per kg: 19 g cooking salt, 2 g pepper, 0.5 g mace, 0.5 g citrus spice, 1 g liquid seasoning, 10 g fresh onions. Optional: 0.5 egg.

Method:
If using egg, add at end of mixing. Blend all ingredients. Stuff into pork casings (30/32 mm, ~140 g per piece). Poach 20–25 min at 75°C. Cool in ice bath. Note: Name protected in St. Gallen canton only.

3. BAUERNBRATWURST (Vaudoise Pork Bratwurst)
Composition per kg:
750 g pork (P II, 5 or 8 mm grind)
250 g neck lard (P V, 5 or 8 mm grind)
Seasonings per kg: 17 g cooking salt, 2 g pepper, 2 g mace, 1 g nutmeg, 1 g marjoram, 10 g white wine.

Method:
Mince pork and lard while still warm. Mix thoroughly with salt and seasonings. Stuff into pork casings (28/30 or 30/32 mm). Sell raw by weight in spiral form.
