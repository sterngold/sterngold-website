Entremétier Soup

Cappuchino of Curry and Lemon grass with scampi

Chef: Ulf Wein Source: Tschuggen Servings: 8

Ingredients

500 g ml Chicken stock, white
400 g dl Cream
100 g dl Noilly prat
50 g cl Port wine, white
80 g cl Wine, white, dry
4 pcs piece Shallots (cut fine)
piece Leeks (1/2 stange)
1 pcs el Curry powder
4 pcs piece Lemon grass
2 pcs piece Lime leaves
4 pcs el Cocos flocken
60 g Butter, cold (in cubes)
1 pcs x Salt
60 g Noodels
# Fruits mix
1 pcs piece Apple (Granny smith)
piece Ananas, mini (1/2 piece)
piece Banana (1/2 piece)
Parsley, fresh

Production

- Noilly prat , port wine and wihte wine well reduced
- add shallots, leeks, and fond and simmer for 5 min
- add cream, curry and cocos and simmer another 10 min
- puree fein fruits and parsley in mixer machine and add to the soup
- simmer for another 5 min and then through towel or china cup and still hot add the butter cubes
- gut wurzig abschmecken
- one scampi a la minute pan fray and add to the soup glass. Soup mix with stab mixer
From Tschuggen · Chef Ulf Wein
